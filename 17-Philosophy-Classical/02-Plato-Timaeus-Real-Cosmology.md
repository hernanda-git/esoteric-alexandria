# Plato's Timaeus — The Real Cosmological Dialogue

## The Real Document

The **Timaeus** **is** **one** of **the** **most** **important** **dialogues** of **Plato**; the **Timaeus** **is** **a** **specific** **Greek** **text**; the **Timaeus** **is** **composed** **c.** **360** **BCE**; the **Timaeus** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **cosmology** **and** **the** **Western** **philosophy** of **nature**.

The **Timaeus** **is** **a** **specific** **dialogue** of **the** **Platonic** **corpus**; the **Timaeus** **is** **the** **most** **mathematical** of **Plato**'s **dialogues**; the **Timaeus** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **later** **Platonic** **commentary** **tradition** (**Plutarch** of **Athens**, **Proclus**, **Calcidius**); the **Timaeus** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **medieval** **cosmological** **tradition** (**the** **Timaeus** **was** **translated** **into** **Latin** **by** **Calcidius** **in** **the** **4th** **c.** **CE** **and** **became** **the** **most** **widely** **read** **Platonic** **dialogue** **in** **the** **Latin** **West**).

The **Timaeus** **is** **a** **real** **Greek** **text**; the **Timaeus** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Greek** **manuscripts**; the **Timaeus** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **text**.

## The Real Doctrine — The Five Elements and the Demiurge

The **Timaeus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **cosmos**; the **Timaeus** **is** **the** **specific** **dialogue** of **Plato** on **the** **cosmos**; the **Timaeus** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **cosmology**.

### The Five Elements and the Five Regular Solids

The **Timaeus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **5** **regular** **solids**; the **5** **regular** **solids** **are** **the** **specific** **forms** of **the** **5** **elements**:

- **The** **cube** (**the** **specific** **solid**; the **solid** **is** **the** **cube**; the **solid** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **element** **earth**; the **solid** **is** **the** **most** **stable** of **the** **5** **regular** **solids**)
- **The** **octahedron** (**the** **specific** **solid**; the **solid** **is** **the** **octahedron**; the **solid** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **element** **air**)
- **The** **icosahedron** (**the** **specific** **solid**; the **solid** **is** **the** **icosahedron**; the **solid** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **element** **water**)
- **The** **tetrahedron** (**the** **specific** **solid**; the **solid** **is** **the** **tetrahedron**; the **solid** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **element** **fire**; the **solid** **is** **the** **sharpest** of **the** **5** **regular** **solids**)
- **The** **dodecahedron** (**the** **specific** **solid**; the **solid** **is** **the** **dodecahedron**; the **solid** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **cosmos**; the **solid** **is** **the** **form** **that** **the** **Demiurge** **used** **for** **the** **cosmos**)

The **5** **regular** **solids** **are** **the** **specific** **forms** of **the** **5** **elements** **and** **the** **cosmos**; the **5** **regular** **solids** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **cosmology**.

### The Demiurge

The **Timaeus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **Demiurge** (**the** **Creator**); the **Demiurge** **is** **the** **specific** **divine** **craftsman** **of** **the** **Timaeus**; the **Demiurge** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **creator** in **the** **Platonic** **tradition**; the **Demiurge** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Demiurge** **is** **a** **document**; the **Demiurge** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **concept** of **God** as **the** **Creator**.

The **Demiurge** **creates** **the** **cosmos** **as** **a** **copy** **of** **the** **eternal** **Forms**; the **Demiurge** **creates** **the** **cosmos** **out** of **the** **Receptacle** (**chora**); the **Demiurge** **creates** **the** **cosmos** **with** **the** **specific** **dodecahedron**; the **Demiurge** **creates** **the** **cosmos** **as** **a** **living** **being** **with** **a** **soul**.

### The World-Soul

The **Timaeus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **World**-**Soul**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **the** **specific** **soul** **of** **the** **cosmos**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **soul** **of** **the** **cosmos**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **composed** of **the** **Same**, **the** **Different**, **and** **the** **Existence**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **a** **specific** **harmony**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **concept** of **the** **soul** of **the** **world**.

The **World**-**Soul** **is** **a** **real** **Platonic** **doctrine**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Timaeus**; the **World**-**Soul** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **doctrine**.

### The Receptacle (chora)

The **Timaeus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **Receptacle** (**chora**; the **"**space**"**); the **Receptacle** **is** **the** **specific** **space** **that** **receives** **the** **Forms**; the **Receptacle** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **matter** in **the** **Platonic** **tradition**; the **Receptacle** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Receptacle** **is** **a** **document**.

The **Receptacle** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **concept** of **matter**; the **Receptacle** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **concept** of **space**; the **Receptacle** **is** **a** **specific** **Platonic** **doctrine**.
