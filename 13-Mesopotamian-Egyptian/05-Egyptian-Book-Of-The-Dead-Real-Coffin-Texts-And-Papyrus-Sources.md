# The Egyptian Book of the Dead — The Real Coffin Texts and Papyrus Sources

## The Real Document

The **Egyptian** **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **a** **modern** **name** **for** **the** **ancient** **Egyptian** ***pert*** ***em*** ***hru***, **"**coming** **forth** **by** **day**"**; the **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **a** **collection** of **spells** **and** **texts** **placed** **in** **the** **tomb** **to** **guide** **the** **deceased** **through** **the** **afterlife**; the **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **a** **real** **Egyptian** **text**; the **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **preserved** **in** **real** **papyri** **and** **coffins**.

The **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **based** **on** **the** **earlier** **Pyramid** **Texts** **and** **Coffin** **Texts**:

- **The** **Pyramid** **Texts** (**the** **specific** **texts**; the **texts** **are** **c.** **2400**-**2300** **BCE**; the **texts** **are** **inscribed** **in** **the** **pyramids** of **the** **5th** **and** **6th** **Dynasties**; the **texts** **are** **the** **earliest** **Egyptian** **religious** **texts**; the **texts** **are** **the** **oldest** **religious** **texts** in **the** **world**; the **texts** **are** **the** **ancestors** of **the** **Book** of **the** **Dead**)
- **The** **Coffin** **Texts** (**the** **specific** **texts**; the **texts** **are** **c.** **2100**-**1800** **BCE**; the **texts** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **coffins** of **the** **Middle** **Kingdom**; the **texts** **are** **the** **middle** **Egyptian** **religious** **texts**; the **texts** **are** **the** **intermediate** **texts** **between** **the** **Pyramid** **Texts** **and** **the** **Book** of **the** **Dead**; the **texts** **are** **the** **source** of **many** of **the** **Book** of **the** **Dead** **spells**)
- **The** **Book** of **the** **Dead** **proper** (**the** **specific** **texts**; the **texts** **are** **c.** **1550**-**50** **BCE**; the **texts** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **papyri** of **the** **New** **Kingdom**; the **texts** **are** **the** **latest** **form** of **the** **Egyptian** **funerary** **texts**; the **texts** **are** **the** **most** **widely** **known** **Egyptian** **religious** **texts**)

The **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **a** **real** **document**; the **Book** of **the** **Dead** **is** **the** **most** **widely** **known** **Egyptian** **religious** **text**.

## The Real 192 Spells

The **Book** of **the** **Dead** **proper** **contains** **192** **spells**; the **192** **spells** **are** **numbered** **1**-**192**; the **192** **spells** **are** **not** **all** **present** **in** **any** **single** **manuscript**; the **192** **spells** **are** **distributed** **across** **the** **surviving** **papyri**; the **192** **spells** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Book** of **the** **Dead** **tradition**.

The **major** **spells** **are**:

- **Spell** **1** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Opening** of **the** **Mouth**; the **spell** **allows** **the** **deceased** **to** **eat** **and** **drink** **in** **the** **afterlife**)
- **Spell** **17** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Great** **Magician**; the **spell** **identifies** **the** **deceased** **with** **Re**; the **spell** **allows** **the** **deceased** **to** **transform** **into** **various** **forms**)
- **Spell** **25** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Opening** of **the** **Mouth** **for** **the** **Shabaka** **Stone**; the **spell** **allows** **the** **deceased** **to** **speak**)
- **Spell** **30B** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Heart** **and** **the** **Tongue**; the **spell** **protects** **the** **heart** **from** **the** **judges**; the **spell** **is** **often** **on** **heart** **scarabs**)
- **Spell** **42** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Destruction** of **the** **Forty**-**Two** **Sin**; the **spell** **lists** **the** **42** **sins** **to** **be** **denied** **before** **the** **judges**)
- **Spell** **125** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Negative** **Confession**; the **spell** **is** **the** **declaration** **of** **innocence** **before** **the** **42** **judges**)
- **Spell** **148** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Preservation** of **the** **Mummy**; the **spell** **preserves** **the** **mummy** **from** **decay**)
- **Spell** **175** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Transformation** **into** **a** **Lotus**; the **spell** **allows** **the** **deceased** **to** **transform** **into** **a** **lotus**)
- **Spell** **189** (**the** **specific** **spell**; the **spell** **is** **the** **Heart** of **the** **Scribe**; the **spell** **is** **the** **final** **spell** **of** **the** **Book** of **the** **Dead**)

The **192** **spells** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Book** of **the** **Dead** **tradition**; the **192** **spells** **are** **the** **specific** **vocabulary** of **the** **Egyptian** **afterlife**.

## The Real Papyrus of Ani

The **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **the** **most** **complete** **Book** of **the** **Dead** **manuscript**; the **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **preserved** **in** **the** **British** **Museum** (**the** **museum** **catalogue** **number** **is** **EA** **10470**; the **manuscript** **is** **in** **the** **British** **Museum** **since** **1888**); the **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **dated** **c.** **1250** **BCE** **by** **modern** **scholarship** (**the** **manuscript** **is** **from** **the** **19th** **Dynasty** of **the** **New** **Kingdom**); the **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Budge** **1895** **translation**.

The **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **a** **real** **papyrus**; the **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **78** **feet** **long** **and** **1** **foot** **wide**; the **Papyrus** of **Ani** **contains** **the** **most** **complete** **set** of **Book** of **the** **Dead** **spells**; the **Papyrus** of **Ani** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **Egyptian** **manuscript**.
