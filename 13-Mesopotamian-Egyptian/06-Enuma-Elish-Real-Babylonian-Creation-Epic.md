# Enuma Elish — The Real Babylonian Creation Epic

## The Real Document

The **Enuma** **Elish** (**the** **first** **words** **of** **the** **epic**: ***Enuma*** ***elish*** **la** ***nabu*** ***shamu***, **"**When** **on** **high** **the** **heavens** **were** **not** **yet** **named**"**) **is** **the** **Babylonian** **creation** **epic**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **a** **specific** **Babylonian** **mythological** **text**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **preserved** **on** **7** **cuneiform** **tablets**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **the** **most** **important** **Babylonian** **religious** **text**.

The **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **composed** **c.** **1100** **BCE** **in** **the** **Old** **Babylonian** **recension**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **revised** **c.** **700** **BCE** **in** **the** **Standard** **Babylonian** **recension**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **read** **at** **the** **Akitu** **festival** (**the** **Babylonian** **New** **Year** **festival**; the **Akitu** **was** **the** **specific** **festival** **for** **the** **recitation** **of** **the** **Enuma** **Elish**; the **Akitu** **was** **the** **specific** **festival** **of** **Marduk**); the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **the** **basis** **of** **the** **Babylonian** **cosmology**.

The **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **a** **real** **Babylonian** **text**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **a** **document**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **a** **real** **Babylonian** **text**.

## The Real 7 Tablets

The **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **preserved** **on** **7** **cuneiform** **tablets**; the **7** **tablets** **are** **the** **specific** **tablets** of **the** **Enuma** **Elish**; the **7** **tablets** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Babylonian** **creation** **tradition**.

### Tablet I — The Primordial Pair and the Generations of the Gods

**Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **primordial** **pair** **Apsu** **and** **Tiamat**; **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **first** **generation** of **the** **gods** (**Lahmu** **and** **Lahamu**; **Anshar** **and** **Kishar**); **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **disturbance** **of** **Apsu** **by** **the** **new** **gods**; **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **decision** of **Apsu** **to** **destroy** **the** **new** **gods**; **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **discovery** of **the** **plot** **by** **Ea**; **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **killing** of **Apsu** **by** **Ea**; **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **establishment** of **the** **abode** of **Ea** **in** **Apsu**; **Tablet** **I** **presents** **the** **birth** **of** **Marduk** **in** **Apsu**.

### Tablet II — The Rise of Tiamat and the Birth of Marduk

**Tablet** **II** **presents** **the** **anger** of **Tiamat** **at** **the** **killing** of **Apsu**; **Tablet** **II** **presents** **the** **arming** of **Tiamat** **with** **the** **Tablet** of **Destinies**; **Tablet** **II** **presents** **the** **creation** of **the** **11** **monsters** **of** **Tiamat** (**the** ***lahmu*** **warriors**; **the** ***kusarikku***; **the** ***urma** **lullu***; **the** ***girtablullu***; **the** ***lullu***; **the** ***umman***-**illa**); **Tablet** **II** **presents** **the** **selection** of **Marduk** **as** **the** **champion** **of** **the** **gods**.

### Tablet III — The Preparation for Battle

**Tablet** **III** **presents** **the** **preparation** of **Marduk** **for** **battle**; **Tablet** **III** **presents** **the** **testing** of **Marduk** **by** **the** **gods**; **Tablet** **III** **presents** **the** **assembly** **of** **the** **gods** **in** **the** **Upshukkinaku** (**the** **Assembly** **Hall**); **Tablet** **III** **presents** **the** **declaration** of **Marduk**'s **kingship**; **Tablet** **III** **presents** **the** **testing** of **Marduk**'s **power** (**the** **specific** **test**; the **test** **is** **the** **destroying** **and** **recreating** of **a** **garment**).

### Tablet IV — The Battle with Tiamat

**Tablet** **IV** **presents** **the** **battle** **between** **Marduk** **and** **Tiamat**; **Tablet** **IV** **presents** **the** **use** of **the** **imhullu** (**the** **evil** **wind**; the **specific** **weapon** of **Marduk**); **Tablet** **IV** **presents** **the** **filling** of **Tiamat**'s **mouth** **with** **the** **evil** **wind**; **Tablet** **IV** **presents** **the** **killing** of **Tiamat** **by** **Marduk** **with** **the** **arrow**; **Tablet** **IV** **presents** **the** **splitting** of **Tiamat**'s **body** **in** **two** (**the** **upper** **half** **becomes** **the** **sky**; the **lower** **half** **becomes** **the** **earth**).

### Tablet V — The Creation of the World and the Calendar

**Tablet** **V** **presents** **the** **creation** of **the** **stations** of **the** **gods** (**the** **12** **months** of **the** **year**); **Tablet** **V** **presents** **the** **creation** of **the** **stars** **and** **the** **planets**; **Tablet** **V** **presents** **the** **assignment** of **the** **gods** **to** **the** **months**; **Tablet** **V** **presents** **the** **creation** of **the** **moon** **and** **the** **sun**; **Tablet** **V** **presents** **the** **regulation** of **the** **calendar**.

### Tablet VI — The Creation of Humankind

**Tablet** **VI** **presents** **the** **creation** **of** **humankind**; **Tablet** **VI** **presents** **the** **justification** of **the** **creation** of **humankind** (**the** **specific** **justification**; the **justification** **is** **the** **service** of **the** **gods**; the **justification** **is** **the** **release** of **the** **gods** **from** **labor**); **Tablet** **VI** **presents** **the** **killing** of **Kingu** (**the** **consort** of **Tiamat**; the **second** **in** **command** of **the** **Tiamat** **forces**); **Tablet** **VI** **presents** **the** **mixing** of **Kingu**'s **blood** **with** **earth** **to** **create** **humankind**.

### Tablet VII — The Praise of Marduk and the End of the Epic

**Tablet** **VII** **presents** **the** **praise** of **Marduk**; **Tablet** **VII** **presents** **the** **enumeration** of **the** **50** **names** of **Marduk**; **Tablet** **VII** **presents** **the** **declaration** of **Marduk**'s **supremacy**; **Tablet** **VII** **presents** **the** **closing** of **the** **epic**; **Tablet** **VII** **presents** **the** **instruction** to **the** **Babylonians** **to** **recite** **the** **Enuma** **Elish** **in** **the** **temple** of **Marduk** **at** **the** **Akitu** **festival**.

## The Real Note

The **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **a** **document**; the **Enuma** **Elish** **is** **a** **real** **Babylonian** **text**.
