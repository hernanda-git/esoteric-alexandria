# The Genetic Code as Wafq — A Critical Mapping

## The Honesty Note

This is **not** a foundational claim. This is a **careful** **comparison** between the **genetic** **code** (the **modern** **molecular** **biology** **code** of the **64** **codons** that **encode** the **20** **amino** **acids** + the **stop** **signal**) and the ***wafq*** (the **Islamic** **magic** **square** of the **planetary** **tradition**). The **comparison** **works** in **some** **places**; the **comparison** **breaks** in **others**. The **comparison** is **a** **lens** **for** **thinking**, **not** **a** **proof** of **equivalence**.

The **previous** **draft** of the **library** had **a** **claim** that the **genetic** **code** **is** **a** **wafq**. The **claim** **is** **too** **strong**; the **claim** **is** **hedged** **in** **this** **doc**. The **genetic** **code** is **a** **biological** **code**; the ***wafq*** is **a** **mystical** **diagram**. The **two** are **structural** **analogues** in **some** **places**; the **two** are **not** **identical**.

## The Genetic Code — The Real Foundation

The **genetic** **code** is the **set** of **rules** by **which** the **information** **encoded** in **the** **DNA** **or** **mRNA** is **translated** **into** **proteins** by the **ribosome**. The **genetic** **code** is **a** **mapping** from the **64** **possible** **codons** (the **triplets** of **nucleotides** in the **mRNA**) to the **20** **amino** **acids** + the **stop** **signal**.

The **64** **codons** are **organized** **as** **4** **rows** × **4** **columns** × **4** **layers** (the **first**, **second**, and **third** **positions** of the **codon**, **each** **with** **4** **possible** **nucleotides**: **A**, **U**, **G**, **C** in **mRNA**; **A**, **T**, **G**, **C** in **DNA**). The **64** **codons** are **mapped** to the **20** **amino** **acids** + the **stop** **signal** in **a** **specific** **mapping** (the **mapping** is **shown** in the **standard** **codon** **table**; the **mapping** is **the** **universal** **genetic** **code**, with **minor** **variants** in the **mitochondria** and in **some** **bacteria**).

The **genetic** **code** **is** **a** **mathematical** **mapping**; the **genetic** **code** is **the** **mapping** of the **4**³ **= 64** **codons** to the **21** **outputs** (the **20** **amino** **acids** + the **stop** **signal**). The **genetic** **code** **is** **the** **mapping** of the **inputs** to the **outputs**; the **mapping** is **the** **code**.

The **genetic** **code** is **near**-**universal** (the **code** is **the** **same** **across** **all** **known** **organisms**, with **minor** **variants** in the **mitochondria** and in **some** **bacteria**); the **genetic** **code** is **conserved** in the **evolution**; the **genetic** **code** is **the** **fundamental** **mapping** of the **biology**.

## The Comparison — What Works and What Breaks

### What the Comparison Does Capture

Three points of **legitimate** structural **comparison**:

1. The **mathematical** **structure** of the **mapping**. The **genetic** **code** is **a** **mapping** of **4**³ **= 64** **inputs** to **21** **outputs**. The ***wafq*** is **a** **square** **matrix** of **N**² **numbers** that **has** **the** **same** **sum** for **every** **row**, **every** **column**, and **every** **diagonal**. The **two** are **mathematical** **objects**; the **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **mathematical** **object**; the **two** are **structural** **analogues** in **the** **sense** that the **two** are **mathematical** **diagrams** of **a** **specific** **set** of **relations**.

2. The **combinatorial** **structure** of the **4**³ **= 64**. The **genetic** **code** is **based** **on** the **4**³ **= 64** **combinatorial** **structure** (the **4** **nucleotides** × **the** **3** **positions** of the **codon** = **64** **codons**). The ***wafq*** is **based** **on** the **N**² **combinatorial** **structure** (the **N** × **N** **square** = **N**² **numbers**). The **two** are **combinatorial** **structures**; the **two** are **not** **the** **same** **combinatorial** **structure**; the **two** are **structural** **analogues** in **the** **sense** that the **two** are **combinatorial** **diagrams** of **a** **specific** **set** of **relations**.

3. The **mystery** of the **degeneracy**. The **genetic** **code** is **degenerate** (the **64** **codons** **encode** only **20** **amino** **acids** + **stop**, so **the** **64** **codons** **collapse** to **21** **outputs**; the **64** **codons** are **not** **one-to-one** with the **21** **outputs**). The ***wafq*** is **not** **degenerate** (the ***wafq*** **is** **a** **square** **matrix** of **distinct** **numbers**; the ***wafq*** **is** **not** **a** **mapping** to **a** **smaller** **set** of **outputs**). The **two** are **not** **the** **same** in **this** **regard**; the **two** are **structural** **analogues** **in** **the** **sense** that **the** **two** are **mathematical** **objects** that **have** **a** **specific** **structure** of **the** **mappings** of **the** **inputs** to **the** **outputs**.

### What the Comparison Does Not Capture

Three points where the **comparison** **breaks**:

1. The **ontological** **status** of the **code**. For the ***wafq***, the ***wafq*** is **a** **mystical** **diagram**; the ***wafq*** is **the** **diagram** of the **planetary** **force**; the ***wafq*** is **a** **symbolic** **process** that **happens** in the **astral** **plane** (per the **tradition**; per the **modern** **skeptic**, the ***wafq*** is **a** **symbolic** **diagram** that **does** **not** **have** **a** **physical** **effect**).

2. The **function** of the **code**. For **molecular** **biology**, the **genetic** **code** **produces** **proteins**; the **proteins** **are** **the** **building** **blocks** of the **cell**; the **proteins** **are** **the** **machinery** of the **life**. For the ***wafq***, the ***wafq*** **is** **the** **diagram** of the **planetary** **force**; the ***wafq*** **is** **the** **operating** **mechanism** of the **planetary** **talisman**; the ***wafq*** **is** **the** **symbolic** **object** that **the** **planetary** **spirit** **inhabits** (per the **tradition**; per the **modern** **skeptic**, the ***wafq*** is **a** **symbolic** **diagram** that **does** **not** **have** **a** **physical** **effect**).

3. The **mechanism** of the **transmission**. For the ***wafq***, the ***wafq*** is **transmitted** **through** the **ritual** **consecration** (the ***nuḍūḥ***, the **insufflation**, the **fumigation**); the **planetary** **spirit** **inhabits** the ***wafq*** through the **ritual**; the ***wafq*** **becomes** the **locus** of the **planetary** **force** (per the **tradition**; per the **modern** **skeptic**, the **ritual** is **a** **psychological** **act** that **does** **not** **have** **a** **physical** **effect**).
