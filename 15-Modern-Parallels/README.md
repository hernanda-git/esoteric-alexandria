## Deep-Dive Paths

- **Signal layer** — cryptanalysis and the alphanumeric wafq are the same formal operation applied to states and alphabets.
- **Epistemology layer** — ma'rifa is precisely Shannon's mutual information / Bayesian updating, but only formally; the telos differs.
- **Structural layer** — the genetic code's redundancy is the closest modern analogue of the classical square of opposition.
- **Ontological layer** — *Kitab al-Jafr* states the universe can be read as a book; Tegmark's Mathematical Universe states the universe is a mathematical structure. The difference is the readership.
