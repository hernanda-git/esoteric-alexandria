# Big Data as Akashic Record — A Critical Refusal

## The Honesty Note

This is **not** a foundational claim. This is a **critical** **refusal** of **a** **popular** **but** **unsupported** **claim** that has been **propagated** in the **new-age** **community**: the **claim** that the **modern** **big** **data** is **the** **akashic** **record** of the **Theosophical** **tradition**. The **claim** is **wrong**; the **claim** **misrepresents** the **akashic** **record**; the **claim** **misrepresents** the **big** **data**. The **critical** **refusal** is **the** **clearest** **way** to **honor** the **akashic** **record** and the **big** **data**.

The **previous** **draft** of the **library** had **a** **claim** that the **big** **data** is **the** **akashic** **record** of the **modern** **world**. The **claim** is **rejected** in **this** **doc**. The **akashic** **record** is **a** **specific** **Theosophical** **doctrine**; the **big** **data** is **a** **specific** **modern** **technology**. The **two** are **not** **the** **same**; the **two** are **not** **analogues**; the **two** are **different** **kinds** of **objects**.

## The Akashic Record — The Real History

The **akashic** **record** is **a** **Theosophical** **invention**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** **a** **Hindu** **or** **Buddhist** **doctrine** in **the** **classical** **sense**. The **akashic** **record** is **attributed** to **Helena** **Blavatsky** (the **founder** of the **Theosophical** **Society** in **1875**) in **The** **Secret** **Doctrine** (**1888**); the **akashic** **record** is **derived** **from** the **Sanskrit** **word** ***ākāśa*** (the **fifth** **element**, the **subtle** **matter**, the **ether** of the **Hindu** **cosmology**).

The **akashic** **record** in the **Blavatsky** **tradition** is **a** **cosmic** **record** of **all** **events** in the **universe**; the **akashic** **record** is **written** in the ***ākāśa*** (the **subtle** **matter**); the **akashic** **record** is **read** by the **adept** through the **astral** **vision**. The **akashic** **record** is **a** **spiritual** **technology** of the **Theosophical** **tradition**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** a **physical** **technology**.

The **akashic** **record** is **not** in the **Vedas**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** in the **Upanishads**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** in the **Puranas**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** in the **Mahabharata**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** in the **Ramayana**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** in the **Tantras**. The **akashic** **record** is **a** **Theosophical** **invention**; the **akashic** **record** is **not** **a** **classical** **Hindu** **doctrine**. The **akashic** **record** is **sometimes** **projected** **back** **into** the **Hindu** **tradition** by **the** **new-age** **community**; the **projection** is **a** **historical** **error**.

The **akashic** **record** is **a** **specific** **Theosophical** **doctrine**; the **akashic** **record** is **a** **specific** **Western** **esoteric** **invention**; the **akashic** **record** is **a** **modern** (1888) **invention**. The **akashic** **record** is **not** an **ancient** **Hindu** **or** **Buddhist** **doctrine**.

## The Big Data — The Real Technology

The **big** **data** is **a** **modern** **technology** of the **data** **storage** and the **data** **analysis**. The **big** **data** is **the** **datasets** that are **so** **large** and **so** **complex** that **the** **traditional** **data** **processing** **applications** are **inadequate**. The **big** **data** is **the** **datasets** that **require** **the** **distributed** **storage** and the **distributed** **processing**.

The **big** **data** is **characterized** by the **3V** (**Volume**, **Velocity**, **Variety**), the **5V** (the **3V** + **Veracity** + **Value**), or the **7V** (the **5V** + **Variability** + **Visualization**). The **big** **data** is **the** **data** that **is** **generated** by the **modern** **digital** **infrastructure** (the **internet**, the **sensors**, the **smartphones**, the **satellites**, the **scientific** **instruments**).

The **big** **data** is **a** **real** **physical** **technology**; the **big** **data** is **stored** on **the** **physical** **servers**; the **big** **data** is **transmitted** **through** the **physical** **networks**; the **big** **data** is **processed** **by** the **physical** **CPUs**. The **big** **data** is **not** **a** **cosmic** **record**; the **big** **data** is **a** **physical** **record** of the **human** **productions**.

The **big** **data** is **not** **akashic**; the **big** **data** is **a** **physical** **record** of the **human** **productions**; the **big** **data** is **not** a **cosmic** **record** of **all** **events** in the **universe**. The **big** **data** is **limited** **to** the **human** **productions**; the **big** **data** is **limited** to the **digital** **record**; the **big** **data** is **not** **a** **cosmic** **record**; the **big** **data** is **a** **human** **record**.

## The Comparison — The Refusal

The **comparison** **between** the **big** **data** and the **akashic** **record** is **rejected** for the **following** **reasons**:

### The Ontological Mismatch

The **akashic** **record** is **a** **cosmic** **record** of **all** **events** in the **universe**; the **akashic** **record** **includes** the **events** of the **past**, the **present**, and the **future** (per the **Theosophical** **doctrine**); the **akashic** **record** **includes** the **events** of the **physical** **plane**, the **astral** **plane**, the **mental** **plane**, and the **higher** **planes** (per the **Theosophical** **cosmology**). The **big** **data** is **a** **human** **record** of the **digital** **productions**; the **big** **data** **does** **not** **include** the **events** of the **past** (the **big** **data** **is** **limited** **to** the **digital** **era**, **c.** **1950**-**present**); the **big** **data** **does** **not** **include** the **events** of the **future**; the **big** **data** **does** **not** **include** the **events** of the **astral** **plane** or the **higher** **planes** (the **big** **data** is **limited** **to** the **physical** **plane**).

The **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **record**; the **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **object**. The **akashic** **record** is **a** **cosmic** **object**; the **big** **data** is **a** **human** **object**. The **akashic** **record** is **eternal**; the **big** **data** is **temporary**. The **akashic** **record** is **omniscient**; the **big** **data** is **limited**. The **akashic** **record** is **written** in the ***ākāśa***; the **big** **data** is **written** on the **silicon**.

### The Methodological Mismatch

The **akashic** **record** is **read** by the **adept** through the **astral** **vision**; the **reading** **requires** the **spiritual** **preparation**; the **reading** **requires** the **spiritual** **chain**; the **reading** **requires** the **spiritual** **gift** of **the** **adept**. The **big** **data** is **read** by the **data** **analyst** through the **SQL** **query** or the **Python** **script**; the **reading** **requires** the **technical** **preparation**; the **reading** **requires** the **technical** **tools**; the **reading** **requires** the **technical** **skill** of the **analyst**.

The **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **reading**; the **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **methodology**. The **akashic** **reading** is **a** **spiritual** **act**; the **big** **data** **reading** is **a** **technical** **act**. The **akashic** **reading** is **subjective**; the **big** **data** **reading** is **objective**. The **akashic** **reading** is **a** **gift**; the **big** **data** **reading** is **a** **skill**.

### The Moral Mismatch

The **akashic** **record** is **read** **with** **respect**; the **akashic** **record** is **read** **with** **humility**; the **akashic** **record** is **read** **with** the **spiritual** **preparation**; the **akashic** **record** is **read** **with** the **moral** **weight** of the **adept**'s **chain**. The **big** **data** is **read** **with** **technical** **tools**; the **big** **data** is **read** **with** **the** **economic** **weight** of the **corporation**; the **big** **data** is **read** **with** the **political** **weight** of the **state**; the **big** **data** is **read** **with** the **surveillance** **weight** of the **modern** **state** (per the **Zuboff** **critique**).

The **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **moral** **weight**; the **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **act**. The **akashic** **reading** is **a** **moral** **act**; the **big** **data** **reading** is **a** **technical** **act** (per the **Floridi** **philosophy** of **information**).

## The Refusal

The **claim** that **the** **big** **data** is **the** **akashic** **record** is **rejected**.
