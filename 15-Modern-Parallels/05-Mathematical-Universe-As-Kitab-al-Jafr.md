# The Mathematical Universe as Kitab al-Jafr — A Critical Mapping

## The Honesty Note

This is **not** a foundational claim. This is a **careful** **comparison** between **Max** **Tegmark**'s **Mathematical** **Universe** **Hypothesis** (the **claim** that **the** **physical** **universe** **is** **a** **mathematical** **structure**) and the ***kitab*** **al**-***jafr*** (the **Islamic** **doctrine** of the **divinatory** **arithmetic** of the **letters**, attributed to the **Imam** **Ja'far** **al**-**Sadiq**). The **comparison** **works** in **some** **places**; the **comparison** **breaks** in **others**. The **comparison** is **a** **lens** **for** **thinking**, **not** **a** **proof** of **equivalence**.

The **previous** **draft** of the **library** had **a** **claim** that **the** **mathematical** **universe** is **the** **modern** **kitab** **al**-**jafr**. The **claim** **is** **too** **strong**; the **claim** **is** **hedged** **in** **this** **doc**. The **mathematical** **universe** is **a** **modern** **mathematical** **hypothesis**; the ***kitab*** **al**-**jafr** is **a** **medieval** **Islamic** **divinatory** **practice**. The **two** are **structural** **analogues** in **some** **places**; the **two** are **not** **identical**.

## The Mathematical Universe — The Real Hypothesis

The **Mathematical** **Universe** **Hypothesis** (**MUH**) is the **claim** that **the** **physical** **universe** **is** **a** **mathematical** **structure**. The **MUH** is **proposed** by **Max** **Tegmark** in **his** **2008** **paper** "**The** **Mathematical** **Universe**" (published in **Foundations** **of** **Physics**) and **in** **his** **2014** **book** **Our** **Mathematical** **Universe**. The **MUH** is **the** **strongest** **form** of **Pythagoreanism** in the **modern** **physics**.

The **MUH** **is** **based** on **four** **levels** of the **mathematical** **structure** of the **universe** (per **Tegmark**):

1. **Level 1: The classical reality** — the **universe** is **described** by the **classical** **mechanics** (the **Newtonian** **mechanics**); the **universe** is **a** **mathematical** **structure** that **obeys** the **Newtonian** **laws**.
2. **Level 2: The quantum reality** — the **universe** is **described** by the **quantum** **mechanics**; the **universe** is **a** **mathematical** **structure** that **obeys** the **Schrödinger** **equation** (or the **Dirac** **equation**, or the **Standard** **Model**).
3. **Level 3: The cosmological reality** — the **universe** **includes** **all** **the** **space** and **all** the **time**; the **universe** is **a** **mathematical** **structure** that **includes** **the** **multiverse** (per the **eternal** **inflation** **theory**).
4. **Level 4: The mathematical reality** — the **universe** is **a** **mathematical** **structure**; the **universe** **is** **the** **mathematical** **structure**; the **physical** **universe** **is** **identical** to the **mathematical** **structure**.

The **MUH** **is** **a** **strong** **claim**; the **MUH** **is** **the** **claim** that the **physical** **universe** **is** **a** **mathematical** **structure**. The **MUH** **is** **not** **a** **claim** that the **mathematical** **structure** **describes** the **physical** **universe**; the **MUH** **is** **a** **claim** that the **mathematical** **structure** **is** the **physical** **universe**. The **MUH** **is** **a** **form** of **mathematical** **monism**.

The **MUH** **is** **a** **specific** **hypothesis** **within** the **physics**; the **MUH** **is** **a** **testable** **hypothesis** (the **MUH** **predicts** **specific** **observable** **consequences**, such as the **existence** of the **multiverse**). The **MUH** **is** **not** **a** **metaphysical** **claim**; the **MUH** **is** **a** **physical** **claim**.

## The Kitab al-Jafr — The Real Foundation

The ***kitab*** **al**-***jafr*** (Arabic كتاب الجفر, "the Book of Jafr") is the **Islamic** **doctrine** of the **divinatory** **arithmetic** of the **letters**. The ***jafr*** **is** **attributed** to the **sixth** **Shia** **Imam** **Ja'far** **al**-**Sadiq** (**c.** **700**-**765** **CE**), the **founder** of the **Ja'fari** **school** of the **Shia** **jurisprudence**. The ***jafr*** **is** **the** **arithmetic** **practice** of the **divination**; the ***jafr*** **is** **based** **on** the **abjad** **numerical** **values** of the **letters**.

The ***jafr*** **is** **the** **practice** **of**:
1. **Taking** a **text** (the **Quran**, the **hadith**, the **sahifa**)
2. **Mapping** the **letters** to the **abjad** **values** (see **[[02-Hurufiyya-Lettrism/02-Abjad-Numerals]]**)
3. **Performing** an **arithmetic** **operation** (the **sum**, the **root**, the **product**, the **modular** **arithmetic**)
4. **Interpreting** the **result** (as a **date**, a **name**, an **event**)

The ***jafr*** **is** **a** **divinatory** **practice**; the ***jafr*** **is** **the** **arithmetic** **of** the **divination**; the ***jafr*** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of the ***'ilm*** **al**-***huruf*** **of** the **Shia** **tradition** (the ***'ilm*** **al**-***huruf*** **is** **the** **science** of the **letters**; the ***jafr*** **is** **the** **divinatory** **branch** of the ***'ilm*** **al**-***huruf***).

The ***jafr*** **is** **attributed** to **Ja'far** **al**-**Sadiq** **in** the **Shia** **tradition**; the ***jafr*** **is** **attributed** to the **Imams** **of** the **Ahl** **al**-**Bayt** in the **later** **Shia** **tradition** (the **Ja'fari** **tradition**). The ***jafr*** **is** **not** **a** **specific** **book**; the ***jafr*** **is** **a** **specific** **practice**; the ***jafr*** **is** **a** **specific** **methodology**; the ***jafr*** **is** **not** **a** **specific** **text** in the **canonical** **Shia** **tradition** (the ***jafr*** **is** **not** in the **Quran**, the ***jafr*** **is** **not** in the **Nahj** **al**-**Balagha**, the ***jafr*** **is** **not** in the ***al**-**Kafi***).

The ***jafr*** **is** **a** **specific** **Islamic** **tradition**; the ***jafr*** **is** **not** **the** **same** as the ***raml*** (the **geomancy**); the ***jafr*** **is** **not** **the** **same** as the ***'ilm*** **al**-***nujum*** (the **astrology**); the ***jafr*** **is** **a** **specific** **divinatory** **practice** of the **letters**.

## The Comparison — What Works and What Breaks

### What the Comparison Does Capture

Three points of **legitimate** structural **comparison**:

1. The **arithmetic** **of** the **letters**. The ***jafr*** **is** **based** **on** the **abjad** **numerical** **values** of the **letters**; the **abjad** **values** **are** the **foundation** of the ***jafr***; the **abjad** **values** **transform** the **letters** to **numbers**; the **numbers** **are** **operated** on **arithmetically**; the **result** is **interpreted**. The **MUH** **is** **based** **on** the **mathematical** **structure** of the **universe**; the **mathematical** **structure** **transforms** the **physical** **reality** to **mathematical** **objects**; the **mathematical** **objects** **are** **operated** on **mathematically**; the **result** is **the** **prediction**. The **two** are **arithmetic** **analogues**; the **two** are **not** **identical**.

2. The **transformation** of the **input** to the **output** **by** **arithmetic**. The ***jafr*** **transforms** the **text** to the **arithmetic** **result**; the **transformation** is **the** **specific** **operation** of the ***jafr***. The **MUH** **transforms** the **physical** **observation** to the **mathematical** **prediction**; the **transformation** is **the** **specific** **operation** of the **MUH**. The **two** are **transformation** **analogues**; the **two** are **not** **identical** (the ***jafr*** **is** **a** **divinatory** **transformation**; the **MUH** **is** **a** **predictive** **transformation**).

3. The **mathematical** **structure** of the **inputs** and the **outputs**. The ***jafr*** **has** the **mathematical** **structure** of the **abjad** **values**; the **abjad** **values** are **a** **mathematical** **structure**. The **MUH** **has** the **mathematical** **structure** of the **physical** **universe**; the **physical** **universe** is **a** **mathematical** **structure**. The **two** are **mathematical** **structure** **analogues**; the **two** are **not** **identical** (the **abjad** **values** are **a** **finite** **mathematical** **structure**; the **physical** **universe** is **an** **infinite** **mathematical** **structure**).

### What the Comparison Does Not Capture

Three points where the **comparison** **breaks**:

1. The **ontological** **status** of the **mathematical** **structure**. For the **MUH**, the **mathematical** **structure** is **the** **physical** **universe**; the **mathematical** **structure** is **real**; the **mathematical** **structure** is **physical**. For the ***jafr***, the **mathematical** **structure** is **a** **symbolic** **transformation**; the **mathematical** **structure** is **a** **tool** of the **divination**; the **mathematical** **structure** is **not** **physical**. The **MUH** is **a** **physical** **claim**; the ***jafr*** is **a** **symbolic** **practice**. The **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **claim**.

2. The **moral** **weight** of the **practice**. For the **MUH**, the **practice** is **technical**; the **practice** is **the** **tool** of the **physicist**; the **practice** is **not** **the** **way** of the **human** **being**. For the ***jafr***, the **practice** is **spiritual**; the **practice** is **the** **way** of the **diviner**; the **practice** is **the** **spiritual** **discipline**. The **two** are **not** **the** **same** **kind** of **weight**.

3. The **direction** of **causation**. For the **MUH**, the **causation** is **bottom-up**; the **physical** **universe** **produces** the **mathematical** **structure**; the **mathematical** **structure** **is** the **physical** **universe**. For the ***jafr***, the **causation** is **top-down**; **God** **produces** the **letters**; the **letters** **produce** the **abjad** **values**; the **abjad** **values** **produce** the **divinatory** **result**. The **two** are **opposite** in the **direction** of **causation**.

## The Note

The **Mathematical** **Universe** **Hypothesis** is **a** **real** **modern** **hypothesis**; the **MUH** **is** **a** **testable** **claim** of the **modern** **physics**. The ***kitab*** **al**-***jafr*** is **a** **real** **medieval** **Islamic** **divinatory** **practice**; the ***jafr*** **is** **a** **specific** **arithmetic** **practice** of the **Shia** **tradition**. The **two** are **structural** **analogues** in **some** **places**; the **two** are **not** **identical**.

The **comparison** is **useful**; the **comparison** is **not** a **proof**; the **comparison** is **a** **lens**. The **practitioner** **uses** the **lens** **to** **clarify** the **practice**; the **practitioner** **does** **not** **use** the **lens** **to** **prove** the **practice**.
