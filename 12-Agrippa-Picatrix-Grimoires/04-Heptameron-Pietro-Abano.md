The **text** is **attributed** **to** **Pietro** **d'Abano** (the **Italian** **philosopher** and **physician**, **c.** **1257**-**1316** **CE**); the **text** is **not** **actually** by **Pietro** **d'Abano**; the **text** is **a** **medieval** **Latin** **compilation** that **was** **attributed** to **d'Abano** for **the** **authority**. The **text** is **likely** **a** **13th**-**c.** **Latin** **compilation** of **earlier** **sources** (the **apuleian** **tradition**, the **honorian** **tradition**, the **picatrix** **tradition**).
