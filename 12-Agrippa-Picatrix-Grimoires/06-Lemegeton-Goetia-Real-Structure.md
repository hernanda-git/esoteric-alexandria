# The Lemegeton — The Lesser Key of Solomon, Real Structure

## The Real Document

The **Lemegeton** (Greek **λημηγετών**, "**the** **little** **collector**" — the **title** **means** "**the** **compendium** of **the** **Solomonic** **magick**") is **a** **Western** **grimoire** **of** **the** **Solomonic** **tradition**. The **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **compendium** of **the** **5** **books** **of** **the** **Solomonic** **magick**; the **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **most** **comprehensive** **single** **grimoire** **of** **the** **Western** **Solomonic** **tradition**.

The **Lemegeton** **is** **not** **actually** **by** **King** **Solomon** (**the** **biblical** **king**, **10th** **c.** **BCE**); the **Lemegeton** **is** **a** **17th**-**c.** **English** **compilation** of **earlier** **sources**; the **Lemegeton** **is** **attributed** **to** **Solomon** **for** **the** **authority**; the **Lemegeton** **is** **not** **actually** **by** **Solomon**. The **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **compendium** of **the** **5** **books**; the **5** **books** **are** **attributed** **to** **different** **authors**; the **5** **books** **range** **in** **date** **from** **the** **14th** **c.** to **the** **17th** **c.**

The **Lemegeton** **is** **preserved** **in** **multiple** **manuscripts**; the **most** **important** **manuscript** **is** **the** **British** **Library** **MS** **Sloane** **2731** (**the** **earliest** **known** **complete** **manuscript**, **c.** **1700**). The **Lemegeton** **is** **also** **preserved** **in** **the** **BnF** **Latin** **7156** (**the** **"**Trithemius**" **manuscript**); the **Lemegeton** **is** **also** **preserved** **in** **multiple** **other** **manuscripts** **in** **the** **European** **libraries**.

## The Real Structure — The Five Books

The **Lemegeton** **is** **divided** **into** **5** **books**; the **5** **books** **are** **distinct** **texts**; the **5** **books** **are** **not** **a** **unified** **work**; the **5** **books** **are** **a** **compilation** of **5** **distinct** **texts** **attributed** **to** **Solomon**.

### The First Book — Goetia (The Invocation of the Evil Spirits)

The **First** **Book**, **titled** **Goetia** (**Greek** **γοητεία**, "**sorcery**" or "**witchcraft**"), **is** **the** **book** **of** **the** **72** **evil** **spirits**; the **First** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton**; the **First** **Book** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **popular** **cultural** **image** of **the** **Western** **magick**.

The **First** **Book** **presents**:

- The **72** **demons** **of** **the** **Goetia** (**the** **72** **inferior** **spirits**; **the** **72** **demons** **of** **the** **Solomonic** **tradition**; **the** **72** **demons** **are** **the** **specific** **spirits** **of** **the** **Goetia**)
- The **seals** of **the** **72** **demons** (**the** **specific** **magical** **sigils** of **the** **72** **demons**; **the** **sigils** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **vessels**)
- The **manifestations** of **the** **72** **demons** (**the** **specific** **form** **that** **each** **demon** **assumes**; **the** **manifestations** **are** **the** **specific** **appearance** of **the** **demon**)
- The **conjurations** of **the** **72** **demons** (**the** **specific** **calls** of **the** **72** **demons**; **the** **conjurations** **are** **the** **specific** **words** **of** **the** **calls**)

The **First** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton**; the **First** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **translated** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton** (**Mathers**'s 1904 **translation** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **translation** of **the** **First** **Book** alone). The **First** **Book** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **popular** **cultural** **image** of **the** **72** **demons** (Bael, **Agares**, **Vassago**, **Samigina**, **and** **the** **others**).

### The Second Book — Theurgia-Goetia (The Spirits of the Cardinal Directions)

The **Second** **Book**, **titled** **Theurgia**-**Goetia** (**the** **"**divine**-**working**"**-**"**sorcery**"**, **the** **hybrid** **name** **refers** **to** **the** **fact** **that** **the** **book** **covers** **both** **the** **angelic** **and** **the** **demonic** **spirits**), **is** **the** **book** of **the** **31** **astral** **spirits**; the **Second** **Book** **is** **the** **complement** of **the** **First** **Book**.

The **Second** **Book** **presents**:

- The **31** **astral** **spirits** (**the** **31** **celestial** **and** **infernal** **spirits**; **the** **spirits** **are** **the** **specific** **astral** **spirits** of **the** **cardinal** **directions**; **the** **spirits** **are** **governed** by **the** **specific** **planetary** **forces**)
- The **seals** of **the** **31** **astral** **spirits** (**the** **specific** **magical** **sigils** of **the** **31** **astral** **spirits**)
- The **manifestations** of **the** **31** **astral** **spirits** (**the** **specific** **form** **that** **each** **astral** **spirit** **assumes**)
- The **conjurations** of **the** **31** **astral** **spirits** (**the** **specific** **calls** of **the** **31** **astral** **spirits**)

The **Second** **Book** **is** **less** **famous** **than** **the** **First** **Book**; the **Second** **Book** **is** **preserved** **only** **in** **a** **few** **manuscripts**; the **Second** **Book** **is** **not** **in** **Mathers**'s **translation** (**Mathers** **translated** **only** **the** **First** **Book**).

### The Third Book — Pauline Art (The Art of the Angels of the Hours)

The **Third** **Book**, **titled** **Paulina** (**after** **the** **Apostle** **Paul**, **the** **attribution** **is** **legendary**; **the** **book** **is** **likely** **the** **work** of **a** **16th**-**c.** **German** **compiler**), **is** **the** **book** of **the** **planetary** **angels**; the **Third** **Book** **is** **the** **book** of **the** **planetary** **angels** **of** **the** **hours**.

The **Third** **Book** **presents**:

- The **angels** of **the** **hours** (**the** **specific** **angels** **who** **govern** **the** **specific** **hours** **of** **the** **day**; **the** **hours** **are** **the** **planetary** **hours**)
- The **angels** of **the** **days** (**the** **specific** **angels** **who** **govern** **the** **specific** **days** **of** **the** **week**; **the** **days** **are** **the** **planetary** **days**)
- The **angels** of **the** **signs** (**the** **specific** **angels** **who** **govern** **the** **specific** **zodiac** **signs**)
- The **seals** of **the** **angels** (**the** **specific** **magical** **sigils** of **the** **angels**)
- The **conjurations** of **the** **angels** (**the** **specific** **calls** of **the** **angels**)

The **Third** **Book** **is** **the** **book** of **the** **planetary** **angels**; the **Third** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **systematic** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton**; the **Third** **Book** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Western** **planetary** **angelic** **magic**.

### The Fourth Book — Almadel (The Altitudes of the Zodiac)

The **Fourth** **Book**, **titled** **Almadel** (**the** **name** **is** **derived** **from** **the** **Hebrew**; **the** **Almadel** **is** **a** **wax** **tablet** **inscribed** **with** **the** **zodiac** **signs**), **is** **the** **book** of **the** **zodiacal** **angels**; the **Fourth** **Book** **is** **the** **book** of **the** **angels** **of** **the** **zodiacal** **altitudes**.

The **Fourth** **Book** **presents**:

- The **construction** of **the** **Almadel** (**the** **wax** **tablet** **inscribed** **with** **the** **zodiac** **signs**; **the** **Almadel** **is** **the** **operating** **device** of **the** **ritual**)
- The **angels** of **the** **first** **altitude** (**the** **specific** **angels** **of** **the** **first** **altitude**; **the** **first** **altitude** **is** **Aries**, **Taurus**, **Gemini**)
- The **angels** of **the** **second** **altitude** (**the** **specific** **angels** **of** **the** **second** **altitude**; **the** **second** **altitude** **is** **Cancer**, **Leo**, **Virgo**)
- The **angels** of **the** **third** **altitude** (**the** **specific** **angels** **of** **the** **third** **altitude**; **the** **third** **altitude** **is** **Libra**, **Scorpio**, **Sagittarius**)
- The **angels** of **the** **fourth** **altitude** (**the** **specific** **angels** **of** **the** **fourth** **altitude**; **the** **fourth** **altitude** **is** **Capricorn**, **Aquarius**, **Pisces**)
- The **conjurations** of **the** **zodiacal** **angels** (**the** **specific** **calls** of **the** **zodiacal** **angels**)

The **Fourth** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **specific** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton**; the **Fourth** **Book** **is** **the** **book** of **the** **zodiacal** **ritual**; the **Fourth** **Book** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Western** **zodiacal** **angelic** **magic**.

### The Fifth Book — Ars Notoria (The Notary Art of Solomon)

The **Fifth** **Book**, **titled** **Ars** **Notoria** (**the** **"**notary**"** **art**; **the** **art** **of** **the** **immediate** **knowledge**), **is** **the** **book** of **the** **rhetoric** **and** **memory**; the **Fifth** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton** (**the** **Ars** **Notoria** **text** **dates** **to** **the** **13th**-**14th** **c.**; **the** **Ars** **Notoria** **is** **a** **medieval** **prayer** **and** **meditation** **manual**).

The **Fifth** **Book** **presents**:

- The **prayers** **for** **the** **gifts** (**the** **specific** **prayers** **for** **the** **specific** **gifts**; **the** **gifts** **are** **the** **specific** **abilities** **of** **the** **mind**; **the** **gifts** **include** **memory**, **understanding**, **eloquence**, **and** **the** **other** **mental** **gifts**)
- The **prayers** **for** **the** **arts** (**the** **specific** **prayers** **for** **the** **specific** **arts**; **the** **arts** **are** **the** **specific** **disciplines** **of** **the** **medieval** **university**; **the** **arts** **include** **grammar**, **logic**, **rhetoric**, **arithmetic**, **music**, **geometry**, **astronomy**)
- The **figures** **of** **the** **prayers** (**the** **specific** **magical** **sigils** **of** **the** **prayers**; **the** **figures** **are** **the** **specific** **diagrams** **of** **the** **prayers**)
- The **operation** **of** **the** **prayers** (**the** **specific** **operation** **of** **the** **prayers**; **the** **operation** **is** **the** **specific** **practice** of **the** **prayers**; **the** **operation** **involves** **the** **specific** **recitation**, **the** **specific** **meditation**, **the** **specific** **visualization**)

The **Fifth** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **different** **part** of **the** **Lemegeton**; the **Fifth** **Book** **is** **a** **medieval** **prayer** **manual**; the **Fifth** **Book** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Western** **meditation** **tradition**.

## The Real Influence

The **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **most** **influential** **single** **grimoire** **of** **the** **Western** **Solomonic** **tradition**; the **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **canonical** **source** of **the** **Western** **Solomonic** **magick**; the **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **grimoire** in **the** **Western** **tradition** (**the** **Mathers**'s **translation** of **the** **First** **Book** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **translation** of **the** **most** **famous** **grimoire**).

The **Lemegeton** **influences**:

- **Agrippa**'s **Three** **Books** **of** **Occult** **Philosophy** (1533)
- **The** **modern** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition** (**the** **Golden** **Dawn**, **Crowley**'s **A∴A∴**, **the** **OTO**, **the** **Typhonian** **OTO**)
- **The** **modern** **Satanic** **tradition** (**the** **Church** **of** **Satan**, **the** **Temple** **of** **Set**)
- **The** **popular** **culture** (**the** **horror** **movies**, **the** **fantasy** **novels**, **the** **video** **games**)

The **Lemegeton** **is** **the** **most** **influential** **single** **grimoire** **of** **the** **Western** **tradition**.
