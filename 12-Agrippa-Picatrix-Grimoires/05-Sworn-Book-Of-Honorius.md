# The Sworn Book of Honorius — The Liber Juratus

## The Real Document

The **Sworn Book of Honorius** (Latin **Liber Juratus Honorii**, the "Sworn Book" of the **legendary** **magus** **Honorius**) is **a** **medieval** **Latin** **grimoire** of **the** **conjurations** of the **planetary** **spirits** and the **astral** **intelligences**. The **text** is **preserved** in **multiple** **Latin** **manuscripts**; the **text** is **a** **real** **historical** **document**; the **text** is **a** **real** **Western** **magical** **text**.

The **text** is **attributed** to **Honorius** **of** **Thebes** (a **legendary** **medieval** **magus**); the **text** is **not** **actually** by **Honorius** **of** **Thebes**; the **text** is **a** **13th**-**14th**-**c.** **Latin** **compilation** that **was** **attributed** to **Honorius** **for** **the** **authority**. The **text** is **sometimes** **confused** **with** **Pope** **Honorius** **III** (the **13th**-**c.** **pope**); the **text** is **not** **by** **Pope** **Honorius** **III**; the **confusion** is **a** **medieval** **error** that **has** **persisted** in **the** **popular** **literature**.

The **text** is **a** **real** **Latin** **manuscript**; the **text** is **preserved** in **multiple** **manuscripts** in **the** **European** **libraries**; the **text** is **preserved** in **at** **least** **14** **Latin** **manuscripts** (the **earliest** **manuscript** is **the** **14th**-**c.** **manuscript** **at** **the** **British** **Library**; the **manuscript** is **Latin** **MS** **Sloane** **313**; the **manuscript** is **the** **textus** **receptus** of **the** **Latin** **text**).

## The Real Structure — The Three Books

The **Sworn** **Book** **is** **divided** **into** **3** **books**; the **3** **books** **have** **specific** **content**; the **3** **books** **are** **a** **specific** **form** of the **conjuratory** **tradition**.

### Book I — The Principles

The **first** **book** **deals** with **the** **principles** of **the** **conjurations**; the **first** **book** **is** **the** **theoretical** **foundation** of **the** **text**; the **first** **book** **discusses**:

- The **preliminary** **fasts** (the **specific** **fasts** **for** **the** **specific** **operations**; the **fasts** **are** **a** **specific** **number** of **days**; the **fasts** **vary** **by** **the** **planetary** **day**)
- The **preliminary** **baptisms** (the **specific** **baptisms** **for** **the** **specific** **operations**; the **baptisms** **are** **the** **specific** **consecration** of **the** **magician**)
- The **planetary** **vessels** (the **specific** **vessels** **for** **the** **planetary** **operations**; the **vessels** **are** **made** **of** **the** **specific** **planetary** **metals**)
- The **planetary** **rings** (the **specific** **rings** **for** **the** **planetary** **operations**; the **rings** **are** **made** **of** **the** **specific** **planetary** **metals**; the **rings** **are** **inscribed** **with** **the** **specific** **planetary** **sigils**)
- The **planetary** **vestments** (the **specific** **vestments** **for** **the** **planetary** **operations**; the **vestments** **are** **made** **of** **the** **specific** **planetary** **colors**)
- The **preliminary** **prayers** (the **specific** **prayers** **for** **the** **specific** **operations**; the **prayers** **are** **the** **specific** **calls** of **the** **planetary** **powers**)

The **first** **book** **is** **the** **most** **systematic** of **the** **3** **books**; the **first** **book** **is** **a** **manual** of **the** **preparatory** **disciplines**; the **first** **book** **is** **a** **manual** of **the** **planetary** **ritual** **preparation**.

### Book II — The Conjurations

The **second** **book** **deals** with **the** **conjurations** of **the** **planetary** **spirits**; the **second** **book** **is** **the** **practical** **core** of **the** **text**; the **second** **book** **discusses**:

- The **conjuration** of **the** **Saturn** **spirit** (**Caziel**, the **7** **planetary** **intelligences** of **Saturn**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Caziel**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Saturn** **hour**)
- The **conjuration** of **the** **Jupiter** **spirit** (**Sachiel**, the **intelligence** of **Jupiter**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Sachiel**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Jupiter** **hour**)
- The **conjuration** of **the** **Mars** **spirit** (**Samael**, the **intelligence** of **Mars**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Samael**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Mars** **hour**)
- The **conjuration** of **the** **Sun** **spirit** (**Michael**, the **intelligence** of **the** **Sun**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Michael**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Sun** **hour**)
- The **conjuration** of **the** **Venus** **spirit** (**Anael**, the **intelligence** of **Venus**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Anael**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Venus** **hour**)
- The **conjuration** of **the** **Mercury** **spirit** (**Raphael**, the **intelligence** of **Mercury**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Raphael**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Mercury** **hour**)
- The **conjuration** of **the** **Moon** **spirit** (**Gabriel**, the **intelligence** of **the** **Moon**; the **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **Gabriel**; the **conjuration** **is** **made** **at** **the** **Moon** **hour**)

The **second** **book** **is** **the** **most** **practical** of **the** **3** **books**; the **second** **book** **is** **a** **manual** of **the** **conjurations**; the **second** **book** **is** **the** **manual** that **is** **used** **by** **the** **medieval** **magicians**.

### Book III — The Specific Operations

The **third** **book** **deals** with **the** **specific** **operations**; the **third** **book** **is** **the** **practical** **conclusion** of **the** **text**; the **third** **book** **discusses**:

- The **operation** of **the** **planetary** **conjurations** (the **specific** **operations** **for** **the** **specific** **purposes**; the **operations** **are** **the** **specific** **effects** of **the** **planetary** **conjurations**)
- The **operation** of **the** **talismanic** **manufacture** (the **specific** **manufacture** **of** **the** **planetary** **talismans**; the **manufacture** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **the** **planetary** **talismans**)
- The **operation** of **the** **planetary** **divination** (the **specific** **divinations** **of** **the** **planetary** **spirits**; the **divinations** **are** **the** **specific** **answers** of **the** **planetary** **spirits**)
- The **operation** of **the** **planetary** **healing** (the **specific** **healings** **of** **the** **planetary** **spirits**; the **healings** **are** **the** **specific** **cures** of **the** **planetary** **diseases**)

The **third** **book** **is** **the** **most** **specific** of **the** **3** **books**; the **third** **book** **is** **a** **manual** of **the** **specific** **operations**; the **third** **book** **is** **the** **manual** that **is** **used** **by** **the** **medieval** **magicians** **for** **the** **specific** **effects**.

## The Real Plan — The Operating Manual

The **Sworn** **Book** **is** **a** **practical** **conjuratory** **manual**; the **Sworn** **Book** **is** **a** **specific** **plan** of **the** **conjuratory** **ritual**; the **Sworn** **Book** **is** **the** **specific** **plan** of **the** **conjuratory** **ritual** in **the** **Western** **medieval** **tradition**.

The **plan** of **the** **Sworn** **Book** **is**:

1. **The** **preparation** (**the** **fast** **for** **7**-**9**-**30** **days** **depending** on **the** **operation**; **the** **specific** **fast** **for** **the** **specific** **planet**)
2. **The** **consecration** of **the** **magician** (**the** **baptism**; **the** **vestment**; **the** **ring**; **the** **vessel**)
3. **The** **consecration** of **the** **place** (**the** **specific** **incense**; **the** **specific** **prayer**; **the** **specific** **timing**)
4. **The** **drawing** of **the** **circle** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **circle**; **the** **circle** **is** **the** **operating** **area**)
5. **The** **inscription** of **the** **names** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **names**; **the** **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **circle**)
6. **The** **fumigation** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **incense**; **the** **incense** **is** **burned** **on** **the** **circle**)
7. **The** **conjuration** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **conjuration**; **the** **conjuration** **is** **the** **specific** **call** of **the** **planetary** **spirit**)
8. **The** **manifestation** (**the** **manifestation** of **the** **planetary** **spirit**; **the** **manifestation** **is** **the** **specific** **response** **to** **the** **conjuration**)
9. **The** **conversation** (**the** **conversation** **with** **the** **planetary** **spirit**; **the** **conversation** **is** **the** **specific** **dialogue**)
10. **The** **license** **to** **depart** (**the** **license** **to** **depart**; **the** **license** **is** **the** **specific** **dismissal**)

The **plan** of **the** **Sworn** **Book** **is** **similar** **to** **the** **plan** of **the** **Heptameron** (see **[[04-Heptameron-Pietro-Abano]]**); the **plan** of **the** **Sworn** **Book** **is** **the** **standard** **plan** of **the** **Western** **conjuratory** **tradition**.

- **The** **modern** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition** (**the** **Golden** **Dawn**, **Crowley**'s **A∴A∴**, **the** **OTO**)

The **Sworn** **Book** **is** **the** **canonical** **source** of **the** **planetary** **conjuratory** **tradition**; **the** **Sworn** **Book** **is** **a** **specific** **medieval** **Western** **grimoire**.
