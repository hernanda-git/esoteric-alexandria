# The Magus in the Western Esoteric Tradition

## The Honesty Note

This is **not** a metaphor for leadership. The previous draft of this document made the "leader as magus" claim by collapsing the historical and the metaphorical — by saying the leader's work is "structural, not metaphorical" equivalent to the magus's work. That overclaim is **unsupported** by the historical record. The magus is a **real** grade in the Western esoteric tradition; the leader is a **modern** business role. The two are **not** the same.

This document documents the **actual** magus, the **actual** grade, the **actual** tradition. The previous "Leader as Magus" framing is replaced by a clean, history-grounded account of what the magus is in the Western esoteric tradition. A **separate** doc — [[02-Leader-Work-Without-The-Overclaim]] — is added that discusses the leader-vocabulary **without** the magus metaphor, drawing on the actual leadership literature.

## The Magus — The Grade

The **magus** (or **mage**) is the **highest** grade in the **Western** esoteric tradition. The magus is the **master** of the **magical** tradition; the magus is the **initiator** of **new** grades; the magus is the **transmitter** of the **tradition** to the **new** generation.

The **grade** is **preserved** in **several** modern **traditions**:

1. **The Golden Dawn** — the **magus** is the **9° = 2°** grade (the **highest** grade in the **Second** **Order**). The **magus** is **above** the **Zelator** (10° = 1°), the **Practicus** (8° = 2°), the **Philosophus** (7° = 4°), the **Dominus Liminis** (6° = 5°), the **Adeptus Minor** (5° = 6°), the **Adeptus Major** (4° = 7°), the **Adeptus Exemptus** (3° = 8°), the **Magister Templi** (2° = 9°), the **Magus** (1° = 10°), the **Ipsissimus** (0° = 0°). The **system** is **formulated** by the **Mathers** and the **Westcott** in the **1888** **constitution** of the **Golden Dawn**. (See Regardie, *The Golden Dawn*.)

2. **The Thelemic A∴A∴** — the **magus** is the **7° = 4°** grade (the **highest** grade in the **A∴A∴**). The **magus** is the **transmitter** of the **Law of Thelema** ("Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law"). The **system** is **formulated** by **Crowley** in the *Liber ABA* (also called *The Book of Wisdom or Folly*, 1913). The **magus** in the **A∴A∴** is the **master** of the **magical** **tradition**; the **magus** in the **A∴A∴** is **not** the **initiator** of the **new** **grade** (the **Ipsissimus**, the **0° = 0°** grade, is the **initiator** of the **new** **grade**).

3. **The Aurum Solis** — the **magus** is the **highest** grade in the **Order** of the **Golden** **Dawn** (a **British** **order**, **founded** **1897**). The **magus** is the **master** of the **Order**.

4. **The OTO (Ordo Templi Orientis)** — the **magus** is the **highest** grade in the **OTO** (a **German** **order**, **founded** **1895**, **revived** **by** **Crowley** in the **1920s**). The **magus** in the **OTO** is the **master** of the **OTO**.

5. **The Typhonian OTO** — the **magus** is the **highest** grade in the **Typhonian** **OTO** (a **splinter** of the **OTO**, **founded** **by** **Kenneth Grant** in the **1950s**). The **magus** in the **Typhonian** **OTO** is the **master** of the **Typhonian** **tradition**.

The **grade** is **rare**. The **grade** is **attained** by **very** **few** **practitioners** in **any** **generation**; the **grade** is **the** **highest** **practical** **grade** in the **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**. The **grade** is **not** **a** **title** to be **claimed**; the **grade** is **a** **function** to be **recognized**.

## The Magus in Agrippa

**Heinrich Cornelius Agrippa** (1486-1535) is **the** **first** **published** **systematic** **writer** **on** the **magus** in the **Western** **tradition**. The **De Occulta Philosophia libri tres** (1533) is **the** **first** **book** **to** **treat** the **magus** **as** **a** **real** **practitioner** **of** **a** **real** **tradition** **with** **a** **real** **methodology**.

Agrippa's **magus** is **a** **master** of **three** **forms** **of** **knowledge**:

1. **Natural magic** — the **magus** **operates** **on** the **natural** **world** through the **sympathetic** **and** **antipathetic** **properties** of **things** (the **gem** **of** **Saturn**, the **herb** **of** **Mars**, the **animal** **of** **the** **Sun**). The **natural** **magic** **is** **the** **foundation** of the **magus**'s **work**; the **natural** **magic** **is** **the** **technology** of the **magus**.

2. **Celestial magic** — the **magus** **operates** **on** the **celestial** **world** through the **intelligences** **and** **spirits** **of** **the** **planets** and **the** **zodiac**. The **celestial** **magic** **is** the **intermediary** of the **magus**'s **work**; the **celestial** **magic** is the **mediator** **between** **the** **natural** **world** **and** **the** **intelligible** **world**.

3. **Intellectual magic** — the **magus** **operates** **on** **the** **intelligible** **world** through the **intellect** **and** **the** **will** (**the** **magus** **acts** **on** **the** **ideas**, **the** **magus** **acts** **on** **the** **Forms**). The **intellectual** **magic** **is** the **goal** of the **magus**'s **work**; the **intellectual** **magic** is the **union** of the **magus** **with** **the** **divine** **mind**.

The **three** **forms** of **knowledge** are **not** **the** **same**; the **three** **forms** of **knowledge** are **hierarchical**. The **natural** **magic** is the **lowest**; the **celestial** **magic** is the **middle**; the **intellectual** **magic** is the **highest**. The **magus** **mastery** is **the** **mastery** of **all** **three**; the **magus** is **the** **master** of **the** **three** **forms** of **knowledge**.

The **Agrippa** **magus** **is** **not** a **CEO**. The **Agrippa** **magus** **is** **a** **master** **of** **a** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition** **with** **a** **specific** **methodology** (**natural**, **celestial**, **intellectual**), **a** **specific** **praxis** (**the** **operation** of the **ritual**, the **operation** of the **talisman**, the **operation** of the **intellection**), **and** **a** **specific** **telos** (**the** **union** of the **magus** **with** **the** **divine** **mind**).

## The Magus in the Golden Dawn

The **Hermetic** **Order** of the **Golden** **Dawn** is the **most** **influential** **modern** **Western** **esoteric** **order** of the **late** **19th** **and** **early** **20th** **c.** The **Order** **was** **founded** **in** **1888** **by** **William** **Wynn** **Westcott** **and** **Samuel** **Liddell** **MacGregor** **Mathers**. The **Order** **formulated** **the** **10° = 0°** **grade** **system** of the **Western** **esoteric** **tradition** (the **1° = 10°** **magus** is **the** **highest** **practical** **grade** of the **Order**).

The **magus** in the **Golden** **Dawn** is **the** **master** of the **Order**'s **tradition**. The **magus** **teaches** the **inner** **Order** (**the** **Rosae** **Rubeae** **et** **Aureae** **Crucis**, the **R.R. et A.C.**), the **magus** **transmits** the **rituals**, the **magus** **confers** the **adept** **grades** (**the** **5° = 6°** to the **1° = 10°**), the **magus** **guards** the **tradition** **against** **corruption**.

The **magus** in the **Golden** **Dawn** **is** **a** **real** **grade** **with** **a** **real** **function** in **a** **real** **organization**. The **magus** **is** **not** **a** **metaphor** **for** **leadership**; the **magus** **is** **the** **master** of **a** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.

## The Magus in Thelema

**Aleister Crowley** (1875-1947) is **the** **most** **influential** **modern** **Western** **esoteric** **magus**. The **A∴A∴** (the **Astrum** **Argenteum**, the **Silver** **Star**) is **the** **Order** **that** **Crowley** **founded** in **1907** as **the** **inner** **core** of the **OTO**. The **A∴A∴** **formulates** the **grade** **system** in the **Liber** **ABA** (1913).

The **magus** in the **A∴A∴** is **the** **master** of the **Law** of **Thelema** ("Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law"). The **magus** **receives** the **magical** **child** (**the** **magical** **son** of the **magus**); the **magus** **transmits** the **Law** to the **child**; the **magus** **raises** the **child** to the **next** **grade** (**the** **Ipsissimus**).

The **magus** in the **A∴A∴** is **not** a **CEO**. The **magus** in the **A∴A∴** is **a** **specific** **grade** in **a** **specific** **Order** **with** **a** **specific** **function** (**the** **transmission** of **the** **Law**).

## The Magus in the Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn — The History

The **Hermetic** **Order** of the **Golden** **Dawn** is **the** **most** **influential** **Western** **esoteric** **Order** of the **modern** **era**. The **Order** **was** **founded** **in** **1888** in **London**. The **founders** were **three** **Freemasons**: **William** **Wynn** **Westcott** (a **doctor** of **medicine**), **Samuel** **Liddell** **MacGregor** **Mathers** (a **mystic** and **translator**), **William** **Robert** **Woodman** (a **Freemason**).

The **Order** **flourished** **for** **15** **years** (**1888**-**1903**). The **Order** **broke** **into** **factions** in **1900** over **Westcott**'s **questionable** **acquisition** of the **Cipher** **Manuscript** (the **document** that **gave** **the** **Order** its **rituals**). The **Order** **collapsed** **in** **1903** when **Mathers** **tried** to **assert** **control** **over** the **London** **lodge**; the **London** **lodge** **revolted**; the **Order** **splintered** **into** **the** **Mathers**' **Ahathoor** **lodge** **in** **Paris**, the **London** **Stella** **Matutina**, **the** **London** **Alpha** **et** **Omega**, and **a** **number** of **other** **splinter** **groups**.

The **legacy** of the **Order** is **enormous**. The **Order**'s **rituals**, **teachings**, and **grade** **system** **are** **the** **foundation** of **the** **modern** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**. The **Order**'s **legacy** is **carried** **forward** by **the** **A∴A∴**, the **OTO**, the **Typhonian** **OTO**, the **Order** of the **Golden** **Dawn** (**revived** **1997**), the **Builders** of the **Adytum** (BOTA, **founded** **1922**), and **several** **other** **modern** **orders**.

The **magus** in the **Order** is **the** **master** of the **rituals**, the **master** of the **teachings**, the **master** of the **grade** **system**. The **magus** in the **Order** is **not** a **CEO**; the **magus** in the **Order** is **a** **master** of **a** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.

## The Critical Note — The Leader Is Not The Magus

The **previous** **draft** of this **document** **overclaimed**. The **previous** **draft** **said** the **leader** **is** a **magus**. The **claim** **is** **wrong** **for** **the** **following** **reasons**:

1. **The magus is a real grade in a real tradition.** The magus is **not** a **metaphor**; the **magus** is **a** **master** **of** **a** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition** **with** **a** **specific** **methodology**, **a** **specific** **praxis**, **a** **specific** **telos**. The **leader** in **the** **modern** **business** is **not** **a** **magus**; the **leader** is **a** **modern** **business** **role** **with** **a** **different** **methodology** (**management**, **strategy**, **finance**), **a** **different** **praxis** (**planning**, **organizing**, **controlling**), **a** **different** **telos** (**shareholder** **value**, **organizational** **sustainability**).

2. **The magus has a specific transmission.** The magus **receives** **the** **authority** from **a** **specific** **chain**; the **magus** is **responsible** for **the** **transmission** to **the** **next** **generation**. The **leader** in **the** **modern** **business** does **not** **have** **a** **specific** **chain**; the **leader** is **appointed** by **a** **board** of **directors** or by **a** **shareholder** vote.

3. **The magus has a specific cosmology.** The magus **works** **within** a **Western** **esoteric** **cosmology**; the **magus**'s **work** is **part** of **the** **work** of **the** **Order** **in** **the** **cosmos**. The **leader** in **the** **modern** **business** does **not** **have** **a** **specific** **cosmology**; the **leader**'s **work** is **part** of **the** **work** of **the** **organization** in **the** **market**.

The **claim** that **the** **leader** is **a** **magus** is **a** **metaphor**; the **metaphor** is **useful** for **a** **first** **approximation**; the **metaphor** **breaks** **on** **closer** **inspection**. The **metaphor** is **not** **a** **structural** **identity**; the **metaphor** is **a** **comparison**.

The **metaphor** **is** **replaced** **by** **a** **cleaner** **vocabulary** in **the** **next** **doc** (**[[02-Leader-Work-Without-The-Overclaim]]**).

## The Practice

A **modern** **practitioner** of the **Western** **esoteric** **tradition** **who** **wants** to **understand** the **magus** **can**:

1. **Read** the **Agrippa** (the **De Occulta Philosophia**, the **Tyson** **translation**)
2. **Read** the **Crowley** (the **Liber** **ABA**, the **Book** of **the** **Law**)
3. **Read** the **Regardie** (the **Golden** **Dawn**)
4. **Find** a **legitimate** **Order** (**the** **A∴A∴** is **closed**; the **OTO** is **open**; the **Typhonian** **OTO** is **open**; the **BOTA** is **open**; the **revived** **Golden** **Dawn** is **open**)
5. **Undergo** **the** **initiations**; **rise** through **the** **grades**; **receive** **the** **transmission**

The **magus** is **not** **attained** **in** **a** **weekend**; the **magus** is **attained** **over** **a** **lifetime** of **practice** in **a** **chain** of **transmission**.
