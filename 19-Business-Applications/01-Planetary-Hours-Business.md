# Planetary Hours — Electional Astrology for Business

## The Practice

**Electional astrology** is the art of choosing the **right time** to begin a specific action. The practice is the **practical application** of the planetary hour system to business decisions.

The principle: every action has a **planetary quality**; the planetary quality of the time of the action should **match** the planetary quality of the action itself. The action is **empowered** by the alignment; the action is **weakened** by the misalignment.

The practice is **ancient** (the Picatrix, Book IV, 10th-11th c. CE) and **modern** (the modern business astrology community, the electional astrology software, the planetary hour apps).

## The Seven Domains

The seven classical planets correspond to **seven business domains**. The alignment is:

| Planet | Domain | Best day of week | Best time of day | Best activities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Sun** | Authority, leadership, public relations | Sunday | 1st hour after sunrise, 8th, 15th, 22nd | Pitching to senior people, public speaking, brand launches |
| **Moon** | Customers, public, change | Monday | Same pattern | Hiring, product launches, customer service |
| **Mercury** | Commerce, communication, learning | Wednesday | Same | Sales, writing, contracts, networking, education |
| **Venus** | Relationships, partnerships, art | Friday | Same | Negotiations, partnerships, art, beauty |
| **Mars** | Action, courage, conflict | Tuesday | Same | Launches, surgery, athletics, decisive action |
| **Jupiter** | Expansion, growth, wisdom | Thursday | Same | Investments, hiring senior people, education, legal |
| **Saturn** | Structure, endurance, binding | Saturday | Same | Real estate, long-term contracts, structural decisions, banishing |

The mapping is **intuitive**: the planetary quality of the planet matches the planetary quality of the action. The Sun (authority) for pitching; Mercury (commerce) for sales; Venus (partnership) for negotiations; Mars (action) for launches; Jupiter (expansion) for investments; Saturn (structure) for real estate; Moon (public) for customer-facing actions.

## The Planetary Hours

The **planetary hours** are the **24 unequal temporal hours** of the day (12 day hours from sunrise to sunset + 12 night hours from sunset to sunrise; the day and night hours are each equal in length to each other, but the day hours differ from the night hours in length — longer in summer, shorter in winter). Each hour is ruled by one of the seven classical planets. The sequence is the **Chaldean order** (Saturn → Jupiter → Mars → Sun → Venus → Mercury → Moon) repeating. The **first hour** after sunrise is ruled by the planet of the day (Sunday = Sun, Monday = Moon, etc.).

For business use:

- The **first hour** of the day is the **most powerful** (the planet is at the maximum)
- The **8th, 15th, and 22nd hours** are the **same planet** (the peak of the same planet on the same day)
- The **night hours** (after sunset) are **less powerful** but **useful** for some operations

The **calculation** of the planetary hours requires:

1. The **local sunrise** and **sunset** times (from any astronomical source)
2. The **division** of the day (sunrise to sunset) into 12 equal hours (the **day hours**)
3. The **division** of the night (sunset to sunrise) into 12 equal hours (the **night hours**)
4. The **assignment** of the planets in the Chaldean order, starting with the planet of the day at the first day hour

The calculation is **non-trivial** (the day hours and the night hours are **not** equal — the day hours are longer in summer, shorter in winter). The modern software (the **Astrodienst** planetary hours calculator, the **iPhemeris** planetary hours, the **Time Nomad** app) handles the calculation.

## The Election

An **election** is the **choice of a specific time** for a specific action, based on the planetary hours and the other astrological factors. The election requires:

1. **The action** — what is being elected? (a contract signing, a product launch, a wedding, a journey)
2. **The planet** — which planet governs the action? (e.g., the planet Mercury for a contract)
3. **The day** — the day of the planet (Wednesday for Mercury)
4. **The hour** — the planetary hour of the planet (the 1st hour, 8th, 15th, or 22nd)
5. **The moon** — the Moon should be in a **favorable sign** (the sign of the planet, or a friendly sign)
6. **The malefics** — the malefics (Mars, Saturn) should be **weak** (combust, retrograde, or in the fall)
7. **The benefics** — the benefics (Jupiter, Venus) should be **strong** (exalted, in the house of the planet, or in a friendly sign)

A **good election** is a **complex calculation**. The electional astrologer is the **specialist** who makes the calculation. The practitioner can use a **software tool** for the basic calculation; the practitioner should consult an **electional astrologer** for the complex cases.

## The Modern Use

A modern business practitioner can use the planetary hours in **three levels of sophistication**:

### Level 1: The Day-Planet Alignment

The simplest. The practitioner matches the day of the week to the planetary quality of the action. The practitioner **does not** calculate the planetary hours; the practitioner uses the **day** as the proxy.

- **Monday** for customer-facing actions
- **Tuesday** for launches and decisive action
- **Wednesday** for sales, contracts, communications
- **Thursday** for investments, hiring, legal
- **Friday** for partnerships, negotiations, beauty
- **Saturday** for structural decisions, real estate, banishing
- **Sunday** for authority, leadership, brand

The level-1 practice is **accessible** to anyone. The level-1 practice is **80% of the value** of the planetary hours system.

### Level 2: The Hour Alignment

The intermediate. The practitioner calculates the planetary hours (using the software) and matches the **hour** to the action. The practitioner **does not** consult an astrologer; the practitioner uses the **software** for the calculation.

The level-2 practice is **more accurate** than the level-1 practice. The level-2 practice requires the **software** (free or paid).

### Level 3: The Full Election

The advanced. The practitioner **consults an electional astrologer** for the complex actions (a major launch, a major contract, a major partnership). The level-3 practice is the **most accurate**; the level-3 practice is **expensive** and **slow**.

The level-3 practice is **reserved** for the **high-stakes** actions.

## The Modern Critique

The planetary hours system is **controversial**. The **skeptic** position:

- The planetary hours are a **cultural artifact** (the tradition of the seven-day week and the seven planets), not a **natural law**
- The **correlations** reported by the practitioners are **selection effects** (the practitioners remember the successes, forget the failures)
- The **scientific** evidence is **weak** or **absent**

The **defender** position:

- The planetary hours are the **operating system** of the **planetary talismanic** tradition (see [[05-Talismanic-Magick/02-Planetary-Talismans]]). The tradition has been **continuously practiced for 1,500 years**. The practitioners report **consistent** results
- The **scientific evidence** is **weak** because **no one has looked**. The tradition has been **ignored** by the modern academy
- The **empirical evidence** is **strong** in the **practitioner community**. The experienced practitioner can **feel** the difference between the hours
- The **psychological** mechanism is **plausible**: the practitioner who is **attuned** to the planetary hour is **more focused**, **more confident**, and **more aligned** with the action. The **placebo effect** (or the **expectation effect**) is a **real** effect, even if the planetary mechanism is not

The library's category 19 takes the **defender** position as the **working hypothesis**. The tradition has been **continuously practiced for 1,500 years**; the modern practitioner has the **right** to **test** the system empirically.

## The Practice

A modern practice of the planetary hours for business:

1. **Start at level 1** — match the day of the week to the action. The level-1 practice is **80% of the value**
2. **Move to level 2** — calculate the planetary hours with the software. The level-2 practice is **more accurate**
3. **Use level 3 for high-stakes** — consult an electional astrologer for the major actions
4. **Track the results** — keep a **log** of the actions, the planetary hour, the result. The log is the **empirical evidence**
5. **Adjust** — after 6-12 months, the practitioner has a **personal database** of the correlations
