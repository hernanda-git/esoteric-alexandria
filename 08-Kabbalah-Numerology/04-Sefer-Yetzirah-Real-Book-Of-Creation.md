# The Sefer Yetzirah — The Real Book of Creation

## The Real Document

The **Sefer** **Yetzirah** (**"**Book** **of** **Creation**"**, **"**Book** **of** **Formation**"**; **Hebrew** ***Sêfer*** ***Yěṣîrâh***) **is** **the** **earliest** **known** **Jewish** **mystical** **text**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **a** **specific** **Jewish** **mystical** **text**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **composed** **c.** **3rd**-**6th** **c.** **CE** **by** **an** **anonymous** **author**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **foundation** of **the** **Kabbalah** **and** **the** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.

The **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **a** **very** **short** **text** (**only** **about** **1,300** **words** **in** **the** **standard** **edition**); the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **most** **commented** **Jewish** **mystical** **text** (**more** **than** **100** **commentaries** **have** **been** **written**); the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **hermetic** **tradition**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.

The **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **a** **real** **Jewish** **mystical** **text**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Hebrew** **manuscripts**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **text**.

## The Real 6 Chapters

The **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **is** **divided** **into** **6** **chapters** (**in** **the** **longer** **recension**; the **shorter** **recension** **has** **1** **chapter** **of** **continuous** **text**; the **shorter** **recension** **is** **the** **older** **form**); the **6** **chapters** **are** **the** **specific** **chapters** of **the** **Sefer** **Yetzirah**.

### Chapter 1 — The 32 Paths

**Chapter** **1** **presents** **the** **32** **Paths** of **Wisdom**; the **32** **Paths** are **the** **specific** **paths** of **the** **Sefer** **Yetzirah**; the **32** **Paths** **are** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **creation**:

- **The** **10** **Sefirot** (**the** **specific** **divine** **emanations**; the **10** **Sefirot** **are** **the** **specific** **forms** of **the** **divine** **emanation**; the **10** **Sefirot** **are** **Keter**, **Chokhmah**, **Binah**, **Chesed**, **Gevurah**, **Tiferet**, **Netzach**, **Hod**, **Yesod**, **Malkhut**)
- **The** **22** **Letters** (**the** **specific** **Hebrew** **letters**; the **22** **Letters** **are** **the** **specific** **forms** of **the** **divine** **speech**; the **22** **Letters** **are** **the** **22** **Hebrew** **letters** of **the** **alphabet**)

The **32** **Paths** = **10** **Sefirot** + **22** **Letters**; the **32** **Paths** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **tradition**.

### Chapter 2 — The 10 Sefirot and the 22 Letters

**Chapter** **2** **presents** **the** **10** **Sefirot** and **the** **22** **Letters**; the **10** **Sefirot** **are** **presented** **as** **the** **specific** **divine** **emanations**; the **22** **Letters** **are** **presented** **as** **the** **specific** **divine** **speech**; the **Sefer** **Yetzirah** **presents** **the** **specific** **correspondences** of **the** **10** **Sefirot**:

- **Keter** (the **Crown**; the **first** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **supreme** **divine** **will**)
- **Chokhmah** (the **Wisdom**; the **second** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **wisdom**)
- **Binah** (the **Understanding**; the **third** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **understanding**)
- **Chesed** (the **Mercy**; the **fourth** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **mercy**)
- **Gevurah** (the **Severity**; the **fifth** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **severity**)

- **Netzach** (the **Victory**; the **seventh** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **victory**)

- **Yesod** (the **Foundation**; the **ninth** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **foundation**)
- **Malkhut** (the **Kingdom**; the **tenth** **Sefirah**; the **specific** **Sefirah** of **the** **divine** **kingdom**)

The **10** **Sefirot** **are** **the** **specific** **divine** **emanations** of **the** **Sefer** **Yetzirah**; the **10** **Sefirot** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Kabbalah** **tradition**.
