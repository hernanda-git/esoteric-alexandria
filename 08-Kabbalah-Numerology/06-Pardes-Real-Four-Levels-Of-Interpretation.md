# Pardes — The Real Four Levels of Interpretation

## The Real Document

The **Pardes** (**Hebrew** ***PRDS***, **"**Orchard**"**; **a** **specific** **acronym**) **is** **the** **4**-**level** **system** of **interpretation** of **the** **Jewish** **hermeneutical** **tradition**; the **Pardes** **is** **a** **specific** **hermeneutical** **doctrine**; the **Pardes** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **hermeneutical** **tradition**; the **Pardes** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Kabbalistic** **hermeneutics**.

The **Pardes** **is** **a** **specific** **acronym**; the **Pardes** **is** **formed** **from** **the** **first** **letters** of **the** **4** **Hebrew** **words** for **the** **4** **levels**:

- **P** = **Peshat** (**פ** = **P**)
- **R** = **Remez** (**ר** = **R**)
- **D** = **Derash** (**ד** = **D**)
- **S** = **Sod** (**ס** = **S**)

The **Pardes** **is** **the** **acronym** of **the** **4** **levels**; the **Pardes** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **hermeneutical** **tradition**; the **Pardes** **is** **a** **real** **Jewish** **hermeneutical** **tradition**.

## The Real 4 Levels

The **4** **levels** of **the** **Pardes** **are** **the** **specific** **levels** of **the** **Jewish** **hermeneutical** **tradition**; the **4** **levels** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **interpretation** of **the** **Torah**; the **4** **levels** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**.

### 1. Peshat (the Plain / the Simple)

The **Peshat** (**"**the** **plain**"**, **"**the** **simple**"**; **Hebrew** ***pěšāṭ***) **is** **the** **first** **level** of **interpretation**; the **Peshat** **is** **the** **plain** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Peshat** **is** **the** **literal** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Peshat** **is** **the** **contextual** **meaning** of **the** **text**.

The **Peshat** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Rashi** **commentary** (**the** **11th**-**c.** **Rashi** **commentary**; the **Rashi** **commentary** **is** **the** **standard** **Peshat** **commentary** of **the** **Torah**; the **Rashi** **commentary** **is** **the** **most** **widely** **studied** **Peshat** **commentary**).

The **Peshat** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **focuses** on **the** **plain** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Peshat** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **used** **in** **the** **halakhic** (**legal**) **decisions**; the **Peshat** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **masoretic** **tradition**.

### 2. Remez (the Hint / the Allusion)

The **Remez** (**"**the** **hint**"**, **"**the** **allusion**"**; **Hebrew** ***rēmēz***) **is** **the** **second** **level** of **interpretation**; the **Remez** **is** **the** **allusive** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Remez** **is** **the** **typological** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Remez** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **looks** **for** **the** **allusions** **in** **the** **text**.

The **Remez** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **focuses** on **the** **typological** **and** **allusive** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Remez** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **used** in **the** **early** **Christian** **interpretation** of **the** **Old** **Testament**; the **Remez** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Christian** **typological** **tradition**.

The **Remez** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **looks** **for** **the** **hidden** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Remez** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **looks** **for** **the** **typological** **meaning** of **the** **text**.

### 3. Derash (the Interpretation / the Homiletic)

The **Derash** (**"**the** **interpretation**"**, **"**the** **homiletic**"**; **Hebrew** ***dĕraš***) **is** **the** **third** **level** of **interpretation**; the **Derash** **is** **the** **homiletical** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Derash** **is** **the** **midrashic** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Derash** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **looks** **for** **the** **homiletical** **meaning** of **the** **text**.

The **Derash** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Midrash** (**the** **rabbinic** **commentary**; the **Midrash** **is** **the** **standard** **Derash** **commentary** of **the** **Torah**; the **Midrash** **is** **the** **most** **widely** **studied** **Derash** **commentary**); the **Derash** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Talmud** (**the** **Talmud** **is** **the** **standard** **Derash** **commentary** of **the** **Mishnah**).

The **Derash** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **focuses** on **the** **homiletical** **and** **midrashic** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Derash** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **used** in **the** **halakhic** **and** **aggadic** **midrashim**; the **Derash** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **midrashic** **tradition**.

### 4. Sod (the Secret / the Mystical)

The **Sod** (**"**the** **secret**"**, **"**the** **mystical**"**; **Hebrew** ***sôd***) **is** **the** **fourth** **level** of **interpretation**; the **Sod** **is** **the** **mystical** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Sod** **is** **the** **kabbalistic** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Sod** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **looks** **for** **the** **mystical** **meaning** of **the** **text**.

The **Sod** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Kabbalah** (**the** **Kabbalah** **is** **the** **standard** **Sod** **commentary** of **the** **Torah**; the **Kabbalah** **is** **the** **most** **widely** **studied** **Sod** **commentary**); the **Sod** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Zohar** (**the** **Zohar** **is** **the** **standard** **Sod** **commentary** of **the** **Torah**).

The **Sod** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **focuses** on **the** **mystical** **and** **kabbalistic** **meaning** of **the** **text**; the **Sod** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Kabbalah**; the **Sod** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **interpretation** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.
