# The Four Worlds — The Real Kabbalistic Ontology

## The Real Document

The **4** **Worlds** (**Hebrew** ***Olamot***, **"**the** **Worlds**"**) **are** **the** **specific** **4** **levels** of **divine** **manifestation** in **the** **Kabbalistic** **tradition**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **emanation** in **the** **Zohar**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **a** **real** **Kabbalistic** **doctrine**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **preserved** in **the** **Zohar** **and** **the** **Lurianic** **Kabbalah**.

The **4** **Worlds** **are** **a** **specific** **doctrine** **of** **the** **13th**-**c.** **Gerona** **school** **and** **the** **Zohar**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **systematized** in **the** **16th**-**c.** **Cordovero** **and** **Luria**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Lurianic** **cosmology**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **the** **most** **important** **doctrine** of **the** **Kabbalistic** **cosmology**.

The **4** **Worlds** **are** **a** **real** **Kabbalistic** **doctrine**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **preserved** in **real** **Zohar** **manuscripts**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **a** **real** **historical** **doctrine**.

## The Real 4 Worlds

The **4** **Worlds** **are** **the** **specific** **4** **levels** of **the** **divine** **manifestation**; the **4** **Worlds** **are** **from** **highest** **to** **lowest**:

### 1. Atzilut (the World of Emanation)

The **Atzilut** (**the** **specific** **world**; the **world** **is** **the** **World** of **Emanation**; the **world** **is** **the** **highest** of **the** **4** **Worlds**; the **world** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **emanation**; the **world** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **En** **Sof** (**the** **Infinite**) **revealed** **as** **the** **Sefirot**); the **Atzilut** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **intimacy**; the **Atzilut** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **self**-**revelation**; the **Atzilut** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **Ein** **Sof** (**the** **divine** **infinity**); the **Atzilut** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **10** **Sefirot** **in** **their** **divine** **aspect**.

The **Atzilut** **is** **the** **most** **subtle** of **the** **4** **Worlds**; the **Atzilut** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **closest** to **the** **En** **Sof**; the **Atzilut** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **most** **abstract**.

### 2. Beriah (the World of Creation)

The **Beriah** (**the** **specific** **world**; the **world** **is** **the** **World** of **Creation**; the **world** **is** **the** **second** of **the** **4** **Worlds**; the **world** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **creation**; the **world** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **revealed** **as** **the** **first** **level** of **created** **beings**); the **Beriah** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **throne** (**the** **merkavah**); the **Beriah** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **angelic** **orders**; the **Beriah** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **highest** **angelic** **beings**.

The **Beriah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **first** **level** of **creation**; the **Beriah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **angelic** **tradition**.

### 3. Yetzirah (the World of Formation)

The **Yetzirah** (**the** **specific** **world**; the **world** **is** **the** **World** of **Formation**; the **world** **is** **the** **third** of **the** **4** **Worlds**; the **world** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **formation**; the **world** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **revealed** **as** **the** **second** **level** of **created** **beings**); the **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **angels**; the **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **angelic** **ministering** **angels**; the **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **angels** **of** **the** **planets** **and** **the** **zodiac**.

The **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **second** **level** of **creation**; the **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **planetary** **and** **zodiacal** **tradition**; the **Yetzirah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **magical** **tradition**.

### 4. Assiah (the World of Action)

The **Assiah** (**the** **specific** **world**; the **world** **is** **the** **World** of **Action**; the **world** **is** **the** **lowest** of **the** **4** **Worlds**; the **world** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** **action**; the **world** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **revealed** **as** **the** **physical** **universe**); the **Assiah** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **physical** **universe**; the **Assiah** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **physical** **bodies**; the **Assiah** **is** **the** **specific** **world** of **the** **material** **creation**.

The **Assiah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **physical** **universe**; the **Assiah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **grossest** of **the** **4** **Worlds**; the **Assiah** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **divine** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **physical** **practice**.
