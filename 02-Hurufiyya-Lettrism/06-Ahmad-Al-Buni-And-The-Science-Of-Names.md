# Ahmad al-Buni and the Science of the Divine Names

## The Man

**Ahmad al-Buni** (c. 1184-1225 CE) is the **magnum** **textual** **factory** of the **Western** Islamic **science** of **letters**. Born in **Buna** (the **Rif** of Morocco), educated as a **Maliki** jurist, initiated in the **Sufi** **method** of *dhikr* and *khalwa*, he died in **Alexandria**, where his **tomb** (*maqam al-Buni*) remains a **site** of **ziyara** — pilgrimage for both **Sunnis** and **Sufis** of the **Shadhili** line, although the exact connection to **al-Shadhili** (d. 1258) is **chronologically** **anachronistic** if taken as personal discipleship.

What makes al-Buni **decisive** is not **novelty**. The *'ilm al-huruf* and *'ilm al-asma'* **pre-exist** him across **Maghribi**, **Andalusi**, and **Egyptian** esoteric circles. His achievement is **systematization**: he assembled **scattered** materials — **Abjad** **numerals**, **magic** **squares**, **divine** **Names**, **planetary** **assignments**, **astrological** **timing**, **theurgy** — into a **single** **reference** **work** that became the **operating** **system** for every later **practitioner** in the **Arabic**-**speaking** **world** and the **gateway** through which the **Ottoman**, **Mughal**, and **Sub-Saharan** **West African** **magico-religious** **practices** inherited the **Babylonian-Abrahamic** **letter** **tradition**.

## The Corpus

The **Shams al-Ma'arif** (The Sun of Knowledge) is the **master** **handbook**. Written sometime in the early **1200s** in Egypt, it is organized in **chapters** (*fasls*) that move alphabetically through the **Arabic** **letters**, then the **divine** **Names**, then the **planetary** **hours**, then the **practical** **operations** -- **healing**, **love**, **victory**, **knowledge**, **protection**, **disappearance**.

Two companion texts shape the reading:

- **Latā'if al-Isharat bi-Funūn al-Jafr wal-Ḥurūf** -- the *jafr* science, which reads **future** **events** and **hidden** **revelations** from the **numerical** **structure** of the **Quran** and the **Arabic** **Name** of **God**.
- **Umdat al-Talib fi al-Nujum** -- planetary magic; connects the *huruf* to **planetary** **decan** **structures** and **talismanic** **timing**.

## The Doctrine of Names

The science treats the **99** *Asma' al-Husna* as **operators**, not merely **epithets**. Each Name:

- Has a specific **letter-structure** in Arabic script.
- Has a specific **Abjad** numerical value (see [[02-Abjad-Numerals]]).
- Has a **planet** or **heavenly** body associated by **resonance** with the total value.
- Has a **specific condition** of **unlocking** -- a **mystical** state, a **ritual** ablution, a **direction** of **facing**, a **number** of **repetitions** (*tahlil*).

The **Ism al-A'zam** (الاسم  
الأعظم, "the **Greatest** **Name**") is distinct from all lesser names. It is a **function**, not a fixed word: its exact configuration depends on **mathematical** **structure**, **astrological** **moment**, and the **operator's** **state** of **purification**. The search for the Greatest Name is the **thread** that unites the Islamic *'ilm al-asma* with the **Hebrew** **Kabbalah** of the **Ineffable** **Name** (see [[08-Kabbalah-Numerology/01-Sefer-Yetzirah-Real-Book-Of-Creation]]) and therefore with the **Christian** **magical** **name-repertoire** that Agrippa later **standardized** in the **West**.

## The Procedure

A typical *'ilm al-Asma* operation follows the chain below, which is the same as the chain in **04-Wafq** and **Planetary-Hour** timing. The **internal** step is the most important:

1. **Purify** (*tahara*) -- ritual purity, preceded by a **mental** **quarantine** in which the operator sets aside **non-task** **desires** and **personality** **grudges**.
2. **Recite** the **Name** the assigned number of times, **sql** **facing** **qibla**, with the fingertip on the heart or on a **written** **tablet** prepared in advance.
3. **Concentrate** on the **letter** **forms** while reciting; the **form** is the **message**, not the sound. Al-Buni explicitly instructs the practitioner to **visualize** the **superscripts** and **subscripts** of the Arabic letters as **celestial** **stations**.
4. **Receive** the ***baraka*** -- the **spiritual** **charge** -- as a **felt** **state** (*ihsas*), not a **verbal** **answer**.
5. **Seal** with a **request** that fits the **Name's** **domain**: healing, protection, success, love, knowledge, or **destruction** of an enemy. The **same** **Name** can be used in **dark** and **light** modes; al-Buni neither **apologizes** for nor **conceals** this.

## The Jafr Connection

The **jafr** system is the predictive **cousin** of the *'ilm al-asma*. It uses the **numerical** **value** of the **Quranic** **text**, the **names** of **people**, **places**, and **events**, to derive **calendaric** **and** **prophetic** **timelines**. The operator **starts** from a **base text**, **converts** letters to values, applies **modular** **arithmetic**, then reads the **result** against the **table** of **significators** -- planets, materials, diseases, weather states, wars.

The **jafr** is the **historical** source of the later **Ottoman** ***Fal* **prediction** manuals and of the **South Asian** **Ilm-e-Qalam** tradition. It is also the direct ancestor of the modern **cryptographic** and **steganographic** use of **Arabic** **letter** **values** in **political** **texts** of the **20th** **century**.

## Cross-Tradition Lineage

The **Abrahamic** **three** -- **Hebrew**, **Arabic**, **Syriac** -- share the **operating** **principle** that the **alphabet** is **more** than **a** **tool** **for** **writing**: it is the **substrate** of **creation** itself.

| Tradition | Root text | Letter count | Central operator |
|---|---|---|---|
| Kabbalah | Sefer Yetzirah | 22 Hebrew letters | 10 sefirot + 22 paths |
| Islam | Quran (Meccan surahs) + *Shams al-Ma'arif* | 28 Arabic letters + diacritics | 99 Names / Ism al-A'zam |
| Syriac | Cave of Treasures / Hermetic influence | 22 Syriac letters | The *memra* as Word |

The **continuity** is not **borrowing** but **preservation** -- each tradition **assumes** that the **letters** are **older** than the **world** and **model** the **world**. The **greatest** **Name** is **hidden** because the **world** is **hidden**; to **know** the Name is to **see** the **world** in **its** **principle**.

## Critical Note

The **science** of the **Names** is **real** as a **historical** **practice** -- it produced **libraries** of **manuscripts**, **institutional** **lineages** of **authorization**, and **observable** **social** **effects** -- but it is **not** **scientifically** **validated** under the **modern** **scientific** **method**. Its **efficacy** is a **matter** of **faith**, **experience**, **cultural** **expectation**, and **social** **reinforcement** -- not controlled experiment. This is the same **operational** **status** as the **psychotherapy** **before** **RCTs**.
