# The Emerald Tablet of Hermes — The Real Source

## The Real Document

The **Emerald** **Tablet** (**Latin** ***Tabula*** **Smaragdina***, **"**the** **emerald** **tablet**"**; **Arabic** ***al-Lawh*** ***al-Zumurrud***; **the** **specific** **text** of **the** **Western** **alchemical** **tradition**) **is** **the** **most** **famous** **and** **most** **quoted** **single** **text** of **Western** **alchemy**; the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **a** **specific** **Hermetic** **text**; the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **the** **foundational** **text** of **the** **Western** **alchemical** **tradition**; the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **a** **real** **Western** **alchemical** **text**; the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Arabic** **and** **Latin** **manuscripts**.

The **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **attributed** **to** **Hermes** **Trismegistus** (**the** **legendary** **"**Hermes** **the** **Thrice**-**Greatest**"**; the **Hermetic** **tradition** **attributes** **the** **Emerald** **Tablet** to **Hermes**); the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **preserved** **in** **an** **Arabic** **version** (**the** **Arabic** **version** **is** **attributed** to **Balinas**/**Apollonius** of **Tyana**; the **Arabic** **version** **is** **the** **older** **form**); the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **preserved** **in** **a** **Latin** **version** (**the** **Latin** **version** **is** **the** **translation** by **Johannes** **Hispalensis** c. **1140** **CE**; the **Latin** **version** **is** **the** **standard** **Western** **alchemical** **text**).

The **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **a** **real** **Hermetic** **text**; the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Arabic** **and** **Latin** **manuscripts**; the **Emerald** **Tablet** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **text**.

## The Real 13 Sentences of the Latin Text

The **Latin** **Emerald** **Tablet** **consists** of **13** **sentences**; the **13** **sentences** **are** **the** **specific** **sentences** of **the** **Latin** **Emerald** **Tablet**; the **13** **sentences** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **alchemical** **tradition**.

The **sentence** **states** that **the** **truth** is **true** without **falsehood**; the **sentence** **states** that **the** **truth** is **certain**; the **sentence** **states** that **the** **truth** is **the** **most** **true**.

### Sentence 2 — That Which is Below is Like That Which is Above

**"**Quod** **est** **inferius** **est** **sicut** **quod** **est** **superius**, **et** **quod** **est** **superius** **est** **sicut** **quod** **est** **inferius**, **ad** **perpetrandum** **miraculum** **unius** **rei**.**"** (**"**That** **which** **is** **below** **is** **like** **that** **which** **is** **above**, **and** **that** **which** **is** **above** **is** **like** **that** **which** **is** **below**, **for** **accomplishing** **the** **miracle** **of** **one** **thing**.**"**)

The **sentence** **states** the **fundamental** **Hermetic** **doctrine** of **correspondence** (**the** **doctrine** of **as** **above**, **so** **below**); the **sentence** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.

The **sentence** **states** the **doctrine** of **emanation**; the **sentence** **states** that **all** **things** **come** **from** **one**; the **sentence** **states** that **all** **things** **were** **born** **from** **this** **one** **thing**.

### Sentence 4 — The Sun is its Father, the Moon its Mother

**"**Pater** **eius** **est** **Sol**, **mater** **eius** **est** **Luna**, **portavit** **illud** **ventus** **in** **ventre** **suo**, **nutrix** **eius** **terra** **est**.**"** (**"**Its** **father** **is** **the** **Sun**, **its** **mother** **is** **the** **Moon**, **the** **wind** **has** **carried** **it** **in** **its** **belly**, **its** **nurse** **is** **the** **earth**.**"**)
