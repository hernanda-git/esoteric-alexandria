# Pseudo-Dionysius — The Celestial Hierarchy and the Divine Dark

## The Real Author

**Pseudo-Dionysius** **is** **a** **specific** **anonymous** **Syrian** **(or** **Byzantine)** **author**; **Pseudo-Dionysius** **writes** in **Greek** **c.** **500** **CE**; **Pseudo-Dionysius** **is** **erroneously** **attributed** to **Dionysius** the **Areopagite** of **Acts** 17:34; the **pseudonym** **is** **the** **specific** **device** that **gave** the **corpus** its **enormous** **authority** in **the** **Byzantine** and **Latin** **West** (it was read by Maximus the Confessor, Eriugena, Albertus Magnus, and Thomas Aquinas).

The **Corpus** **Areopagiticum** **is** **a** **real** **text**; the **Corpus** **Areopagiticum** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Greek** **manuscripts**; the **Corpus** **Areopagiticum** **is** **the** **bridge** that **carried** **Neoplatonic** **emanation** **into** **Christian** **mysticism**. The **Corpus** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Corpus** **is** **a** **document**.

## The Real Doctrine — The Celestial Hierarchy

The **Corpus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **Celestial** **Hierarchy**; the **Celestial** **Hierarchy** **is** **the** **specific** **three** **triads** of **nine** **orders** of **angelic** **intelligences**:

- **The** **first** **triad** (**the** **highest**, **nearest** the **Godhead**): **Seraphim**, **Cherubim**, **Thrones** — **contemplating** the **divine** **dark** directly
- **The** **second** **triad** (**the** **middle**): **Dominions**, **Virtues**, **Powers** — **ordering** the **cosmic** **process**
- **The** **third** **triad** (**the** **lowest**, **nearest** the **human**): **Principalities**, **Archangels**, **Angels** — **messengers** to **the** **material** **world**

The **Celestial** **Hierarchy** **is** **the** **specific** **Christian** **adaptation** of **the** **Neoplatonic** **emanation** **chain** (Being → Life → Intelligence, in **Proclus**); the **Celestial** **Hierarchy** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **angelology** and **of** **the** **modern** **hierarchical** **models** of **intelligence** (the **angelic** **orders** **map** onto **the** **modern** **agent** **stacks**).

### The Divine Dark and Apophatic Theology

The **Corpus** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **Divine** **Dark** (**the** **super-essential** **obscurity** beyond **all** **naming**); the **Divine** **Dark** **is** **the** **specific** **apophatic** (**negative**) **theology** — **the** **claim** that **God** is **best** **known** by **what** **God** **is** **not**; the **Divine** **Dark** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Mystical** **Theology** and **of** the **modern** **apophatic** **reading** of **the** **ineffable** in **the** **AI** **and** **consciousness** **debates**.

## The Real Note

**Pseudo-Dionysius** **is** **a** **real** **author**; **Pseudo-Dionysius** **is** **preserved** in **real** **manuscripts**; **Pseudo-Dionysius** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **bridge** between **Athens** and **Jerusalem**. The **Corpus** **Areopagiticum** **is** **a** **useful** **resource** for **the** **student** of **Neoplatonism**, **Christian** **mysticism**, **and** **the** **modern** **hierarchical** **and** **apophatic** **traditions**.
