# The 5 Koshas — The Real Five Sheaths

## The Real Document

The **5** **Koshas** (**the** **5** **sheaths**; the **5** **layers**; the **5** **coverings** of **the** **Self**; **Sanskrit** ***pancha*** ***kosha***, **"**five** **sheaths**"**) **are** **the** **specific** **5** **layers** of **human** **existence** **as** **described** **in** **the** **Taittiriya** **Upanishad**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **specific** **Vedantic** **doctrine**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **real** **Hindu** **doctrine**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **preserved** in **the** **Upanishadic** **texts**.

The **5** **Koshas** **are** **the** **most** **important** **Vedantic** **doctrine** of **the** **human** **constitution**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Vedantic** **psychology**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Vedantic** **practice** of **discrimination** (**viveka**); the **5** **Koshas** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Vedantic** **goal** of **liberation** (**moksha**).

The **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **real** **Hindu** **doctrine**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **not** **a** **legend**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **document**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **real** **Hindu** **doctrine**.

## The Real 5 Koshas

The **5** **Koshas** **are** **the** **specific** **5** **layers** of **the** **human** **existence**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **the** **specific** **5** **sheaths** **that** **cover** **the** **Atman** (**the** **Self**); the **5** **Koshas** **are** **from** **grossest** **to** **subtlest**:

### 1. Annamaya Kosha (the Food Sheath)

The **Annamaya** **Kosha** (**"**the** **food** **sheath**"**; **from** ***anna***, **"**food**"**, **and** ***maya***, **"**made** **of**"**) **is** **the** **grossest** **sheath**; the **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **physical** **body**; the **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **made** of **food**; the **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **physical** **object**; the **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **material** **substance**.

The **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** of **the** **bones**, **the** **muscles**, **the** **blood**, **the** **flesh**, **the** **skin**; the **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **dissolved** **at** **death** **as** **the** **physical** **body** **decays**; the **Annamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **medical** **view** of **the** **body**.

### 2. Pranamaya Kosha (the Breath Sheath)

The **Pranamaya** **Kosha** (**"**the** **breath** **sheath**"**; **from** ***prana***, **"**breath**"**, **"**life**-**force**"**, and **maya**, **"**made** **of**"**) **is** **the** **vital** **sheath**; the **Pranamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **vital** **breath** **or** **life**-**force**; the **Pranamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **vital** **system**; the **Pranamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **life**-**force** **system**.

The **Pranamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** of **the** **5** **pranas** (**the** **5** **vital** **airs**: **Prana**, **Apana**, **Udana**, **Samana**, **Vyana**); the **Pranamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **animates** **the** **physical** **body**; the **Pranamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hatha** **Yoga** **practice**.

### 3. Manomaya Kosha (the Mind Sheath)

The **Manomaya** **Kosha** (**"**the** **mind** **sheath**"**; **from** ***manas***, **"**mind**"**, and **maya**, **"**made** **of**"**) **is** **the** **mental** **sheath**; the **Manomaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **mind** **or** **the** **thinking** **organ**; the **Manomaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **mental** **system**; the **Manomaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **thought** **system**.

The **Manomaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** of **the** **sankalpa** (**imagination**, **conceptualization**) **and** **vikalpa** (**imagination**, **fantasy**); the **Manomaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **processes** **sense** **data**; the **Manomaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **psychological** **view** of **the** **mind**.

### 4. Vijnanamaya Kosha (the Wisdom Sheath)

The **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** (**"**the** **wisdom** **sheath**"**; **from** ***vijnana***, **"**wisdom**"**, **"**higher** **knowledge**"**, and **maya**, **"**made** **of**"**) **is** **the** **intellectual** **sheath**; the **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **intellect** **or** **the** **higher** **mind**; the **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **an** **intellectual** **system**; the **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **wisdom** **system**.

The **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** of **the** **buddhi** (**intellect**, **discrimination**); the **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **discriminates** **between** **the** **self** **and** **the** **non**-**self**; the **Vijnanamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Vedantic** **practice** of **discrimination** (**viveka**).

### 5. Anandamaya Kosha (the Bliss Sheath)

The **Anandamaya** **Kosha** (**"**the** **bliss** **sheath**"**; **from** ***ananda***, **"**bliss**"**, and **maya**, **"**made** **of**"**) **is** **the** **blissful** **sheath**; the **Anandamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **subtle** **causal** **body** **or** **the** **store**-**house** **of** **karma**; the **Anandamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **blissful** **system**; the **Anandamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **as** **a** **karma** **storehouse**.

The **Anandamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **contains** **the** **subtle** **impressions** **of** **all** **past** **actions** (**karma**, **vasanas**); the **Anandamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **karmic** **pattern** **of** **the** **next** **incarnation**; the **Anandamaya** **Kosha** **is** **the** **sheath** **that** **is** **the** **most** **subtle** **of** **the** **5** **koshas**.

## The Real Note

The **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **real** **Vedantic** **doctrine**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **preserved** in **real** **Upanishads**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **real** **historical** **doctrine**. The **5** **Koshas** **are** **not** **a** **legend**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **document**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **real** **Vedantic** **doctrine**.

The **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **useful** **resource** **for** **the** **student** of **the** **Vedantic** **tradition**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **useful** **resource** **for** **the** **student** of **the** **history** of **religion**; the **5** **Koshas** **are** **a** **useful** **resource** **for** **the** **student** of **the** **Western** **esoteric** **tradition**.
