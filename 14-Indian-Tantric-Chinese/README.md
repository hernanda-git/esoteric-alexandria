## Deep-Dive Paths

- **Subtle-body layer** — 5 koshas cover the Patanjali-equivalent phenomenology; seven chakras are the later tantric visualization; the gap between them is where hatha yoga operates.
- **Alchemical layer** — neidan as Chinese parallel to Western magnification; waidan as external cinnabar practice shows China had its own outer-alchemy moment.
- **Practice layer** — Vijnana Bhairava 112 practices are the densest practical manual of awareness in the canon; study alongside *Tantra* docs for comparison.
- **Philosophical layer** — left-hand vs right-hand paths are not "white/black"; Vamachara is methodologically rigorous.
