# Vijnana Bhairava Tantra — The Real Source of 112 Meditations

## The Real Document

The **Vijnana** **Bhairava** **Tantra** (**"**the** **Tantra** **of** **the** **Direct** **Awareness** **of** **Bhairava**"**, **VBT**) **is** **a** **Tantric** **text** **of** **the** **Kashmir** **Shaivism** **tradition**; the **VBT** **is** **composed** **c.** **800**-**1000** **CE**; the **VBT** **is** **a** **specific** **Hindu** **text**; the **VBT** **is** **the** **most** **important** **single** **text** of **the** **Tantric** **meditation** **tradition**; the **VBT** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Sanskrit** **manuscripts**.

The **VBT** **is** **a** **dialogue** **between** **Bhairava** (**Shiva**) **and** **Bhairavi** (**Shakti**, **Devi**); the **VBT** **presents** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **direct** **awareness** (**pratyabhijna**); the **VBT** **presents** **the** **specific** **112** **dharanas** (**methods** **of** **meditation**); the **VBT** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Kashmir** **Shaivism** **tradition**.

The **VBT** **is** **a** **real** **Hindu** **text**; the **VBT** **is** **preserved** in **real** **Sanskrit** **manuscripts**; the **VBT** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **text**.

## The Real 112 Dharanas

The **VBT** **presents** **the** **specific** **112** **dharanas** (**methods** **of** **meditation**; the **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **methods** of **the** **Tantric** **meditation** **tradition**; the **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **meditation** **methods** of **the** **Kashmir** **Shaivism** **tradition**).

The **112** **dharanas** **include**:

### The Breath Dharanas (Dharana 1-22)

The **breath** **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **breath** **methods** of **the** **VBT**; the **breath** **dharanas** **include**:

- **Suspended** **breath** **meditation** (the **specific** **meditation** on **the** **suspension** of **the** **breath**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **kumbhaka** **practice**)
- **Shambhavi** **Mudra** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **eyes** **centered** **at** **the** **brow**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **Shambhavi** **Mudra** **practice**)
- **Unmanā** **meditation** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **mind** **suspended**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **unmanā** **practice**)
- **Khecari** **Mudra** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **tongue** **in** **the** **nasopharynx**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **khecari** **practice**)

### The Body Dharanas (Dharana 23-50)

The **body** **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **body** **methods** of **the** **VBT**; the **body** **dharanas** **include**:

- **Viparita** **dharana** (the **inverted** **meditation**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **inverted** **practice**)
- **Vyāpti** **dharana** (the **pervasion** **meditation**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **pervasion** **practice**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **tip** **of** **the** **nose** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **tip** of **the** **nose**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **middle** **of** **the** **brow** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **middle** of **the** **brow**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **palate** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **palate**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **heart** **lotus** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **heart** **lotus**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **navel** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **navel**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **Muladhara** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **Muladhara**)
- **Dharana** **at** **the** **Sahasrara** (the **specific** **meditation** **with** **the** **attention** **on** **the** **Sahasrara**)

### The Sense Dharanas (Dharana 51-70)

The **sense** **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **sense** **methods** of **the** **VBT**; the **sense** **dharanas** **include**:

- **Dharana** **on** **the** **intermediate** **space** **of** **the** **senses** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **intermediate** **space** **between** **two** **sense** **objects**)
- **Dharana** **on** **the** **first** **sound** (**nada**) (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **first** **sound**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **nada** **practice**)
- **Dharana** **on** **the** **inner** **sound** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **inner** **sound**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **nada**-**anusandhana** **practice**)
- **Dharana** **on** **the** **sounds** **of** **the** **body** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **sounds** **of** **the** **body**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **body** **sounds** **practice**)

### The Mental Dharanas (Dharana 71-90)

The **mental** **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **mental** **methods** of **the** **VBT**; the **mental** **dharanas** **include**:

- **Dharana** **on** **the** **void** (**shunya**) (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **void**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **void** **meditation**)
- **Dharana** **on** **the** **emptiness** **of** **the** **mind** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **emptiness** of **the** **mind**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **mind** **emptiness** **meditation**)

- **Dharana** **on** **the** **supreme** **identity** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **supreme** **identity**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **aham** **meditation**)

The **cosmic** **dharanas** **are** **the** **specific** **cosmic** **methods** of **the** **VBT**; the **cosmic** **dharanas** **include**:

- **Dharana** **on** **the** **fire** **of** **consciousness** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **fire** of **consciousness**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **fire** **meditation**)
- **Dharana** **on** **the** **moon** (the **specific** **meditation** **on** **the** **moon**; the **meditation** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **moon** **meditation**)
