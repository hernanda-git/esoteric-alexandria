# Zoroastrian Amesha Spentas — The Immortal Holy Ones

## The Real Document

The **Amesha** **Spentas** (**Aməša** **Spənta**, **"**Immortal** **Holy** **Ones**"**, **singular**: **Amesha** **Spenta**) **are** **the** **seven** **divine** **beings** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **specific** **guardians** of **the** **seven** **divine** **creations** of **Ahura** **Mazda** (the **Wise** **Lord**); the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **angelology**.

The **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **preserved** in **the** **Avestan** **texts** (**the** **Yasna**, **the** **Yasht**, **the** **Vendidad**); the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **a** **real** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **a** **document**.

The **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **monotheistic** **angelology** in **the** **world**; the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **monotheistic** **tradition** of **the** **angels**; the **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **monotheistic** **tradition** of **the** **guardian** **beings**.

## The Real Origin — The Gathas of Zoroaster

The **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **introduced** in **the** **Gathas** of **Zoroaster** (**the** **17** **hymns** of **Zoroaster**; **the** **Gathas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **part** of **the** **Avesta**; **the** **Gathas** **are** **preserved** in **the** **Yasna** **chapters** **28**-**34**; **the** **Gathas** **are** **dated** **c.** **6th**-**4th** **c.** **BCE** **by** **modern** **critical** **scholarship** (**c.** **1500**-**1200** **BCE** **by** **traditional** **Zoroastrian** **dating**)).

The **Gathas** **introduce**:

- **Ahura** **Mazda** (**the** **Wise** **Lord**; **the** **supreme** **deity** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; **the** **specific** **divine** **being** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **monotheism**; **Ahura** **Mazda** **is** **the** **creator** of **all** **things**)
- **Spenta** **Mainyu** (**the** **Holy** **Spirit**; **the** **beneficent** **spirit**; **the** **Holy** **Spirit** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; **Spenta** **Mainyu** **is** **the** **specific** **spirit** of **the** **good**; **Spenta** **Mainyu** **is** **the** **opposite** of **Angra** **Mainyu**)
- **Angra** **Mainyu** (**the** **Destructive** **Spirit**; **the** **evil** **spirit**; **the** **opponent** of **Ahura** **Mazda**; **Angra** **Mainyu** **is** **the** **specific** **spirit** of **the** **evil**; **Angra** **Mainyu** **is** **the** **opposite** of **Spenta** **Mainyu**)
- **The** **Amesha** **Spentas** (**the** **Immortal** **Holy** **Ones**; **the** **specific** **divine** **beings** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; **the** **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **introduced** in **the** **Gathas**)

The **Gathas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **monotheistic** **texts** in **the** **world**; the **Gathas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **monotheistic** **tradition** of **the** **angels**; the **Gathas** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **monotheistic** **tradition** of **the** **creator** **deity**.

## The Real Seven Amesha Spentas

The **7** **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **specific** **divine** **beings** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; the **7** **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **specific** **guardians** of **the** **7** **divine** **creations** of **Ahura** **Mazda**; the **7** **Amesha** **Spentas** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **angelology**.

**Ahura** **Mazda** **guards** **the** **good** **thought** (**Vohu** **Manah**); the **Avestan** **name** **is** **Ahura** **Mazda** (**Avestan** **script**); the **Pahlavi** **name** **is** **Ohrmazd**; the **Persian** **name** **is** **Ormuzd**.

**Vohu** **Manah** (**the** **Good** **Mind**; **the** **Good** **Thought**; **the** **specific** **divine** **being** of **the** **good** **mind**; **Vohu** **Manah** **is** **the** **guardian** of **the** **good** **thought**; **Vohu** **Manah** **is** **the** **specific** **guardian** of **the** **animals**).

**Asha** **Vahishta** (**the** **Best** **Righteousness**; **the** **Best** **Truth**; **the** **specific** **divine** **being** of **the** **truth**; **Asha** **Vahishta** **is** **the** **guardian** of **the** **fire**).

**Asha** **Vahishta** **guards** **the** **fire** (**the** **good** **creation** of **the** **fire**; the **fire** **is** **the** **symbol** of **the** **truth** in **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**; the **fire** **is** **the** **specific** **fire** of **the** **Zoroastrian** **temple**); the **Avestan** **name** **is** **Asha** **Vahishta**; the **Pahlavi** **name** **is** **Ardwahisht**; the **Persian** **name** **is** **Ordibehesht**.

**Khshathra** **Vairya** **guards** **the** **metals** (**the** **good** **creation** of **the** **metals**; the **metals** **are** **the** **symbol** of **the** **kingdom** in **the** **Zoroastrian** **tradition**); the **Avestan** **name** **is** **Khshathra** **Vairya**; the **Pahlavi** **name** **is** **Shahrewar**; the **Persian** **name** **is** **Shahrivar**.
