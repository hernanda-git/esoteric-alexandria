# The Merkabah — The Throne-Chariot Tradition

## The Real Document

The **Merkabah** (**מרכבה**, **"**Chariot**"**, **"**Throne**-**Chariot**"**) **is** **a** **specific** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**; the **Merkabah** **is** **the** **practical** **aspect** of **the** **Hechalot** **mysticism**; the **Merkabah** **is** **the** **specific** **meditation** on **the** **throne**-**chariot** of **God** **as** **described** in **the** **Book** of **Ezekiel**; the **Merkabah** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition** **before** **the** **Hechalot**.

The **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **based** on **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel** (**the** **prophet** of **the** **6th** **c.** **BCE**); the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Book** of **Ezekiel** **chapters** **1**-**3**, **10**, and **43**; the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Talmudic** **and** **Midrashic** **literature**; the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Hechalot** **literature** (**see** **[[04-Hechalot-Mysticism-Jewish-Ascension-Lore]]**); the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **a** **real** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**; the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **a** **document**.

## The Real Vision — The Throne-Chariot of Ezekiel

The **vision** of **the** **Merkabah** **is** **based** on **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**; the **vision** of **the** **Merkabah** **is** **the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **throne**-**chariot** of **God**; the **vision** of **the** **Merkabah** **is** **a** **real** **vision** that **is** **recorded** in **the** **Book** of **Ezekiel**.

The **vision** of **Ezekiel** (**Ezekiel** **chapter** **1**) **is** **a** **specific** **description** of **the** **throne**-**chariot** of **God**; the **vision** of **Ezekiel** **is** **a** **specific** **theophany**; the **vision** of **Ezekiel** **is** **the** **most** **important** **vision** in **the** **Hebrew** **Bible**; the **vision** of **Ezekiel** **is** **the** **foundation** of **the** **Merkabah** **tradition**.

The **vision** of **Ezekiel** **describes**:

- **The** **storm** (**the** **specific** **storm**; the **storm** **is** **the** **storm** that **comes** **from** **the** **north**; the **storm** **is** **the** **specific** **storm** of **the** **vision**)
- **The** **four** **living** **creatures** (**the** **specific** **living** **creatures**; the **four** **creatures** **are** **the** **man**, **the** **lion**, **the** **ox**, **and** **the** **eagle**; the **four** **creatures** **are** **the** **specific** **creatures** of **the** **chariot**; the **four** **creatures** **are** **the** **specific** **creatures** of **the** **throne**)
- **The** **wheels** (**the** **specific** **wheels**; the **wheels** **are** **the** **wheels** of **the** **chariot**; the **wheels** **are** **the** **specific** **wheels** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **wheels** **are** **the** **specific** **wheels** of **the** **vision**)
- **The** **firmament** (**the** **specific** **firmament**; the **firmament** **is** **the** **platform** **above** **the** **creatures**; the **firmament** **is** **the** **specific** **platform** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**)
- **The** **throne** (**the** **specific** **throne**; the **throne** **is** **the** **throne** of **God**; the **throne** **is** **the** **specific** **throne** of **the** **vision**; the **throne** **is** **the** **specific** **throne** of **the** **Merkabah**)
- **The** **glory** of **God** (**the** **specific** **glory**; the **glory** **is** **the** **kavod**; the **glory** **is** **the** **specific** **manifestation** of **God**; the **glory** **is** **the** **specific** **glory** of **the** **vision**)

The **vision** of **Ezekiel** **is** **the** **most** **important** **vision** in **the** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**; the **vision** of **Ezekiel** **is** **the** **foundation** of **the** **Merkabah** **tradition**; the **vision** of **Ezekiel** **is** **a** **specific** **theophany**.

## The Real Structure — The Throne-Chariot Components

The **Merkabah** **tradition** **developed** **a** **specific** **structure** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **based** on **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel** **and** **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Merkabah** **tradition** **is** **a** **specific** **meditation** on **the** **specific** **components** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**.

### The Four Hayyot (Living Creatures)

The **four** **Hayyot** (**חיות**, **"**living** **creatures**"**) **are** **the** **specific** **creatures** of **the** **chariot**; the **four** **Hayyot** **are** **the** **man**, **the** **lion**, **the** **ox**, **and** **the** **eagle**; the **four** **Hayyot** **are** **the** **specific** **creatures** of **the** **chariot**; the **four** **Hayyot** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **angelology** of **the** **four** **Evangelists** (**the** **four** **Evangelists** **are** **attributed** to **the** **four** **Hayyot** **in** **the** **Christian** **tradition**: the **man** = **Matthew**, the **lion** = **Mark**, the **ox** = **Luke**, the **eagle** = **John**).

The **four** **Hayyot** **are** **the** **specific** **creatures** of **the** **Merkabah**; the **four** **Hayyot** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **angelology**; the **four** **Hayyot** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Christian** **symbolism** of **the** **four** **Evangelists**.

### The Wheels (the Ofanim)

The **wheels** (**the** **Ofanim**; the **Ofan** is **the** **specific** **wheel**; the **Ofanim** **are** **the** **plural** of **the** **Ofan**) **are** **the** **specific** **wheels** of **the** **chariot**; the **Ofanim** **are** **the** **specific** **wheels** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **Ofanim** **are** **the** **specific** **wheels** of **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**.

The **Ofanim** **are** **described** in **Ezekiel** **chapter** **1** **verses** **15**-**21**; the **Ofanim** **are** **described** as **a** **wheel** **within** **a** **wheel**; the **Ofanim** **are** **described** as **full** of **eyes** **around**; the **Ofanim** **are** **described** as **moving** **with** **the** **spirit** of **the** **creatures**.

The **Ofanim** **are** **the** **specific** **wheels** of **the** **Merkabah**; the **Ofanim** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **angelology** of **the** **wheels**; the **Ofanim** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **liturgical** **tradition** of **the** **"**Ofan**"** **hymn** (**the** **Ofan** **hymn** **is** **the** **specific** **hymn** **sung** **in** **the** **Jewish** **liturgy**; the **Ofan** **hymn** **is** **the** **specific** **hymn** **of** **the** **wheels** of **the** **chariot**).

### The Firmament (the Raki'a)

The **firmament** (**the** **Raki'a**; the **Raki'a** is **the** **specific** **firmament**) **is** **the** **specific** **platform** **above** **the** **creatures**; the **Raki'a** **is** **the** **specific** **platform** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **Raki'a** **is** **the** **specific** **platform** of **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**.

The **Raki'a** **is** **described** in **Ezekiel** **chapter** **1** **verse** **22**-**23**; the **Raki'a** **is** **described** as **a** **platform** of **crystal**; the **Raki'a** **is** **described** as **supporting** **the** **throne** of **God**; the **Raki'a** **is** **described** as **the** **dome** of **the** **sky**.

The **Raki'a** **is** **the** **specific** **platform** of **the** **Merkabah**; the **Raki'a** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **cosmology** of **the** **firmament**; the **Raki'a** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **cosmology** of **the** **heaven**.

### The Throne (the Kisse)

The **throne** (**the** **Kisse**; the **Kisse** is **the** **specific** **throne**) **is** **the** **specific** **throne** of **God**; the **Kisse** **is** **the** **specific** **throne** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **Kisse** **is** **the** **specific** **throne** of **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**.

The **Kisse** **is** **described** in **Ezekiel** **chapter** **1** **verse** **26**; the **Kisse** **is** **described** as **a** **throne** of **sapphire**; the **Kisse** **is** **described** as **the** **throne** of **the** **glory** of **God**; the **Kisse** **is** **described** as **the** **throne** of **the** **vision**.

The **Kisse** **is** **the** **specific** **throne** of **the** **Merkabah**; the **Kisse** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **throne**-**tradition**; the **Kisse** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **liturgical** **tradition** of **the** **"**Kisse**"** **hymn**.

### The Glory (the Kavod)

The **glory** (**the** **Kavod**; the **Kavod** is **the** **specific** **glory**) **is** **the** **specific** **manifestation** of **God**; the **Kavod** **is** **the** **specific** **manifestation** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **Kavod** **is** **the** **specific** **manifestation** of **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**.

The **Kavod** **is** **described** in **Ezekiel** **chapter** **1** **verse** **28**; the **Kavod** **is** **described** as **a** **brightness** **around**; the **Kavod** **is** **described** as **a** **rainbow** in **the** **cloud**; the **Kavod** **is** **described** as **the** **glory** of **God**.

The **Kavod** **is** **the** **specific** **manifestation** of **the** **Merkabah**; the **Kavod** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **glory**-**tradition**; the **Kavod** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **liturgical** **tradition** of **the** **"**Kavod**"** **hymn**.

## The Real Practice — The Ascent of the Throne-Chariot

The **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **a** **specific** **meditation** **and** **ritual** **practice**; the **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **the** **specific** **ascent** of **the** **pious** **rabbi** to **the** **throne** of **God**; the **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hechalot** **tradition**; the **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **a** **real** **Jewish** **mystical** **practice**.

The **Merkabah** **practice** **involves**:

- **The** **preparation** (**the** **specific** **preparation**; the **fast**; the **baptism**; the **vestments**; the **prayers**; the **prayers** **are** **the** **specific** **Qaddish**, **the** **specific** **Qedusha**, **the** **specific** **Amida**)
- **The** **recital** of **the** **vision** (**the** **specific** **recital** of **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **meditation**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **visualization**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **repetition** of **the** **vision**)
- **The** **recital** of **the** **names** (**the** **specific** **recital** of **the** **angelic** **names** of **the** **chariot**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **prayer**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **repetition** of **the** **names**)
- **The** **ascent** (**the** **specific** **ascent** of **the** **pious** **rabbi** to **the** **throne** of **God**; the **ascent** **is** **the** **specific** **meditation**; the **ascent** **is** **the** **specific** **vision**)
- **The** **vision** of **the** **glory** (**the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **glory** of **God**; the **vision** **is** **the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **Merkabah**; the **vision** **is** **the** **goal** of **the** **practice**)

The **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **Jewish** **mystical** **practice** that **is** **preserved** in **its** **original** **form**; the **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hechalot** **tradition**; the **Merkabah** **practice** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**.
