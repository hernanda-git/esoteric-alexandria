# The Mandaeans — The Real Last Gnostics of Mesopotamia

## The Real People

The **Mandaeans** (**Arabic** **الصابئة المندائيون**, **al**-**Ṣābi'ah** **al**-**Mandā'iyūn**; the **Mandaean** **self**-**name** is **manda**-**d**-**na** = **"**gnostics**"**) **are** **a** **gnostic** **religious** **ethnoreligious** **community** that **has** **been** **continuously** **in** **existence** **in** **southern** **Mesopotamia** (**modern** **Iraq** **and** **Iran**) **from** **at** **least** **the** **3rd** **c.** **CE** **to** **the** **present** **day**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **a** **real** **people**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **a** **real** **religious** **tradition**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **a** **real** **ethnoreligious** **community**.

The **Mandaeans** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **continuously** **existing** **gnostic** **community** in **the** **world**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **continuously** **existing** **Sabaean** (**"**Sabaean**"**, **the** **Arabic** **designation** **of** **the** **Mandaeans** as **one** **of** **the** **three** **recognized** **Sabians** of **the** **Quran**) **religious** **community** in **the** **world**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **continuously** **existing** **baptist** (**"**they** **baptize**"**) **religious** **community** in **the** **world**; the **Mandaeans** **are** **the** **most** **ancient** **continuously** **existing** **pre**-**Islamic** **religious** **community** in **the** **Middle** **East**.

The **Mandaeans** **were** **classified** as **"**Sabaeans**"** **in** **the** **Quran** (**Quran** **2**:**62**; **5**:**69**; **22**:**17**) **and** **were** **given** **the** **status** of **the** **People** of **the** **Book** (**dhimmī**) in **the** **Abbasid** **caliphate** (**the** **Abbasid** **classification** **saved** **the** **Mandaeans** from **the** **forced** **conversion** **of** **the** **11th** **c.** **CE**; the **Abbasid** **classification** **preserved** **the** **Mandaeans** **as** **a** **religious** **community** **from** **the** **8th** **c.** **CE** **onwards**).

## The Real History

The **Mandaean** **history** **is** **a** **continuous** **history** **of** **the** **Mandaean** **community** **in** **southern** **Mesopotamia**; the **Mandaean** **history** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **continuously** **existing** **gnostic** **history** in **the** **world**.

The **Mandaean** **history** **is** **divided** **into** **three** **major** **periods**:

### The Origin Period (c. 1st-3rd c. CE)

The **Mandaean** **tradition** **attributes** **the** **founding** **of** **the** **community** to **John** **the** **Baptist** (**the** **1st**-**c.** **Jewish** **prophet**; the **Mandaeans** **regard** **John** as **the** **greatest** **prophet**; the **Mandaeans** **do** **not** **regard** **Jesus** as **a** **prophet**); the **Mandaean** **tradition** **holds** **that** **John** **baptized** **in** **the** **Jordan** **river**; the **Mandaean** **tradition** **holds** **that** **the** **first** **Mandaean** **priest** was **Anush** **Uthra** (**the** **heavenly** **messenger**; the **first** **priest** **of** **the** **Mandaeans**).

The **historical** **evidence** **suggests** **the** **Mandaeans** **emerged** as **a** **distinct** **community** in **the** **1st**-**3rd** **c.** **CE** **in** **southern** **Mesopotamia**; the **Mandaeans** **developed** **from** **a** **Jewish**-**baptist** **sect** **into** **a** **distinct** **gnostic** **religion**; the **Mandaeans** **incorporated** **elements** **of** **the** **Iranian** (**Zoroastrian**), **Babylonian**, and **Jewish** **traditions**; the **Mandaeans** **developed** **a** **distinctive** **gnostic** **mythology** **and** **liturgy**.

### The Classical Period (c. 3rd-7th c. CE)

The **Mandaean** **classical** **period** **is** **the** **period** of **the** **composition** of **the** **Mandaean** **scriptures**; the **Mandaean** **classical** **period** **is** **the** **period** of **the** **Ginza** **Rabba**; the **Mandaean** **classical** **period** **is** **the** **period** of **the** **Qolasta**; the **Mandaean** **classical** **period** **is** **the** **period** of **the** **Mandaean** **Book** of **John**.

The **Mandaean** **scriptures** **are** **composed** **in** **Mandaean** **language** (**a** **dialect** of **Eastern** **Aramaic**; the **Mandaean** **language** **is** **a** **distinct** **language** **that** **is** **closely** **related** to **the** **Syriac** **Aramaic**); the **Mandaean** **scriptures** **are** **written** in **the** **Mandaean** **script** (**a** **distinct** **cursive** **script**; the **Mandaean** **script** **is** **used** **only** by **the** **Mandaeans**).

### The Islamic Period (c. 7th c. CE to present)

The **Islamic** **period** **is** **the** **period** of **the** **Mandaean** **community** **under** **Islamic** **rule**; the **Mandaean** **community** **survived** **the** **Islamic** **conquest** **as** **a** **dhimmī** **minority**; the **Mandaean** **community** **preserved** **its** **religion** **and** **scriptures** **through** **the** **Islamic** **period**; the **Mandaean** **community** **continues** **to** **exist** **in** **Iraq** **and** **Iran** **and** **the** **diaspora** **today** (the **Mandaean** **population** **is** **estimated** at **60**,**000**-**70**,**000** **as** of **2024**).

The **Mandaean** **community** **in** **Iraq** **and** **Iran** **has** **declined** **sharply** in **the** **20th**-**21st** **c.** **due** to **the** **Iraq** **wars**, **the** **Iranian** **discrimination**, and **the** **emigration** of **the** **Mandaean** **community**; the **Mandaean** **diaspora** **is** **in** **Sweden**, **Australia**, **the** **United** **States**, and **Western** **Europe**.

## The Real Doctrine — The Mandaean Gnosis

The **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **a** **specific** **gnostic** **doctrine**; the **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **a** **real** **gnostic** **tradition**; the **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **gnostic** **mythology**; the **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **a** **document**.

The **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **based** on **the** **principle** of **the** **separation** of **the** **Light** from **the** **Darkness**; the **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **based** on **the** **principle** of **the** **ascent** of **the** **soul** from **the** **world** of **the** **Darkness** to **the** **world** of **the** **Light**; the **Mandaean** **doctrine** **is** **based** on **the** **principle** of **the** **redemption** of **the** **soul** through **the** **baptism** **and** **the** **cultic** **practice**.

### The Cosmology — The World of Light and the World of Darkness

The **Mandaean** **cosmology** **is** **a** **dualist** **cosmology**; the **Mandaean** **cosmology** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **gnostic** **cosmology**; the **Mandaean** **cosmology** **is** **based** on **the** **two** **worlds**:

- **The** **World** of **Light** (**the** **Aina**; the **upper** **world**; the **world** of **the** **Father**; the **world** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **world** of **the** **"**Great** **Life**"**)
- **The** **World** of **Darkness** (**the** **Tibil**; the **lower** **world**; the **world** of **the** **earth**; the **world** of **the** **matter**; the **world** of **the** **Ruh** (the **evil** **spirit**; the **Lord** of **the** **World** of **Darkness**))

The **Mandaean** **cosmology** **is** **based** on **the** **emanation** of **the** **World** of **Light**; the **Mandaean** **cosmology** **is** **based** on **the** **descent** of **the** **Adam** to **the** **World** of **Darkness**; the **Mandaean** **cosmology** **is** **based** on **the** **redemption** of **the** **Adam** through **the** **baptism** **and** **the** **cultic** **practice**.

### The Emanation — The uthra (the Angels)

The **Mandaean** **emanation** **is** **the** **specific** **emanation** of **the** **World** of **Light**; the **Mandaean** **emanation** **is** **a** **specific** **angelology**; the **Mandaean** **emanation** **is** **based** on **the** **"**uthra**"** (**the** **angels** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**; the **"**uthra**"** **is** **the** **plural** of **the** **"**utra**"** (**the** **angel**)).

The **major** **uthra** **are**:

- **Yushamin** (**the** **"**Name**"**; the **First** **emanation**; the **emanation** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **first** **being** **of** **the** **World** of **Light**)
- **Abathur** (**the** **"**Father**"**; the **second** **emanation**; the **emanation** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **Father** of **the** **uthra**; the **keeper** of **the** **gate** of **the** **World** of **Light**)
- **Manda** **d**-**Hiia** (**the** **"**Knowledge** of** **Life**"**; the **third** **emanation**; the **emanation** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **knower** of **the** **truth**; the **archangel** of **the** **Mandaeans**)
- **Hibil** **Ziwa** (**the** **"**Light**"**; the **fourth** **emanation**; the **emanation** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **first** **of** **the** **redemptors**; the **angel** of **the** **baptism**)
- **Shithil** (**the** **fifth** **emanation**; the **emanation** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **angel** of **the** **cultic** **practice**)
- **Anush** **Uthra** (**the** **sixth** **emanation**; the **emanation** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **first** **Mandaean** **priest**; the **angel** of **the** **priesthood**)

The **uthra** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**; the **uthra** **are** **the** **specific** **emanations** of **the** **Source** of **Light**; the **uthra** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Mandaean** **angelology**.

### The Redemption — The Baptism (the Masbuta)

The **Mandaean** **redemption** **is** **the** **specific** **redemption** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**; the **Mandaean** **redemption** **is** **based** on **the** **baptism** (**the** **masbuta**; the **Mandaean** **baptism** is **a** **ritual** **immersion** in **flowing** **water**; the **Mandaean** **baptism** is **the** **specific** **sacrament** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**).

The **Mandaean** **baptism** **is** **performed** **in** **a** **running** **river** (**the** **Mandaean** **river** is **the** **specific** **river** of **the** **Mandaean** **community**; the **Mandaean** **river** is **typically** **a** **small** **canal** or **a** **small** **stream**); the **Mandaean** **baptism** **is** **performed** by **a** **priest** (**the** **tarmida**); the **Mandaean** **baptism** **involves** **a** **triple** **immersion** (**the** **specific** **triple** **immersion**; the **immersion** **is** **the** **specific** **rite** of **the** **Mandaean** **baptism**; the **immersion** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **the** **baptism**).

The **Mandaean** **baptism** **is** **the** **specific** **sacrament** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**; the **Mandaean** **baptism** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Mandaean** **redemption**; the **Mandaean** **baptism** **is** **the** **most** **important** **ritual** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**.

### The Eschatology — The Ascent of the Soul

The **Mandaean** **eschatology** **is** **the** **specific** **eschatology** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**; the **Mandaean** **eschatology** **is** **based** on **the** **ascent** of **the** **soul** through **the** **7** **stages** of **the** **post**-**mortem** **world**; the **Mandaean** **eschatology** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **gnostic** **eschatology**.

The **Mandaean** **eschatology** **involves**:

- **The** **departure** of **the** **soul** from **the** **body** (the **specific** **departure**; the **departure** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **the** **soul** **leaving** **the** **body**; the **departure** **is** **the** **specific** **moment** of **the** **death**)
- **The** **ascent** through **the** **7** **stages** (the **specific** **ascent**; the **7** **stages** **are** **the** **specific** **stages** of **the** **post**-**mortem** **world**; the **7** **stages** **are** **the** **specific** **stages** of **the** **Mandaean** **eschatology**)
- **The** **judgment** of **the** **soul** (the **specific** **judgment**; the **judgment** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **the** **Mandaean** **judgment**; the **judgment** **is** **based** on **the** **Mandaean** **ethics**; the **judgment** **is** **based** on **the** **Mandaean** **baptisms**)
- **The** **ascent** to **the** **World** of **Light** (the **specific** **ascent**; the **ascent** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **the** **soul** **ascending** to **the** **World** of **Light**; the **ascent** **is** **the** **goal** of **the** **Mandaean** **eschatology**)

The **Mandaean** **eschatology** **is** **the** **specific** **eschatology** of **the** **Mandaean** **tradition**; the **Mandaean** **eschatology** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **gnostic** **eschatology**; the **Mandaean** **eschatology** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Mandaean** **practice**.
