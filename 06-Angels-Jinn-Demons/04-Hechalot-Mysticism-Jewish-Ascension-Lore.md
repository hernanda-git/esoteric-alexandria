# Hechalot Mysticism — Jewish Ascension Lore

## The Real Document

The **Hechalot** (**היכלות**, **"**Palaces**"**, **"**Halls**"**) **literature** **is** **the** **corpus** of **Jewish** **mystical** **texts** **describing** **the** **ascent** of **the** **pious** **rabbi** through **the** **heavenly** **palaces** to **the** **throne** of **God**; the **Hechalot** **literature** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition** that **is** **preserved** in **its** **original** **form**; the **Hechalot** **literature** **is** **the** **direct** **ancestor** of **the** **Kabbalah** of **the** **Middle** **Ages**.

The **Hechalot** **literature** **is** **preserved** in **real** **manuscripts**; the **Hechalot** **literature** **is** **a** **real** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**; the **Hechalot** **literature** **is** **the** **most** **important** **source** of **the** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition** **before** **the** **Kabbalah** **of** **the** **Middle** **Ages**.

## The Real Texts — The 4 Major Hechalot Texts

The **Hechalot** **corpus** **is** **composed** of **4** **major** **texts**; the **4** **major** **texts** **are** **the** **canonical** **texts** of **the** **Hechalot** **tradition**; the **4** **major** **texts** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hechalot** **practice**.

### The Hechalot Rabbati (Greater Hechalot)

The **Hechalot** **Rabbati** (**the** **Greater** **Hechalot**) **is** **the** **major** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **is** **preserved** in **multiple** **manuscripts** (the **earliest** **manuscript** **is** the **10th**-**c.** **manuscript**); the **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **is** **the** **foundational** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **tradition**; the **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **is** **the** **text** that **is** **attributed** to **Rabbi** **Yishmael** (the **tanna** of **the** **2nd** **c.** **CE**; the **rabbi** who **entered** **the** **Holy** of **Holies** **in** **the** **Temple**).

The **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **presents**:

- The **prayer** of **the** **descender** (**the** **specific** **prayer** of **the** **descender**; the **prayer** **is** **the** **specific** **recital** of **the** **ascent**; the **prayer** **is** **the** **specific** **prayer** for **the** **descent** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**; the **prayer** **is** **the** **specific** **prayer** for **the** **vision** of **the** **throne**-**chariot**)
- The **names** of **the** **angels** of **the** **palaces** (**the** **specific** **angelic** **names** of **the** **7** **heavenly** **palaces**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **specific** **palaces**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **gatekeepers** of **the** **specific** **palaces**)
- The **formulae** of **the** **ascent** (**the** **specific** **formulae** of **the** **ascent**; the **formulae** **are** **the** **specific** **prayers**; the **formulae** **are** **the** **specific** **recitals**; the **formulae** **are** **the** **specific** **seals** of **the** **palaces**)
- The **practices** of **the** **ascent** (**the** **specific** **practices** of **the** **ascent**; the **practices** **are** **the** **specific** **fasts**; the **practices** **are** **the** **specific** **incense**; the **practices** **are** **the** **specific** **meditation**; the **practices** **are** **the** **specific** **rituals**)

The **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **is** **the** **most** **complete** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hechalot** **tradition**; the **Hechalot** **Rabbati** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Hechalot** **practice**.

### The Hechalot Zutarti (Lesser Hechalot)

The **Hechalot** **Zutarti** **presents**:

- The **names** of **the** **angels** of **the** **chariot** (**the** **specific** **angelic** **names** of **the** **chariot**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **chariot**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **operators** of **the** **chariot**)

The **Hechalot** **Zutarti** **is** **the** **second** **most** **important** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Hechalot** **Zutarti** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **vision** of **the** **chariot** **tradition**.

### The Ma'aseh Merkabah (Work of the Chariot)

The **Ma'aseh** **Merkabah** (**the** **Work** of **the** **Chariot**) **is** **the** **third** **major** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Ma'aseh** **Merkabah** **is** **preserved** in **multiple** **manuscripts**; the **Ma'aseh** **Merkabah** **is** **the** **text** that **is** **attributed** to **Rabbi** **Yishmael** (the **tanna** of **the** **2nd** **c.** **CE**; the **rabbi** who **performed** **the** **Work** of **the** **Chariot**).

The **Ma'aseh** **Merkabah** **presents**:

- The **vision** of **the** **chariot** (**the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **chariot**; the **vision** **is** **the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **chariot** of **Ezekiel**; the **vision** **is** **the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **chariot** of **Moses**; the **vision** **is** **the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **chariot** of **the** **pious** **rabbi**)
- The **names** of **the** **angels** of **the** **chariot** (**the** **specific** **angelic** **names** of **the** **chariot**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **chariot**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **operators** of **the** **chariot**)

The **Ma'aseh** **Merkabah** **is** **the** **third** **most** **important** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Ma'aseh** **Merkabah** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Work** of **the** **chariot** **tradition**.

### The Shi'ur Qomah (The Measurement of the Body)

The **Shi'ur** **Qomah** (**the** **Measurement** of **the** **Body**) **is** **the** **fourth** **major** **text** of **the** **Hechalot** **literature**; the **Shi'ur** **Qomah** **is** **preserved** in **multiple** **manuscripts**; the **Shi'ur** **Qomah** **is** **the** **text** that **is** **attributed** to **Rabbi** **Akiva** (the **tanna** of **the** **2nd** **c.** **CE**; the **rabbi** who **measured** **the** **body** of **God**).

The **Shi'ur** **Qomah** **presents**:

## The Real Practice — The Ascent through the 7 Palaces

The **Hechalot** **practice** **is** **a** **specific** **meditation** **and** **ritual** **practice**; the **Hechalot** **practice** **is** **the** **specific** **ascent** of **the** **pious** **rabbi** through **the** **7** **heavenly** **palaces** to **the** **throne** of **God**; the **Hechalot** **practice** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hechalot** **tradition**.

The **7** **heavenly** **palaces** **are**:

- **The** **1st** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **firmament**; the **palace** of **the** **visible** **sky**; the **palace** of **the** **creation**)
- **The** **2nd** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **clouds**; the **palace** of **the** **rain**; the **palace** of **the** **atmosphere**)
- **The** **3rd** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **fire**; the **palace** of **the** **lightning**; the **palace** of **the** **elemental** **fire**)
- **The** **4th** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **chariot**; the **palace** of **the** **throne**-**chariot** of **Ezekiel**; the **palace** of **the** **vision** of **Ezekiel**)
- **The** **5th** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **angels**; the **palace** of **the** **angelic** **orders**; the **palace** of **the** **angelic** **liturgy**)
- **The** **6th** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **archangels**; the **palace** of **the** **archangelic** **orders**; the **palace** of **the** **archangelic** **liturgy**)
- **The** **7th** **Palace** (**the** **palace** of **the** **throne**; the **palace** of **the** **throne** of **God**; the **palace** of **the** **Holy** of **Holies** of **the** **heavens**)

The **7** **palaces** **are** **guarded** by **the** **gatekeeper** **angels**; the **7** **palaces** **are** **accessed** by **the** **specific** **seals**; the **7** **palaces** **are** **the** **specific** **stages** of **the** **ascent**.

The **practice** of **the** **Hechalot** **involves**:

- **The** **preparation** (**the** **specific** **preparation**; the **fast**; the **baptism**; the **vestments**; the **prayers**; the **prayers** **are** **the** **specific** **Qaddish**, **the** **specific** **Qedusha**, **the** **specific** **Amida**)
- **The** **recital** of **the** **seals** (**the** **specific** **recital** of **the** **seals** of **the** **palaces**; the **seals** **are** **the** **specific** **angelic** **names**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **prayer**; the **recital** **is** **the** **specific** **prayer** of **the** **gatekeeper**)
- **The** **ascent** (**the** **specific** **ascent** through **the** **7** **palaces**; the **ascent** **is** **the** **specific** **meditation**; the **ascent** **is** **the** **specific** **vision**)
- **The** **vision** of **the** **throne** (**the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **throne** of **God**; the **vision** **is** **the** **specific** **vision** of **the** **Holy** of **Holies** of **the** **heavens**; the **vision** **is** **the** **goal** of **the** **practice**)
- **The** **return** (**the** **specific** **return** of **the** **pious** **rabbi** to **the** **normal** **state**; the **return** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **closing** of **the** **vision**; the **return** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **returning** to **the** **earthly** **state**)

The **Hechalot** **practice** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **Jewish** **mystical** **practice** that **is** **preserved** in **its** **original** **form**; the **Hechalot** **practice** **is** **the** **direct** **ancestor** of **the** **Kabbalah** of **the** **Middle** **Ages**; the **Hechalot** **practice** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Jewish** **mystical** **tradition**.
