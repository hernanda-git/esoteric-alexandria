# The 12-Month Esoteric Self-Study Curriculum

## The Claim

A modern practitioner of the esoteric tradition can **self-study** the major texts and the major practices of the tradition in **12 months** of focused work. The curriculum is **not** a substitute for a **lineage teacher** (the chain tradition requires the chain), but the curriculum is a **foundation** for serious engagement with the tradition.

The 12-month curriculum is **one option** among many. The library's category 22 also offers the **3-month quick curriculum** (see [[02-3-Month-Foundations-Quick-Curriculum]]) and the **2-year deep-dive curriculum** (see [[03-Deep-Dive-Alchemy-2-Year-Curriculum]]).

The 12-month curriculum is the **right size** for the **serious practitioner** who has **other commitments** (a job, a family, a community) and **2-3 hours per day** to dedicate to the study.

## The 12 Months

### Month 1 — Foundations: The Esoteric Tradition

**The work**: read the **foundations** of the esoteric tradition. Understand the **esoteric / exoteric** distinction, the **transmission** chains, the **perennial philosophy**.

**Texts**:
- [[01-Origins-Traditions/01-What-Is-Esotericism]] (this library)
- [[01-Origins-Traditions/02-Esoteric-Vs-Exoteric]] (this library)
- [[01-Origins-Traditions/05-Perennial-Philosophy]] (this library)
- Wouter Hanegraaff, *Western Esotericism: A Guide for the Perplexed* (2013)

**Practice**:
- Keep a **journal** of the readings
- **Identify** the tradition that **calls** to you (which is your entry point?)
- **Identify** the tradition that **challenges** you (which is your growth point?)

### Month 2 — The Islamic Tradition: 'Ilm al-Huruf and Sufism

**The work**: enter the **Islamic esoteric tradition** through the 'Ilm al-Huruf and the Sufism.

**Texts**:
- [[02-Hurufiyya-Lettrism/01-Ilm-al-Huruf-Overview]] (this library)
- [[02-Hurufiyya-Lettrism/02-Abjad-Numerals]] (this library)
- [[09-Sufism-Irfan/01-Sufism-Overview]] (this library)
- The Shams al-Ma'arif (Al-Buni, 13th c.) — the **core text** of the tradition
- William Chittick, *The Sufi Path of Knowledge* (1989) — the **modern standard** on Ibn Arabi

**Practice**:
- **Calculate** the abjad value of your own name and the names of your family
- **Practice** the Sufi *dhikr* (remembrance) — the repetition of the 99 names of God, the recitation of the basmala
- **Journal** the experience

### Month 3 — The Western Tradition: Hermeticism, Agrippa, Picatrix

**The work**: enter the **Western esoteric tradition** through the Hermetic, Agrippan, and Picatrix traditions.

**Texts**:
- [[10-Hermeticism-Gnosticism/01-Corpus-Hermeticum]] (this library)

- The *Corpus Hermeticum* (Copenhaver translation, 1992)
- The *Three Books of Occult Philosophy* (Agrippa, Tyson translation, 1993)
- The *Picatrix* (Greer & Warnock translation, 2010)

**Practice**:
- **Read** the *Poimandres* (the first treatise of the Corpus Hermeticum) every morning for a week
- **Meditate** on the Hermetic vision
- **Build** a simple sigil for a specific purpose (a small ambition, a personal goal)

## The 12-Month Discipline

A successful 12-month curriculum requires **discipline**. The minimum:

- **2-3 hours per day** of reading, practice, journaling
- **1 day per week** of silent retreat (Saturday or Sunday)
- **1 weekend per quarter** of longer retreat (3 days)
- **1 week per year** of extended retreat (7 days)

The discipline is **the same** as the discipline of any serious practice (the athlete, the musician, the scholar). The discipline is **the practice**.

## The Practice Note

A modern practitioner of the 12-month curriculum requires:

1. **The texts** — read the texts in the **order given**. The order is **deliberate** — the foundations first, the tradition-specific second, the integration last
2. **The practice** — practice **the discipline**. The practice is the **lifelong** work
3. **The community** — find a **community** of practitioners (a local group, an online group, a teacher)
4. **The lineage** — find a **lineage teacher** (the chain tradition). The 12-month curriculum is the **foundation**; the lineage is the **transmission**
5. **The journal** — keep a **lifelong journal** of the practice

The library's category 22 is the **textual foundation**. The practice requires the community.
