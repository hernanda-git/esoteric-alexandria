# Geomancy — When the Earth Speaks

## The Method

**Geomancy** (Arabic *'ilm al-raml*, "the science of the sand"; Latin *geomantia*) is a **cleromantic** method that produces 16 "figures" (combinations of 4 binary elements) and reads the question's answer in their configuration.

The basic procedure:

1. **Generate the figures** — make 16 marks on the ground (or on paper), each by random (counting, clicking, or "poking" the ground). The marks are counted in **groups of 2**, giving 1 or 2 dots per row, 4 rows per figure. Each figure is one of 16 possibilities (2⁴ = 16).
2. **The 16 figures** are the **master alphabet** of geomancy. Each has a name, a character, a planet, a zodiacal correspondence.
3. **Combine the 16 figures** in a **House Chart** (4 rows of 4 figures, called "houses", each governing a different area of the question: the querent, the queried, the witnesses, the judge).
4. **Read the chart** — the "witnesses" combine to give the "judge", which is the **final answer** to the question.
5. **The 4 "daughters"** of the judge give the **supporting details** of the answer.

The method is **fast, simple, and surprisingly accurate**. The classic geomancy masters reported a 70-80% accuracy rate (the al-Buni tradition claims 90%).

## The 12 Astrological Houses

The geomancy chart has **12 houses** (matching the astrological houses of the natal chart):

| House | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1 | The querent (the one asking) |
| 2 | Money, possessions, resources |
| 3 | Siblings, neighbors, short travel, communication |
| 4 | Parents, home, property, the end of the matter |
| 5 | Children, pleasure, creativity, speculation |
| 6 | Health, servants, small animals, daily work |
| 7 | The queried (the one asked about), marriage, open enemies |
| 8 | Death, sex, transformation, inheritance, occult |
| 9 | Long travel, religion, higher learning, the law |
| 10 | Career, social standing, the mother (in some systems), honor |
| 11 | Friends, hopes, the querent's allies |
| 12 | Hidden enemies, prison, sorrow, self-undoing |

The 4 **witnesses** (houses 1, 4, 7, 10) combine to give the **judge** (the final answer). The 4 **daughters** (houses 11, 12, 5, 9) are the supporting details.

## The Generation Methods

There are several methods to generate the 16 figures:

1. **The original Arabic method** — mark the sand with a reed pen, count the marks
2. **The Latin method** — make 16 lines of dots by random (1 dot or 2 dots per line, 4 lines per figure)
3. **The medieval method** — cast 16 pebbles or coins
4. **The modern method** — generate 16 random binary numbers (each figure is 4 binary digits, so the generation is a single random number)
5. **The "computer" method** — use a `random.randint(1, 2)` for each line

The method is **insignificant** for the result (this is the stochastic theory of divination in operation). The randomness of the generation is the **sympathetic sample** of the larger pattern.

## The Reading

The reading is the **hard part**. A trained geomancer reads:

1. **The judge** — the final answer
2. **The witnesses** — the two opposing forces
3. **The houses** — the conditions in each area
4. **The aspects** — which figures are friendly, which are opposed
5. **The zodiacal positions** — the figures' rulers in the chart
6. **The planetary** — the figures' planetary rulers and their state

A reading is **not** a single sentence ("yes" or "no"). It is a **multi-layered interpretation** of the chart's structure. A trained geomancer can give a 15-30 minute reading from a single chart.

## The Modern Note

Geomancy is alive in three communities:

1. **The Arabic-Islamic tradition** — the *'ilm al-raml* is still practiced in Morocco, Tunisia, Egypt, the Gulf states, and Indonesia. There are many **living masters** with continuous traditions.
2. **The Western esoteric tradition** — the Golden Dawn, the modern Wicca, the chaos magic tradition, and the IOT all have geomancy in their systems. The *geomantia* of the Latin West is the most-reproduced form in modern English.
3. **The West African Ifa tradition** — the **Ifa** of the Yoruba and the **Opele** of the related traditions are **structurally identical** to the Arabic *'ilm al-raml*. The 16 Odu of Ifa = the 16 geomancy figures. The convergence is a famous case of cross-tradition concordance (possibly through the Berber-Arab trade routes of the trans-Saharan caravan).

The library's category 07 treats geomancy as the **most reliable** and **most accessible** of the classical divination systems. The figures are few (16), the chart is simple (16 figures in 12 houses), the method is fast (5 minutes), and the readings are accurate in the hands of a trained practitioner.

## The Practice

A modern geomancy practice requires:

1. **Learn the 16 figures** — their names, their composition, their planetary rulers
2. **Learn the 12 houses** — what each governs
3. **Practice the generation** — 16 figures per question, always random
4. **Practice the reading** — keep a log of every reading, the question, the chart, the verification
5. **Find a teacher** — the tradition is oral, the readings are best learned in person
6. **Develop the intuition** — the **first reading** of the chart is usually right; the geomancer who second-guesses is the geomancer who misses
