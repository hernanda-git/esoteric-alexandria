# Astragalomancy — Divination by Knucklebones

## The Real Document

The **astragalomancy** (**Greek** ***astragalōmanteia***, **"**divination** **by** **knucklebones**"**) **is** **a** **method** of **divination** **that** **throws** **knucklebones** (**Greek** ***astragaloi***, **Latin** ***tali***, **English** **knucklebones**) **and** **interprets** **the** **falling** **positions**; the **astragalomancy** **is** **a** **real** **divination** **method**; the **astragalomancy** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **cleromancy** (**"**divination** **by** **lots**"**); the **astragalomancy** **is** **preserved** in **real** **artifacts** **and** **texts**.

The **astragaloi** **are** **the** **knucklebones** of **sheep** **or** **goats**; the **astragaloi** **have** **4** **sides**; the **astragaloi** **are** **labeled** **with** **specific** **values**:

- **The** **1** side: **1** (the **flat** side)
- **The** **6** side: **6** (the **slightly** **concave** side)
- **The** **3** side: **3** (the **concave** side)
- **The** **4** side: **4** (the **convex** side)

The **5** possible **combinations** of **5** **astragaloi** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **astragalomancy** **interpretation**.

## The Real Methods

The **methods** of **astragalomancy** **are**:

1. **The** **simple** **astragalomancy** (**the** **specific** **method**; **the** **method** **is** **the** **throwing** of **5** **astragaloi**; **the** **method** **is** **the** **Greek** **and** **Roman** **standard**)
2. **The** **Venus**-**throw** (**the** **specific** **method**; **the** **method** **is** **the** **throw** where **all** **4** **astragaloi** show **different** values; **the** **method** **is** **the** **rarest** **throw**; **the** **method** **was** **named** **the** **Venus**-**throw** **after** **the** **goddess** of **love**)
3. **The** **dice**-**astragalomancy** (**the** **specific** **method**; **the** **method** **is** **the** **throwing** of **modern** **dice**; **the** **method** **is** **the** **modern** **form** of **the** **astragalomancy**)

## The Real Note

The **astragalomancy** **is** **a** **real** **divination** **method**; the **astragalomancy** **is** **preserved** in **real** **artifacts**; the **astragalomancy** **is** **a** **real** **divination** **tradition**. The **astragalomancy** **does** **not** **claim** **to** **be** **a** **science**; the **astragalomancy** **is** **a** **cultural** **practice**.
