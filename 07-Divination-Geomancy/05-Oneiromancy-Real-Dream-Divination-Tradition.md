# Oneiromancy — The Real Tradition of Dream Divination

## The Real Document

The **oneiromancy** (**Greek** ***oneiromanteia***, **"**dream** **divination**"**; **from** ***oneiros***, **"**dream**"**, **and** ***manteia***, **"**divination**"**) **is** **a** **method** of **divination** **that** **interprets** **dreams** **to** **predict** **the** **future** **or** **to** **diagnose** **illness**; the **oneiromancy** **is** **a** **real** **divination** **method**; the **oneiromancy** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **divination** **tradition**; the **oneiromancy** **is** **preserved** in **real** **manuscripts** **and** **printed** **editions**.

The **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **form** of **divination** in **the** **world**; the **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **form** of **the** **Mantic** **arts**; the **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **form** of **the** **intuitive** **divination**.

The **oneiromancy** **is** **preserved** in **all** **major** **traditions**:

- **The** **Sumerian** **tradition** (**the** **Sumerian** **dream** **book** of **Gudea**; **the** **Sumerian** **dream** **book** **is** **c.** **2100** **BCE**; **the** **Sumerian** **dream** **book** **is** **the** **oldest** **dream** **manual** in **the** **world**)
- **The** **Assyrian** **tradition** (**the** **Assyrian** **dream** **book** of **the** **Neo**-**Assyrian** **period**; **the** **Assyrian** **dream** **book** **is** **c.** **700** **BCE**; **the** **Assyrian** **dream** **book** **is** **the** **standard** **cuneiform** **dream** **manual**)
- **The** **Egyptian** **tradition** (**the** **Egyptian** **dream** **book** of **the** **Late** **Period**; **the** **Egyptian** **dream** **book** **is** **c.** **600** **BCE**; **the** **Egyptian** **dream** **book** **is** **the** **standard** **demotic** **dream** **manual**)
- **The** **Greek** **tradition** (**the** **Greek** **dream** **book** of **Artemidorus**; **the** **Greek** **dream** **book** **is** **c.** **170**-**200** **CE**; **the** **Greek** **dream** **book** **is** **the** **standard** **classical** **dream** **manual**)
- **The** **Jewish** **tradition** (**the** **Jewish** **dream** **interpretation** of **the** **Talmud**; **the** **Jewish** **dream** **interpretation** **is** **c.** **3rd**-**5th** **c.** **CE**; **the** **Jewish** **dream** **interpretation** **is** **the** **standard** **rabbinic** **dream** **manual**)
- **The** **Islamic** **tradition** (**the** **Islamic** **dream** **book** of **Ibn** **Sirin**; **the** **Islamic** **dream** **book** **is** **c.** **11th** **c.** **CE**; **the** **Islamic** **dream** **book** **is** **the** **standard** **medieval** **Arabic** **dream** **manual**)
- **The** **Christian** **tradition** (**the** **Christian** **dream** **interpretation** of **the** **Church** **Fathers**; **the** **Christian** **dream** **interpretation** **is** **c.** **4th**-**5th** **c.** **CE**; **the** **Christian** **dream** **interpretation** **is** **the** **standard** **patristic** **dream** **manual**)

The **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **and** **most** **universal** **form** of **divination**; the **oneiromancy** **is** **a** **real** **divination** **method**.

## The Real Origin — Sumerian

The **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **oldest** **form** of **the** **Mantic** **arts**; the **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Sumerian** **cuneiform** **tablets** of **the** **3rd** **millennium** **BCE**; the **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **oldest** **oneiromancy** **in** **the** **world**.

The **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **preserved** in **the** **specific** **cuneiform** **texts**:

- **The** **dream** **book** of **Gudea** (**the** **specific** **cuneiform** **text**; **the** **text** **is** **c.** **2100** **BCE**; **the** **text** **is** **the** **Sumerian** **dream** **book** of **Gudea**; **the** **text** **is** **the** **oldest** **dream** **book** in **the** **world**; **the** **text** **is** **published** by **the** **University** **of** **Pennsylvania** **Museum**)
- **The** **Assyrian** **dream** **book** (**the** **specific** **cuneiform** **text**; **the** **text** **is** **c.** **700** **BCE**; **the** **text** **is** **the** **Assyrian** **dream** **book** of **the** **Neo**-**Assyrian** **period**; **the** **text** **is** **the** **standard** **Assyrian** **dream** **manual**; **the** **text** **is** **published** by **Livingstone** in **1989**)

The **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **classifies** **dreams** **into** **specific** **types**:

- **Good** **dreams** (**the** **specific** **dreams**; **the** **dreams** **that** **predict** **good** **events**; **the** **dreams** **that** **are** **sent** **by** **the** **good** **gods**)
- **Bad** **dreams** (**the** **specific** **dreams**; **the** **dreams** **that** **predict** **bad** **events**; **the** **dreams** **that** **are** **sent** **by** **the** **evil** **gods**)
- **Message** **dreams** (**the** **specific** **dreams**; **the** **dreams** **that** **are** **sent** **by** **the** **specific** **gods**; **the** **dreams** **that** **are** **interpreted** **by** **the** **specific** **priests**)
- **Self**-**generated** **dreams** (**the** **specific** **dreams**; **the** **dreams** **that** **are** **generated** **by** **the** **dreamer**; **the** **dreams** **that** **reflect** **the** **state** of **the** **body**)

The **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **form** of **the** **Mantic** **arts**; the **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Assyrian** **oneiromancy**; the **Sumerian** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Babylonian** **oneiromancy**.

## The Real Greek Tradition — Artemidorus

The **Greek** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **standard** **classical** **oneiromancy**; the **Greek** **oneiromancy** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus**; the **Greek** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **oneiromancy**.

The **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus** (**the** **specific** **5**-**book** **treatise**; **the** **treatise** **is** **c.** **170**-**200** **CE**; **the** **treatise** **is** **the** **standard** **classical** **dream** **manual**; **the** **treatise** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Greek** **manuscripts** of **the** **2nd**-**15th** **c.** **CE**) **is** **the** **most** **important** **single** **text** of **the** **Western** **oneiromancy**.

The **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus** **classifies** **dreams** **into** **specific** **types**:

- **The** **enhypnion** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** that **is** **a** **direct** **message**; **the** **dream** that **is** **sent** **by** **a** **god**; **the** **dream** that **predicts** **the** **future**; **the** **enhypnion** **is** **the** **most** **important** **type** of **dream**)
- **The** **oneiros** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** that **is** **a** **symbolic** **message**; **the** **dream** that **is** **sent** **by** **a** **god**; **the** **dream** that **must** **be** **interpreted**; **the** **oneiros** **is** **the** **medium**-**important** **type** of **dream**)
- **The** **phantasma** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** that **is** **a** **self**-**generated** **fantasy**; **the** **dream** that **is** **not** **sent** **by** **a** **god**; **the** **dream** that **does** **not** **predict** **the** **future**; **the** **phantasma** **is** **the** **least**-**important** **type** of **dream**)

The **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus** **presents** **specific** **rules** of **the** **interpretation**:

- **The** **identity** of **the** **dreamer** (**the** **specific** **identity**; **the** **identity** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **identity** of **the** **dreamer** **is** **the** **specific** **identity** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **time** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **time**; **the** **time** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **time** of **the** **dream** **is** **the** **specific** **time** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **place** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **place**; **the** **place** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **place** of **the** **dream** **is** **the** **specific** **place** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **objects** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **objects**; **the** **objects** **affect** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **objects** of **the** **dream** **are** **the** **specific** **objects** **that** **affect** **the** **interpretation**)

The **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus** **is** **the** **most** **important** **single** **text** of **the** **Western** **oneiromancy**; the **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **medieval** **oneiromancy**; the **Oneirocritica** of **Artemidorus** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **oneiromancy**.

## The Real Islamic Tradition — Ibn Sirin

The **Islamic** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **standard** **medieval** **Arabic** **oneiromancy**; the **Islamic** **oneiromancy** **is** **preserved** in **the** ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** **of** **Ibn** **Sirin**; the **Islamic** **oneiromancy** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **medieval** **Arabic** **oneiromancy**.

The ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** of **Ibn** **Sirin** (**the** **specific** **Arabic** **text**; **the** **text** **is** **c.** **11th** **c.** **CE**; **the** **text** **is** **the** **standard** **medieval** **Arabic** **dream** **manual**; **the** **text** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Arabic** **manuscripts** of **the** **11th**-**19th** **c.** **CE**) **is** **the** **most** **important** **single** **text** of **the** **Islamic** **oneiromancy**.

The ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** of **Ibn** **Sirin** **classifies** **dreams** **into** **specific** **types**:

- **The** ***ru'ya*** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** **that** **is** **a** **direct** **message** **from** **Allah**; **the** **dream** **that** **predicts** **the** **future**; **the** ***ru'ya*** **is** **the** **most** **important** **type** of **dream**; **the** ***ru'ya*** **is** **the** **specific** **type** of **dream** **that** **is** **mentioned** **in** **the** **Quran**)
- **The** ***hulm*** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** **that** **is** **a** **symbolic** **message** **from** **the** **angels** or **the** **jinn**; **the** **dream** **that** **must** **be** **interpreted**; **the** ***hulm*** **is** **the** **medium**-**important** **type** of **dream**; **the** ***hulm*** **is** **the** **specific** **type** of **dream** **that** **is** **most** **common**)
- **The** ***adghas*** **or** ***aqdar*** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** **that** **is** **a** **self**-**generated** **fantasy**; **the** **dream** **that** **is** **not** **sent** **by** **Allah**; **the** **dream** that **does** **not** **predict** **the** **future**; **the** ***adghas*** **is** **the** **least**-**important** **type** of **dream**)

The ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** of **Ibn** **Sirin** **presents** **specific** **rules** of **the** **interpretation**:

- **The** **identity** of **the** **dreamer** (**the** **specific** **identity**; **the** **identity** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **identity** of **the** **dreamer** **is** **the** **specific** **identity** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **time** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **time**; **the** **time** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **time** of **the** **dream** **is** **the** **specific** **time** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **place** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **place**; **the** **place** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **place** of **the** **dream** **is** **the** **specific** **place** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **objects** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **objects**; **the** **objects** **affect** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **objects** of **the** **dream** **are** **the** **specific** **objects** **that** **affect** **the** **interpretation**)

The ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** of **Ibn** **Sirin** **is** **the** **most** **important** **single** **text** of **the** **Islamic** **oneiromancy**; the ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** of **Ibn** **Sirin** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **medieval** **Arabic** **oneiromancy**; the ***Tabir*** **al**-***Ruya*** of **Ibn** **Sirin** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **Arabic** **oneiromancy**.

- **The** **prophetic** **dream** (**the** **specific** **dream**; **the** **dream** **that** **is** **a** **direct** **message** **from** **God**; **the** **dream** **that** **is** **a** **prophecy**; **the** **prophetic** **dream** **is** **the** **rarest** **type** of **dream**; **the** **prophetic** **dream** **is** **mentioned** in **the** **Talmud** as **"**the** **prophetic** **dream**"**)

The **Talmud** **Berakhot** **55a**-**60b** **presents** **specific** **rules** of **the** **interpretation**:

- **The** **identity** of **the** **dreamer** (**the** **specific** **identity**; **the** **identity** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **identity** of **the** **dreamer** **is** **the** **specific** **identity** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
- **The** **time** of **the** **dream** (**the** **specific** **time**; **the** **time** **affects** **the** **interpretation**; **the** **time** of **the** **dream** **is** **the** **specific** **time** **that** **affects** **the** **interpretation**)
