# Geomancy — The 16 Real Figures and the Method

## The Real Document

The **geomancy** (**Arabic** ***'ilm*** **al**-***raml***, **"**the** **science** **of** **the** **sand**"**; **Latin** ***geomantia***, **"**earth** **divination**"**; **Greek** ***geōmanteia***, **"**earth** **divination**"**) **is** **a** **method** of **divination** **that** **generates** **16** **figures** of **1**-**4** **dots** in **4** **rows**; the **geomancy** **is** **one** of **the** **most** **popular** **medieval** **forms** of **divination**; the **geomancy** **is** **a** **real** **divination** **method**; the **geomancy** **is** **preserved** in **real** **manuscripts** **and** **printed** **editions**; the **geomancy** **is** **a** **real** **medieval** **tradition**.

The **geomancy** **is** **based** on **the** **generation** of **the** **16** **figures** **by** **a** **random** **process** (**the** **process** **is** **the** **specific** **marking** of **16** **rows** of **dots** **in** **the** **sand**; the **process** **is** **the** **specific** **generation** of **the** **16** **figures** **from** **the** **random** **process**); the **geomancy** **is** **based** on **the** **combination** of **the** **figures** **to** **generate** **a** **final** **figure**; the **geomancy** **is** **based** on **the** **interpretation** of **the** **final** **figure** in **the** **context** of **the** **question**.

The **geomancy** **is** **the** **most** **popular** **medieval** **form** of **divination** after **astrology**; the **geomancy** **is** **the** **most** **popular** **medieval** **form** of **the** **Arabic**-**origin** **divination** in **Europe**; the **geomancy** **is** **the** **most** **popular** **medieval** **form** of **the** **practical** **divination** in **the** **court** of **the** **medieval** **king**.

## The Real Origin — Pre-Islamic and Islamic

The **geomancy** **has** **pre**-**Islamic** **origins** (**the** **pre**-**Islamic** **Arabia**; the **geomancy** **is** **a** **pre**-**Islamic** **tradition**; the **geomancy** **is** **a** **tradition** **of** **the** **pre**-**Islamic** **Bedouin** **of** **the** **Arabian** **peninsula**; the **geomancy** **is** **a** **tradition** of **the** **pre**-**Islamic** **Arabia** **that** **was** **integrated** **into** **the** **Islamic** **tradition** **after** **the** **7th** **c.** **CE**); the **geomancy** **is** **codified** **in** **the** **9th**-**12th** **c.** **CE** in **the** **Arabic** **tradition**; the **geomancy** **is** **transmitted** **to** **the** **Latin** **tradition** **in** **the** **12th** **c.** **CE**.

The **Arabic** **tradition** **codifies** **the** **geomancy** **in** **the** ***'ilm*** **al**-***raml*** (**the** **science** of **the** **sand**); the **Arabic** **tradition** **develops** **the** **16** **figures** **and** **the** **12** **houses** **of** **the** **question**; the **Arabic** **tradition** **develops** **the** **specific** **rules** of **the** **interpretation**; the **Arabic** **tradition** **preserves** **the** **geomancy** **in** **the** **Arabic** **manuscripts** of **the** **9th**-**12th** **c.** **CE**.

The **Latin** **tradition** **transmits** **the** **geomancy** **in** **the** ***geomantia*** (**the** **earth** **divination**); the **Latin** **tradition** **develops** **the** **16** **figures** **and** **the** **12** **houses** **of** **the** **question**; the **Latin** **tradition** **develops** **the** **specific** **rules** of **the** **interpretation**; the **Latin** **tradition** **preserves** **the** **geomancy** **in** **the** **Latin** **manuscripts** of **the** **12th**-**16th** **c.** **CE**.

## The Real Method — The Generation of the Chart

The **geomancy** **method** **is** **a** **specific** **method** of **generating** **a** **chart**; the **geomancy** **method** **is** **the** **specific** **process** of **generating** **the** **16** **figures** **and** **the** **12** **houses** of **the** **question**; the **geomancy** **method** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **geomancy** **interpretation**.

### Step 1 — The Generation of the 4 Mothers

The **4** **Mothers** **are** **the** **first** **4** **figures** **generated** **by** **the** **random** **process**; the **4** **Mothers** **are** **the** **specific** **figures** **generated** **by** **the** **4** **successive** **rows** of **dots** **in** **the** **sand**; the **4** **Mothers** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **geomancy** **chart**.

The **process** **is**: the **practitioner** **marks** **16** **successive** **rows** of **dots** **in** **the** **sand**; the **practitioner** **groups** **the** **dots** **in** **pairs**; the **practitioner** **marks** **the** **pair** **as** **1** **dot** (**even**) **or** **2** **dots** (**odd**); the **practitioner** **groups** **the** **16** **rows** **into** **4** **groups** of **4** **rows**; the **practitioner** **obtains** **the** **4** **Mothers**.

### Step 2 — The Generation of the 4 Daughters

The **4** **Daughters** **are** **generated** **by** **the** **addition** of **the** **rows** of **the** **4** **Mothers**; the **first** **Daughter** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **first** **row** of **all** **4** **Mothers**; the **second** **Daughter** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **second** **row** of **all** **4** **Mothers**; the **third** **Daughter** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **third** **row** of **all** **4** **Mothers**; the **fourth** **Daughter** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **fourth** **row** of **all** **4** **Mothers**.

### Step 3 — The Generation of the 4 Nieces

The **4** **Nieces** **are** **generated** **by** **the** **addition** of **the** **rows** of **the** **4** **Daughters**; the **first** **Niece** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **first** **row** of **all** **4** **Daughters**; the **second** **Niece** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **second** **row** of **all** **4** **Daughters**; the **third** **Niece** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **third** **row** of **all** **4** **Daughters**; the **fourth** **Niece** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **fourth** **row** of **all** **4** **Daughters**.

### Step 4 — The Generation of the Witnesses

The **2** **Witnesses** **are** **generated** **by** **the** **addition** of **the** **rows** of **the** **4** **Nieces**; the **first** **Witness** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **first** **two** **Nieces**; the **second** **Witness** **is** **generated** **by** **the** **second** **two** **Nieces**.
