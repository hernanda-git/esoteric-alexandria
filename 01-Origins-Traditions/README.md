## Deep-Dive Paths

- **Meta layer** — the esoteric/exoteric decision is itself an initiation marker; the seven liberal arts are the *techne* into the *theoria* boundary.
- **Comparative layer** — initiation rites across the master-disciple chain reveal the hidden subjects the cultures cannot articulate directly.
- **Method layer** — doc 08 formalizes how esotericists actually build cross-tradition bridges without collapsing specificity.
- **Source layer** — the isnad chain is not just historical tooling; it's the teleological structure of the transmission itself.
