# Open Questions — The Unresolved Mysteries

## The Honest Note

The esoteric tradition is **2,500 years old**. The modern scientific tradition is **500 years old**. The two traditions have produced **mountains** of claims, **volumes** of texts, and **centuries** of practice. The two traditions have **not** produced a **single, complete, agreed** answer to the deepest questions.

This document is the **honest catalogue** of the **unresolved** questions. The questions are **not** shameful; the questions are **the frontier**. The questions are the **research directions** for the next generation of the tradition.

The 20 open questions are organized by **tradition area**. The 20 questions are the **most important** open questions; there are **many** more, but these 20 are the **frontier**.

## The 20 Open Questions

### 1. The Efficacy of the Planetary Hours — Verified or Placebo?

**The tradition**: the planetary hours are the **operating system** of the talismanic tradition. The hours have been **continuously practiced for 1,500 years** by serious practitioners who report **consistent** results.

**The question**: are the planetary hours **objectively** effective, or are they **placebo** (or **expectation effect**)? Modern astrology researchers have **never** run a **rigorous, blinded, controlled** test of the planetary hours.

**The research direction**: a **double-blind, randomized, controlled trial** of the planetary hours. The study would compare the **outcomes** of actions taken at the **favorable** planetary hours vs. the **unfavorable** planetary hours, with the **practitioners blinded** to the hour.

### 2. The Reality of the Jinn — Phenomenon or Symbol?

**The tradition**: the jinn are **real, intelligent, non-human beings** who interact with humans. The jinn are mentioned in the **Quran** (Sura 72) and the **Hadith**. The jinn are **central** to the 'ilm al-huruf tradition.

**The question**: are the jinn **real** entities, or are they **psychological symbols** of the shadow, the autonomous complex, the sub-personal pattern? The modern psychology (Jung) and the modern cognitive science have **similar** concepts (the autonomous complex, the sub-personal pattern) that **might** correspond to the jinn.

**The research direction**: a **comparative study** of the jinn reports (in the Islamic literature) and the **autonomous complex** reports (in the Jungian literature). The study would compare the **phenomenology**, the **behavior**, the **effects** of the two.

### 3. The Mandala as Information — Is the Mandala a Knowledge Graph?

**The tradition**: the mandala is the **visual representation** of the deity, the cosmos, the Self. The mandala is a **symbol** that **encodes** information in a **geometric** form.

**The question**: is the mandala a **knowledge graph**? Is the mandala a **visual** encoding of a **relational structure** that **could be** (and perhaps **should be**) represented as a **graph** in a **modern** knowledge-management system?

**The research direction**: a **formal** representation of the mandala as a **graph** in a **graph database** (Neo4j, etc.). The study would compare the **information** encoded in the mandala with the **information** encoded in the modern **knowledge graph** and ask whether the two are **isomorphic**.

### 4. The Magic Square as Cipher — Is the Wafq a Cryptographic Protocol?

**The question**: is the *wafq* a **cryptographic protocol**? Is the *wafq* a **visual** representation of a **numerical** protocol that **could be** (and perhaps **should be**) implemented as a **modern** cryptographic protocol?

### 5. The I Ching as Information System — Is the I Ching a Generative AI?

**The question**: is the I Ching a **generative AI**? The I Ching **generates** a **new** state (the secondary hexagram) from the **current** state (the primary hexagram) and the **changing lines**. The I Ching is a **transformation** of the **input** (the question, the coins) into the **output** (the judgment, the line texts).

The next phase is **not** the **end** of the esoteric tradition. The next phase is the **next chapter**. The esoteric tradition has been **continuously** evolved for 2,500 years. The next chapter is **another** evolution.

The library is the **map**. The frontier is the **territory**. The questions are the **passage**.
