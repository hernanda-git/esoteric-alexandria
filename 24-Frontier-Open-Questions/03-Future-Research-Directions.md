# Future Research Directions

## The Next Decade (2026-2036)

The Esoteric Alexandria library is a **snapshot** of the 21st-century esoteric tradition. The next decade will see **dramatic** changes in the field. The 10 research directions are the **most promising** for the next 10 years.

The 10 directions are **not** independent; the 10 directions are **interconnected**. The 10 directions form a **single research program** that **integrates** the esoteric tradition with the modern science and the modern technology.

## The 10 Directions

### 1. The Computational Modeling of the Esoteric Knowledge Graph

**The opportunity**: the esoteric tradition is a **knowledge graph** (the 24 categories, the 60+ documents, the hundreds of cross-links). The knowledge graph can be **modeled** as a **modern** graph database (Neo4j, etc.) and **analyzed** with the modern **graph algorithms** (the PageRank, the community detection, the link prediction).

**The research direction**:

- **Convert** the library to a **graph database**
- **Apply** the modern **graph algorithms** to the **esoteric knowledge graph**
- **Discover** the **hidden** structures (the **communities** of concepts, the **bridges** between traditions, the **central** concepts)
- **Use** the **link prediction** to **suggest** the **new** cross-links (the next docs to write)
- **Build** the **API** of the **esoteric tradition** for the **modern** consumption (the chat agents, the search systems, the knowledge management tools)

**The expected impact**: a **computational** understanding of the **esoteric tradition** that is **complementary** to the **textual** understanding. The **two** together would give a **complete** picture of the tradition.

### 2. The Empirical Testing of the Planetary Hours

**The opportunity**: the planetary hours are the **operating system** of the talismanic tradition. The planetary hours have been **continuously** practiced for **1,500 years**. The planetary hours have **never** been **rigorously** tested.

**The research direction**:

- **Design** a **double-blind, randomized, controlled trial** of the planetary hours
- **Recruit** a **large** sample of **practitioners** (and **non**-practitioners as a control)
- **Randomly** assign the **actions** to the **planetary hours** (the favorable hours vs. the unfavorable hours)
- **Measure** the **outcomes** (the success of the action, the satisfaction of the practitioner, the objective measures)
- **Analyze** the **results** with the **modern** statistical methods
- **Publish** the **results** in a **peer-reviewed** journal

**The expected impact**: a **rigorous** answer to the question of whether the planetary hours are **objectively** effective. The answer is **either** "yes, the planetary hours are objectively effective" (which would be a **major** finding) or "no, the planetary hours are not objectively effective" (which would be a **major** finding for the practitioners).

### 3. The Cross-Tradition Concordance Database

**The opportunity**: the cross-tradition concordance is the **most powerful** evidence for the **perennial philosophy**. The concordance is the **bridge** between the traditions. The concordance can be **formalized** as a **database** of the **same** concept in the **different** traditions.

**The research direction**:

- **Build** a **database** of the **cross-tradition concordance** (the 30 major concordances, with **all** the **named** concepts in the **all** the **named** traditions)
- **Link** the **concepts** to the **primary** sources (the **texts**, the **terms**, the **rituals**)
- **Apply** the **modern** NLP to **discover** the **hidden** concordances (the **un-named** concepts that **are** the **same** in the **different** traditions)
- **Publish** the **database** as an **open-source** project
- **Use** the **database** to **train** an **LLM** on the **esoteric tradition**

**The expected impact**: a **computational** version of the **perennial philosophy**. The database would be the **first** systematic attempt to **map** the **esoteric tradition** in a **machine-readable** format.

### 4. The Neuroimaging of the Meditative States

**The opportunity**: the meditative states (the *samadhi* of the Hindu, the *jhana* of the Buddhist, the *fana'* of the Sufi, the *unio mystica* of the Christian) are the **first-person data** of the esoteric tradition. The meditative states can be **studied** with the **modern** neuroimaging (fMRI, EEG, MEG, fNIRS).

**The research direction**:

- **Recruit** experienced **practitioners** of the **different** meditative traditions
- **Measure** the **brain activity** during the **meditative states** (the *samadhi*, the *jhana*, the *fana'*)
- **Compare** the **brain activity** across the **traditions** (the commonalities, the differences)
- **Correlate** the **brain activity** with the **first-person** reports (the phenomenology)
- **Publish** the **results** in a **peer-reviewed** journal

**The expected impact**: a **neuroscience** of the meditative states. The study would be the **first** systematic attempt to **map** the **first-person** data of the esoteric tradition onto the **third-person** data of the modern neuroscience.

### 5. The Psychedelic Renaissance and the Esoteric Tradition

**The opportunity**: the modern **psychedelic renaissance** (the **psilocybin** trials at Johns Hopkins, Imperial College, NYU; the **MDMA** trials at MAPS; the **ayahuasca** and **ibogaine** research) is the **most promising** modern research direction for the **integration** of the esoteric tradition with the modern science. The **psychedelic** experience is the **most direct** modern access to the **esoteric** states.

**The research direction**:

- **Study** the **psychedelic** experience in the **context** of the **esoteric tradition** (the **set and setting** of the **ceremonial** tradition, the **integration** of the **esoteric** framework, the **long-term** outcomes)
- **Compare** the **psychedelic** experience with the **meditative** experience (the commonalities, the differences)
- **Develop** a **clinical** protocol that **integrates** the **esoteric** framework with the **psychedelic** therapy
- **Train** the **therapists** in the **esoteric** framework (the **esoteric** is the **most sophisticated** framework for the **integration** of the **psychedelic** experience)
- **Publish** the **results** in a **peer-reviewed** journal

**The expected impact**: a **clinical** protocol that **integrates** the **psychedelic** therapy with the **esoteric** framework. The protocol would be the **first** modern **clinical** application of the esoteric tradition.

### 6. The AI-Augmented Esoteric Practice

**The opportunity**: the modern **AI** (the **LLM**, the **agent**, the **RAG** system) is the **most powerful** tool for the **augmentation** of the esoteric practice. The AI can **answer** the **questions**, **generate** the **rituals**, **simulate** the **meditation**, and **integrate** the **knowledge**.

**The research direction**:

- **Build** an **AI agent** that **acts** as the **esoteric practitioner** (the **.palm-reader.ai** of the esoteric tradition)
- **Train** the **agent** on the **esoteric** corpus (the library, the source texts, the modern academic literature)
- **Deploy** the **agent** in the **practice** (the **agent** acts as the **oracle**, the **advisor**, the **companion**)
- **Study** the **outcomes** (the user satisfaction, the practice depth, the long-term effects)
- **Publish** the **results** in a **peer-reviewed** journal

**The expected impact**: the **first** modern **AI-augmented esoteric practice**. The agent would be the **first** systematic attempt to **integrate** the **esoteric tradition** with the **modern AI**.

### 7. The Cypher Library of the Esoteric Tradition

**The opportunity**: the esoteric tradition is a **library** of **texts** (the **Shams al-Ma'arif**, the **Picatrix**, the **Agrippa**, the **Zohar**, the **I Ching**, the **Tao Te Ching**, etc.). The library can be **digitalized**, **analyzed**, and **made searchable** with the **modern** tools.

**The research direction**:

- **Digitalize** the **entire** esoteric corpus (the source texts, the modern academic literature, the practitioner's journals)
- **Build** a **searchable** database of the **esoteric** corpus
- **Apply** the **modern** NLP to **discover** the **hidden** patterns (the **recurring** concepts, the **un-named** structures, the **latent** knowledge)
- **Publish** the **database** as an **open-source** project
- **Use** the **database** to **train** a **specialized** LLM on the **esoteric tradition**

**The expected impact**: the **first** modern **searchable** database of the **esoteric tradition**. The database would be the **foundation** of all the **other** research directions.

### 8. The Modern Grimoire of the Esoteric Tradition

**The opportunity**: the **grimoires** (the **Goetia**, the **Sworn Book**, the **Picatrix**, the **Book of Abramelin**) are the **practical manuals** of the esoteric tradition. The grimoires can be **modernized** for the **21st-century** practitioner.

**The research direction**:

- **Modernize** the **grimoires** (the **translation** of the **medieval** language to the **modern** language, the **re-formatting** for the **modern** medium, the **re-validation** of the **practices**)
- **Test** the **modernized** grimoires in the **practitioner** community
- **Document** the **outcomes** in the **practitioner's journal** (the empirical tradition)
- **Publish** the **modernized** grimoires as a **modern** open-source project
- **Build** the **community** of the **modern** practitioners

**The expected impact**: a **modern** grimoire that is **accessible** to the **21st-century** practitioner. The grimoire would be the **first** modern attempt to **systematize** the **practical** esoteric tradition.

### 9. The Embodied Practice of the Esoteric Tradition

**The opportunity**: the esoteric tradition is **embodied** (the **ritual**, the **meditation**, the **practice**). The embodied practice is the **most important** of the **research** directions. The embodied practice is the **only** way to **verify** the **empirical** claims of the tradition.

**The research direction**:

- **Establish** a **modern** retreat center (the **esoteric** retreat center, the **modern** monastic)
- **Develop** the **embodied** practice (the **ritual**, the **meditation**, the **practice**)
- **Document** the **outcomes** in the **practitioner's journal**
- **Study** the **embodied** practice with the **modern** neuroscience (the **neuroimaging** of the **practitioner** during the **ritual**)
- **Publish** the **outcomes** in a **peer-reviewed** journal

**The expected impact**: a **modern** retreat center that is the **embodied** expression of the esoteric tradition. The center would be the **first** modern attempt to **integrate** the **embodied** tradition with the **modern** research.

### 10. The Educational Curriculum of the Esoteric Tradition

**The opportunity**: the esoteric tradition is **educational** (the **transmission** from the **master** to the **disciple**, the **curriculum** of the **tradition**). The educational curriculum can be **formalized** as a **modern** educational program (the **12-month curriculum** of the library is the **first** attempt).

**The research direction**:

- **Develop** a **modern** educational program of the esoteric tradition (the **3-month foundations**, the **12-month practitioner**, the **2-year deep-dive**)
- **Establish** the **modern** lineage (the **modern** masters, the **modern** communities, the **modern** schools)
- **Build** the **modern** community (the **online** community, the **local** groups, the **retreat** centers)
- **Certify** the **modern** practitioners (the **modern** credentials, the **modern** degrees)
- **Publish** the **curriculum** as a **modern** open-source project

**The expected impact**: a **modern** educational program that is the **first** systematic attempt to **transmit** the esoteric tradition in a **modern** educational format. The program would be the **foundation** of the **modern** lineage.

## The Integration

The 10 research directions are **not** independent. The 10 directions are **interconnected**. The 10 directions form a **single research program** that **integrates** the **esoteric** tradition with the **modern** science and the **modern** technology.

The 10 directions are **not** a **plan**. The 10 directions are **opportunities**. The opportunities are **open** to the **practitioners**, the **researchers**, the **builders**, the **scholars**, the **curious**.

The library is the **map**. The 10 directions are the **routes**. The **next decade** is the **journey**.
