# Synchronicity — The Acausal Connecting Principle

## The Real Essay

**Synchronicity** **is** **a** **specific** **Jungian** **concept**; **synchronicity** **is** **defined** by **Jung** as **"**a** **meaningful** **coincidence** **of** **two** **or** **more** **events** **where** **there** **is** **no** **causal** **connection** **between** **them**"**; **synchronicity** **is** **the** **specific** **acausal** **counterpart** to **the** **causal** **principle** of **ordinary** **physics**; **synchronicity** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **Jungian** **account** of **meaningful** **chance**.

The **essay** **is** **a** **real** **text**; the **essay** **is** **published** in **1952**; the **essay** **is** **co-authored** in **spirit** with **Wolfgang** **Pauli** (**the** **quantum** **physicist** and **Nobel** **laureate**); the **essay** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Collected** **Works** (volume 8). The **essay** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **essay** **is** **a** **document**.

## The Real Doctrine — Meaningful Coincidence

### The Three Criteria

**Jung** **presents** **the** **specific** **criteria** of **a** **synchronicity**:

- **A** **meaningful** **parallel** between **an** **inner** **psychic** **state** and **an** **outer** **event** that **cannot** be **causally** **linked**
- **The** **temporal** **coincidence** of **the** **two** **events** (they occur **together** or **in** **near** **sequence**)
- **The** **absence** of **any** **causal** **chain** that **could** **explain** the **coincidence** by **ordinary** **physics**

### The Fourfold Typology

**Jung** **presents** **the** **specific** **fourfold** **typology** of **synchronicity**: (1) **a** **psychic** **state** **coincides** with **an** **outer** **event**; (2) **an** **outer** **event** **coincides** with **a** **later** **psychic** **state**; (3) **an** **outer** **event** **coincides** with **another** **outer** **event**; (4) **a** **meaningful** **dream** **anticipates** an **outer** **event**.

### The Archetypal Basis and the Unus Mundus

**Synchronicity** **rests** **on** **the** **archetype** as **the** **organizing** **factor** of **the** **collective** **unconscious**; **synchronicity** **presupposes** the **unus** **mundus** (**the** **one** **world**) — **the** **underlying** **unity** of **psyche** and **matter** **beneath** the **split** of **subject** and **object**; **synchronicity** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **dialogue** between **depth** **psychology** and **quantum** **physics** (the **Pauli** **connection**, **the** **modern** **implicate** **order**).

## The Real Note

**Synchronicity** **is** **a** **real** **Jungian** **concept**; **synchronicity** **is** **preserved** in **real** **texts**; **synchronicity** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **bridge** between **psychology** and **physics**. **Synchronicity** **is** **a** **useful** **resource** for **the** **student** of **depth** **psychology**, **the** **modern** **trading** **archetype** of **meaningful** **signal**, **and** **the** **modern** **AI** **and** **resonance** **traditions**.
