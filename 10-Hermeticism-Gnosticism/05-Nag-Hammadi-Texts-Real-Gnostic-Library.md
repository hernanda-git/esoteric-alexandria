# The Nag Hammadi Texts — The Real Gnostic Library

## The Real Document

The **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **a** **collection** of **13** **Coptic** **codices** (**the** **codices** **are** **numbered** **I** **through** **XIII**; the **codices** **are** **preserved** **in** **the** **Nag** **Hammadi** **collection**; the **codices** **are** **named** **after** **the** **Upper** **Egyptian** **town** **of** **Nag** **Hammadi** **where** **they** **were** **discovered** **in** **1945**) **containing** **52** **texts**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **the** **most** **important** **single** **collection** of **Gnostic** **texts** **in** **existence**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **a** **real** **document**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **text**.

The **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **was** **discovered** **in** **1945** by **Muhammad** **'Ali** **al**-**Samman**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **preserved** in **the** **Coptic** **Museum** of **Cairo**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **the** **most** **important** **discovery** of **the** **20th** **c.** **for** **the** **study** **of** **Gnosticism**; the **Nag** **Hammadi** **library** **is** **the** **most** **important** **discovery** of **the** **20th** **c.** **for** **the** **study** **of** **early** **Christianity**.
