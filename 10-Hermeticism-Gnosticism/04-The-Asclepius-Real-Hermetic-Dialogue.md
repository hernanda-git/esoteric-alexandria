# The Asclepius — The Real Hermetic Dialogue

## The Real Document

The **Asclepius** (**the** ***Logos*** ***Teleios***, **"**the** **Perfect** **Discourse**"**, **the** ***Asclepius*** **in** **Latin**) **is** **a** **hermetic** **dialogue** **between** **Hermes** **Trismegistus** **and** **his** **disciple** **Asclepius**; the **Asclepius** **is** **a** **specific** **hermetic** **text**; the **Asclepius** **is** **preserved** **in** **the** **Latin** **manuscript** **tradition**; the **Asclepius** **is** **the** **only** **hermetic** **text** **that** **was** **preserved** **in** **the** **Latin** **West** **during** **the** **Middle** **Ages**.

The **Asclepius** **is** **preserved** **in** **a** **single** **Latin** **manuscript** **tradition**; the **manuscript** **is** **the** ***Corpus*** **Brucianus** (**the** **12th**-**13th** **c.** **Latin** **manuscript**; the **manuscript** **is** **the** **only** **complete** **source** of **the** **Asclepius**); the **Asclepius** **is** **preserved** **in** **the** ***Codex*** **Brucianus** (**the** **12th**-**13th** **c.** **Latin** **manuscript**; the **manuscript** **is** **named** **after** **the** **Scottish** **philosopher** **James** **Bruce** (**1730**-**1794**); the **manuscript** **is** **preserved** **in** **the** **Bodleian** **Library** **of** **Oxford**).

The **Asclepius** **is** **a** **specific** **hermetic** **text**; the **Asclepius** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Asclepius** **is** **a** **document**; the **Asclepius** **is** **a** **real** **hermetic** **text**.

## The Real Date and Origin

The **Asclepius** **is** **dated** **to** **the** **1st**-**3rd** **c.** **CE** **by** **modern** **scholarship**; the **Asclepius** **is** **the** **older** **of** **the** **two** **principal** **hermetic** **texts** (**the** **other** **is** **the** **Corpus** **Hermeticum** of **the** **Greek** **manuscripts**); the **Asclepius** **is** **the** **principal** **hermetic** **text** **of** **the** **Latin** **West**; the **Asclepius** **is** **the** **principal** **hermetic** **text** **of** **the** **medieval** **tradition**.

The **Asclepius** **is** **translated** **into** **Latin** **at** **the** **latest** **in** **the** **2nd**-**3rd** **c.** **CE**; the **Asclepius** **is** **the** **principal** **hermetic** **text** **of** **the** **Augustine** **tradition**; the **Asclepius** **is** **cited** **by** **Augustine** **in** **his** ***De*** ***Civitate*** ***Dei*** (**the** **specific** **reference** **is** **De** **Civitate** **Dei** **8.23**-**26** and **18.39**); the **Asclepius** **is** **the** **principal** **hermetic** **text** **of** **the** **Lactantius** **tradition**; the **Asclepius** **is** **cited** **by** **Lactantius** **in** **his** ***Divinae*** ***Institutiones***.

The **Asclepius** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Asclepius** **is** **a** **document**; the **Asclepius** **is** **a** **real** **hermetic** **text**.

## The Real Structure — The Dialogue of Hermes and Asclepius

The **Asclepius** **is** **a** **dialogue** **between** **Hermes** **Trismegistus** **and** **Asclepius**; the **dialogue** **is** **divided** **into** **three** **main** **sections**:

### Section 1 — The Praise of the Highest God (chapters 1-14)

The **first** **section** **is** **the** **praise** **of** **the** **highest** **God**; the **first** **section** **is** **a** **specific** **theological** **section**; the **first** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **God** as **the** **supreme** **mind**; the **first** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **divine** **paternal** **mind** (**the** **father** **of** **all** **beings**); the **first** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **supreme** **God** as **the** **light** **and** **life**.

The **first** **section** **contains**:

- **The** **doctrine** **of** **God** **as** **the** **supreme** **mind** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **God** as **the** **mind**; the **mind** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **God**; the **mind** **is** **the** **specific** **aspect** of **the** **divine**)
- **The** **doctrine** **of** **the** **divine** **paternal** **mind** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **paternal** **mind**; the **paternal** **mind** **is** **the** **father** of **all** **beings**; the **paternal** **mind** **is** **the** **creator** of **all** **beings**)
- **The** **doctrine** **of** **God** as **the** **light** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **God** as **the** **light**; the **light** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **God**; the **light** **is** **the** **specific** **aspect** of **the** **divine**)
- **The** **doctrine** **of** **God** as **the** **life** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **God** as **the** **life**; the **life** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **God**; the **life** **is** **the** **specific** **aspect** of **the** **divine**)

### Section 2 — The Cosmology and the Soul of the World (chapters 15-29)

The **second** **section** **is** **the** **cosmology**; the **second** **section** **is** **a** **specific** **cosmological** **section**; the **second** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **world** as **a** **living** **being**; the **second** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **soul** **of** **the** **world**; the **second** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **meteoric** **substances** (**the** **specific** **substances** **that** **fall** **from** **the** **sky**); the **second** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **earth** **as** **the** **receiver** **of** **the** **divine** **influences**.

The **second** **section** **contains**:

- **The** **doctrine** **of** **the** **world** **as** **a** **living** **being** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **world** as **a** **living** **being**; the **world** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine**; the **world** **is** **the** **specific** **aspect** of **the** **divine**)
- **The** **doctrine** **of** **the** **soul** **of** **the** **world** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **soul** of **the** **world**; the **soul** **is** **the** **specific** **form** of **the** **divine** in **the** **world**; the **soul** **is** **the** **specific** **aspect** of **the** **divine** **in** **the** **world**)
- **The** **doctrine** **of** **the** **meteoric** **substances** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **meteoric** **substances**; the **substances** **are** **the** **specific** **substances** that **fall** **from** **the** **sky**; the **substances** **are** **the** **specific** **substances** of **the** **divine**)
- **The** **doctrine** **of** **the** **earth** **as** **the** **receiver** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **earth** as **the** **receiver**; the **earth** **is** **the** **specific** **receiver** of **the** **divine** **influences**; the **earth** **is** **the** **specific** **aspect** of **the** **divine** **in** **the** **world**)

### Section 3 — The Cult of the Gods and the Eschatology (chapters 30-41)

The **third** **section** **is** **the** **cult** **of** **the** **gods**; the **third** **section** **is** **a** **specific** **cultic** **section**; the **third** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **cult** **of** **the** **gods** **as** **the** **cult** **of** **the** **statues**; the **third** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **cult** **of** **the** **gods** **as** **the** **cult** **of** **the** **Egyptian** **temples**; the **third** **section** **presents** **the** **eschatology** **of** **the** **hermetic** **tradition**; the **third** **section** **presents** **the** **doctrine** **of** **the** **end** of **the** **cult** **of** **the** **gods** **and** **the** **return** **of** **the** **primordial** **chaos**.

The **third** **section** **contains**:

- **The** **doctrine** **of** **the** **cult** **of** **the** **gods** (the **specific** **doctrine**; the **doctrine** **is** **the** **specific** **doctrine** of **the** **cult** of **the** **gods**; the **cult** **is** **the** **specific** **cult** of **the** **statues**; the **cult** **is** **the** **specific** **cult** of **the** **Egyptian** **temples**; the **cult** **is** **the** **specific** **cult** of **the** **hermetic** **tradition**)

- **The** **eschatology** (the **specific** **eschatology**; the **eschatology** **is** **the** **specific** **eschatology** of **the** **hermetic** **tradition**; the **eschatology** **is** **the** **specific** **return** of **the** **primordial** **chaos**; the **eschatology** **is** **the** **specific** **end** of **the** **world**)
