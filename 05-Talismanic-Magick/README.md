## Deep-Dive Paths

- **Structure layer** — the 7-component talismanic plan is the most important operational blueprint in the Western magical syllabus: material/image/inscription/number/planet/time/incense.
- **Ritual layer** — consecration and suffumigation describe the exact temporal geometry (outer and inner time) of the working.
- **Solomonic layer** — the Pentacle of Solomon is the passive twin of the active planetary seal; study alongside Goetia and the Lemegeton.
- **Lunar layer** — the 28 mansions are independent from the 12 zodiac houses; they are the Arabic-astrological compression of lunar phase work.
