# The Plan of the Talisman — The Real Structure

The **talisman** (**Greek** **τέλεσμα**, **"**something** **consecrated**"**) **is** **a** **specific** **magical** **object** that **operates** **through** **the** **planetary** **forces**; the **talisman** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **magical** **object**; the **talisman** **is** **distinguished** **from** **the** **amulet** (the **amulet** **is** **a** **magical** **object** that **protects**; the **talisman** **is** **a** **magical** **object** that **acts**; the **amulet** **is** **defensive**; the **talisman** **is** **active**).

The **talisman** **is** **the** **central** **form** of **the** **Western** **planetary** **magick**; the **talisman** **is** **the** **most** **common** **form** of **the** **Western** **magical** **object**; the **talisman** **is** **the** **form** of **the** **magical** **object** that **is** **most** **described** in **the** **Western** **grimoire** **tradition**.
