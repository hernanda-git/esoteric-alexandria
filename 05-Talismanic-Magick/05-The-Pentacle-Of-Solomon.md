# The Pentacle of Solomon — The Real Magical Seal

## The Real Document

The **Pentacle** of **Solomon** (**also** called the **Seal** of **Solomon**, the **Sigillum** **Solomonis**, the **Magen** **David** in **its** **hexagram** **form**, the **Seal** of **the** **Divine** **Name**) **is** **a** **specific** **Solomonic** **magical** **device**; the **Pentacle** of **Solomon** **is** **a** **magical** **diagram** **inscribed** **with** **the** **planetary** **names**, **the** **angelic** **names**, **the** **divine** **names**, and **the** **planetary** **sigils**; the **Pentacle** of **Solomon** **is** **the** **most** **important** **magical** **device** of **the** **Solomonic** **tradition**.

The **Pentacle** of **Solomon** **is** **preserved** **in** **real** **manuscripts**; the **Pentacle** of **Solomon** **is** **a** **real** **Solomonic** **device**; the **Pentacle** of **Solomon** **is** **a** **specific** **form** of **the** **magical** **talisman**.

## The Real Two Forms — Lesser and Greater

The **Pentacle** of **Solomon** **has** **two** **principal** **forms**: **the** **Lesser** **Pentacle** and **the** **Greater** **Pentacle**. The **two** **forms** **are** **distinct** **devices**; the **two** **forms** **have** **different** **purposes**; the **two** **forms** **are** **complementary**.

### The Lesser Pentacle

The **Lesser** **Pentacle** (**the** **small** **pentacle**; the **pentacle** of **the** **specific** **operation**; the **pentacle** of **the** **specific** **spirit**; the **pentacle** of **the** **specific** **angel**) **is** **a** **circular** **diagram** of **a** **specific** **size** (the **size** **varies** from **2** to **6** **inches** in **diameter**; the **size** **is** **the** **specific** **size** **for** **the** **specific** **operation**; the **size** **is** **the** **specific** **size** **for** **the** **specific** **spirit**; the **size** **is** **the** **specific** **size** **for** **the** **specific** **angel**).

The **Lesser** **Pentacle** **is** **inscribed** **with**:

- **The** **planetary** **names** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **names**; the **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **names** **are** **the** **planetary** **intelligences** of **the** **specific** **operation**)
- **The** **angelic** **names** (**the** **specific** **angelic** **names**; the **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **specific** **planetary** **order**)
- **The** **divine** **names** (**the** **specific** **divine** **names**; the **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **divine** **names** of **the** **specific** **planetary** **order**)
- **The** **planetary** **sigils** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **sigils**; the **sigils** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **sigils** **are** **the** **specific** **planetary** **sigils** of **the** **specific** **planetary** **order**)

The **Lesser** **Pentacle** **is** **the** **most** **common** **form** of **the** **Pentacle** of **Solomon**; the **Lesser** **Pentacle** **is** **used** for **the** **specific** **operations**; the **Lesser** **Pentacle** **is** **the** **specific** **pentacle** for **the** **specific** **planetary** **operation**.

### The Greater Pentacle

The **Greater** **Pentacle** (**the** **large** **pentacle**; the **pentacle** of **the** **general** **protection**; the **pentacle** of **the** **specific** **magician**; the **pentacle** of **the** **master** **of** **the** **art**) **is** **a** **hexagram** **or** **octagram** **diagram** of **a** **specific** **size** (the **size** **varies** from **6** to **12** **inches** in **diameter**; the **size** **is** **the** **specific** **size** for **the** **general** **protection**).

The **Greater** **Pentacle** **is** **inscribed** **with**:

- **The** **Tetragrammaton** (**the** **specific** **Tetragrammaton**; the **Tetragrammaton** **is** **inscribed** **at** **the** **center** of **the** **hexagram**; the **Tetragrammaton** **is** **the** **specific** **4**-**letter** **divine** **name** of **the** **Hebrew** **tradition**; the **Tetragrammaton** **is** **YHWH**)
- **The** **planetary** **names** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **names**; the **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **points** of **the** **hexagram**; the **names** **are** **the** **planetary** **intelligences** of **the** **7** **planets**)
- **The** **angelic** **names** (**the** **specific** **angelic** **names**; the **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **angels** of **the** **specific** **planetary** **order**)
- **The** **divine** **names** (**the** **specific** **divine** **names**; the **names** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **names** **are** **the** **specific** **divine** **names** of **the** **specific** **planetary** **order**)
- **The** **planetary** **sigils** (**the** **specific** **planetary** **sigils**; the **sigils** **are** **inscribed** **on** **the** **specific** **arcs**; the **sigils** **are** **the** **specific** **planetary** **sigils** of **the** **specific** **planetary** **order**)

The **Greater** **Pentacle** **is** **the** **most** **important** **form** of **the** **Pentacle** of **Solomon**; the **Greater** **Pentacle** **is** **worn** **by** **the** **magician**; the **Greater** **Pentacle** **is** **the** **specific** **pentacle** for **the** **general** **protection**.
