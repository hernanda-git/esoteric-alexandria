# Babylonian Astrology — The Real Omen Tradition

## The Real Document

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **the** **astrological** **tradition** of **ancient** **Mesopotamia**; the **tradition** **is** **the** **oldest** **documented** **astrological** **tradition** in **the** **world**; the **tradition** **is** **preserved** **in** **cuneiform** **tablets** **dating** **from** **c.** **1900** **BCE** to **c.** **539** **BCE** (the **Achaemenid** **conquest** of **Babylon**). The **tradition** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **tradition** **is** **a** **document**; the **tradition** **is** **preserved** **in** **the** **British** **Museum**, **the** **Louvre**, **the** **Vorderasiatisches** **Museum** in **Berlin**, **and** **the** **Penn** **Museum** in **Philadelphia** (the **tens** **of** **thousands** **of** **cuneiform** **tablets** **of** **the** **tradition**).

The **tradition** **is** **the** **foundation** of **all** **subsequent** **Western** **astrology**; the **tradition** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology**; the **tradition** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Indian** **astrology**; the **tradition** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Arabic** **astrology**; the **tradition** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **modern** **Western** **astrology**.

## The Real Sources — The Cuneiform Tablets

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **preserved** in **two** **principal** **cuneiform** **collections**:

### The Enuma Anu Enlil (EAE) Series

The **Enuma** **Anu** **Enlil** (**EAE**, **"**When** **Anu** **and** **Enlil**", **the** **title** **is** **the** **opening** **words** of **the** **series**) **is** **the** **major** **astrological** **omen** **series** of **Mesopotamia**; the **EAE** **series** **is** **composed** **of** **70** **tablets** **organized** **in** **4** **books** (**the** **4** **books** **are** **organized** **by** **the** **celestial** **phenomenon**; **the** **4** **books** **cover** **the** **lunar** **phenomena**, **the** **solar** **phenomena**, **the** **planetary** **phenomena**, **and** **the** **meteorological** **phenomena**). The **EAE** **is** **the** **major** **source** of **the** **Mesopotamian** **astrology**; the **EAE** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Mesopotamian** **horoscopic** **astrology**; the **EAE** **is** **preserved** **in** **multiple** **cuneiform** **manuscripts** **dating** **from** **the** **Old** **Babylonian** **period** **to** **the** **Late** **Babylonian** **period** (the **earliest** **manuscripts** **are** **from** **c.** **1900** **BCE**; **the** **latest** **manuscripts** **are** **from** **c.** **100** **BCE**).

The **EAE** **is** **organized** **as** **a** **collection** of **celestial** **omens**; the **EAE** **presents** **the** **omens** **in** **the** **standard** **omen** **format** (**"**if** **a** **celestial** **phenomenon** **occurs**, **then** **a** **specific** **terrestrial** **event** **will** **occur**"**); the **EAE** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Assyro**-**Babylonian** **celestial** **divination**.

The **EAE** **is** **the** **major** **source** of **the** **Mesopotamian** **celestial** **divination**; the **EAE** **is** **the** **major** **source** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology**; the **EAE** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **astrology** **tradition**.

### The MUL.APIN Compendium

The **MUL**.**APIN** (**"**the** **Plough** **Star**", **the** **name** **is** **the** **opening** **words** of **the** **series**) **is** **the** **major** **astronomical** **compendium** of **Mesopotamia**; the **MUL**.**APIN** **is** **composed** of **2** **tablets** **organized** **as** **an** **astronomical** **reference**; the **MUL**.**APIN** **is** **preserved** **in** **multiple** **cuneiform** **manuscripts** **dating** **from** **c.** **1000** **BCE** to **c.** **300** **BCE**.

The **MUL**.**APIN** **presents**:

- The **list** of **the** **stars** **and** **the** **constellations** (**the** **list** **of** **the** **66** **constellations**; **the** **constellations** **are** **the** **specific** **Mesopotamian** **constellations**; **the** **constellations** **are** **the** **ancestors** of **the** **modern** **zodiacal** **constellations**)
- The **list** of **the** **planetary** **phenomena** (**the** **list** of **the** **5** **planets**; **the** **5** **planets** **are** **the** **specific** **Mesopotamian** **planets**; **the** **5** **planets** **are** **the** **ancestors** of **the** **modern** **classical** **planets**; **the** **5** **planets** **are** **Jupiter**, **Venus**, **Saturn**, **Mercury**, **Mars**; the **sun** **and** **the** **moon** **are** **treated** **separately**)
- The **astronomical** **observations** (**the** **specific** **astronomical** **observations**; **the** **observations** **include** **the** **rising** **and** **setting** **times**, **the** **positions** **of** **the** **planets**, **the** **eclipses**)
- The **mathematical** **astronomy** (**the** **mathematical** **models** **of** **the** **planetary** **motion**; **the** **mathematical** **astronomy** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **mathematical** **astronomy**)

The **MUL**.**APIN** **is** **the** **major** **source** of **the** **Mesopotamian** **astronomy**; the **MUL**.**APIN** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astronomy**; the **MUL**.**APIN** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **astronomy**.

## The Real Content — The 4 Books of EAE

### Book 1 — The Lunar Omens (Lunar Phenomena)

The **first** **book** of **the** **EAE** **deals** **with** **the** **lunar** **phenomena**; the **first** **book** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **part** of **the** **EAE**; the **first** **book** **presents** **the** **omens** **for** **the** **lunar** **halos**, **the** **lunar** **eclipses**, **the** **lunar** **rises** **and** **settings**, **the** **first** **crescent** **of** **the** **new** **moon**.

The **first** **book** **is** **organized** **as** **a** **collection** of **lunar** **omens**; the **first** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **lunar** **astrology**; the **first** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **lunar** **astrology** **tradition**.

### Book 2 — The Solar Omens (Solar Phenomena)

The **second** **book** of **the** **EAE** **deals** **with** **the** **solar** **phenomena**; the **second** **book** **presents** **the** **omens** **for** **the** **solar** **halos**, **the** **solar** **eclipses**, **the** **solar** **rises** **and** **settings**, **the** **parhelia** (**the** **sun** **dogs**).

The **second** **book** **is** **organized** **as** **a** **collection** of **solar** **omens**; the **second** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **solar** **astrology**; the **second** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **solar** **astrology** **tradition**.

### Book 3 — The Planetary Omens (Planetary Phenomena)

The **third** **book** of **the** **EAE** **deals** **with** **the** **planetary** **phenomena**; the **third** **book** **presents** **the** **omens** **for** **the** **planetary** **rises** **and** **settings**, **the** **planetary** **stationary** **points**, **the** **planetary** **conjunctions**, **the** **planetary** **colors** **and** **appearances**.

The **third** **book** **is** **organized** **as** **a** **collection** of **planetary** **omens**; the **third** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **planetary** **astrology**; the **third** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **planetary** **astrology** **tradition**.

### Book 4 — The Meteorological Omens (Atmospheric Phenomena)

The **fourth** **book** of **the** **EAE** **deals** **with** **the** **meteorological** **phenomena**; the **fourth** **book** **presents** **the** **omens** **for** **the** **thunder**, **the** **lightning**, **the** **rain**, **the** **clouds**, **the** **earthquakes**, **the** **meteors**.

The **fourth** **book** **is** **organized** **as** **a** **collection** of **meteorological** **omens**; the **fourth** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **meteorological** **astrology**; the **fourth** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Western** **meteorological** **astrology** **tradition**.

## The Real Practice — The Babylonian Horoscopic Astrology

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **evolved** **from** **omen**-**based** **celestial** **divination** **to** **horoscopic** **astrology** **in** **the** **Late** **Babylonian** **period** (**c.** **410** **BCE** **onwards**; the **earliest** **known** **horoscope** **is** **from** **c.** **410** **BCE**; the **earliest** **horoscope** **is** **preserved** **in** **a** **cuneiform** **tablet** **in** **the** **British** **Museum**; the **horoscope** **is** **the** **Babylonian** **BM** **34576**). The **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **a** **fundamental** **shift** **from** **the** **earlier** **omen**-**based** **tradition**; the **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **the** **tradition** **that** **was** **transmitted** **to** **the** **Hellenistic** **world**; the **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **the** **tradition** **that** **becomes** **the** **Western** **astrology**.

The **Babylonian** **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **organized** **as** **follows**:

1. **The** **date** **of** **birth** (**the** **specific** **date**; **the** **date** **is** **recorded** **in** **the** **Babylonian** **calendar**; **the** **date** **includes** **the** **year**, **the** **month**, **the** **day**)
2. **The** **positions** of **the** **planets** (**the** **specific** **positions** of **the** **5** **planets** + **the** **sun** + **the** **moon**; **the** **positions** **are** **recorded** **in** **the** **zodiacal** **signs**; **the** **positions** **are** **the** **specific** **celestial** **coordinates**)
3. **The** **interpretation** (**the** **specific** **interpretation** of **the** **positions**; **the** **interpretation** **is** **based** on **the** **EAE** **and** **the** **celestial** **divination** **tradition**)
4. **The** **prediction** (**the** **specific** **prediction** of **the** **future**; **the** **prediction** **is** **based** on **the** **interpretation**; **the** **prediction** **is** **the** **specific** **prognostication** of **the** **life** **events**)

The **Babylonian** **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **horoscopic** **astrology**; the **Babylonian** **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **Western** **astrology**.

## The Real Influence

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **the** **foundation** of **all** **subsequent** **Western** **astrology**. The **Babylonian** **astrology** **influences**:

- **The** **Hellenistic** **astrology** (**c.** **2nd** **c.** **BCE** **onwards**; **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology** **is** **the** **transmission** of **the** **Babylonian** **astrology** **to** **the** **Greek** **world**; **see** **[[04-Hellenistic-Astrology-Vettius-Valens]]**)
- **The** **Indian** **astrology** (**the** **Indian** **astrology** **is** **transmitted** **to** **the** **Indian** **subcontinent** **in** **c.** **1st** **c.** **CE**; **the** **Indian** **astrology** **is** **the** **Yavanajataka** **tradition**)
- **The** **Arabic** **astrology** (**the** **Arabic** **astrology** **is** **transmitted** **to** **the** **Islamic** **world** **in** **c.** **8th**-**9th** **c.** **CE**; **the** **Arabic** **astrology** **is** **the** **tradition** of **the** **Abbasid** **translation** **movement**)
- **The** **Latin** **astrology** (**the** **Latin** **astrology** **is** **transmitted** **to** **the** **Latin** **world** **in** **c.** **11th**-**12th** **c.** **CE**; **the** **Latin** **astrology** **is** **the** **tradition** of **the** **Toledo** **translation** **school**)
- **The** **modern** **Western** **astrology** (**the** **modern** **Western** **astrology** **is** **the** **direct** **descendant** of **the** **Babylonian** **astrology**)

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **the** **foundation** of **all** **subsequent** **Western** **astrology**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **tradition**.

## The Note

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **a** **real** **cuneiform** **tradition**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **preserved** **in** **tens** **of** **thousands** **of** **cuneiform** **tablets**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **the** **foundation** of **all** **subsequent** **Western** **astrology**. The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **a** **document**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **a** **real** **historical** **tradition**.

The **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **a** **useful** **resource** **for** **the** **student** of **the** **history** **of** **astrology**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **a** **useful** **resource** **for** **the** **student** of **the** **ancient** **history**; the **Babylonian** **astrology** **is** **a** **useful** **resource** **for** **the** **student** of **the** **celestial** **divination** **tradition**. The **Babylonian** **astrology** **should** **be** **studied** **on** **its** **own** **terms**.
