# Medical Astrology — The Doctrine of Critical Days and Planetary Rulerships

## The Real Practice

**Medical astrology** (Greek *iatromathematika* — "the **medical-mathematical** art") is a **real** branch of the **traditional** astrology; it is the **art** of **correlating** the **seven** **planets** with the **parts** of the **body**, the **humors**, the **temperaments**, and the **critical** **moments** of **illness**. It is **not** a **legend**; it is a **document**, preserved in **Hellenistic**, **Perso-Arabic**, **medieval-Latin**, and **early-modern-English** **manuscripts** and **printed** **editions**, and was a **standard** part of the **physician's** **training** until the **18th century**.

The **doctrine** rests on two pillars: the **melothesia** (the **mapping** of the **planets** and **signs** onto the **body**), and the **critical days** (the **timing** of the **crisis** of a **disease** by the **cycles** of the **Moon**).

## The Melothesia — Planetary Rulership of the Body

The **seven** **planets** **rule** the **body** in a **descending** **order** from the **head** to the **feet**, mirrored in the **zodiac** from **Aries** (head) to **Pisces** (feet). The **standard** **Hellenistic** **assignment** (Ptolemy, Tetrabiblos II.10) is:

- **Saturn** — the **spleen**, the **bones**, the **teeth**, the **right** **ear**, the **cold** **and** **dry** **constitution**
- **Jupiter** — the **lungs**, the **arteries**, the **vital** **breath**, the **blood** (in the **humoral** **sense**)
- **Mars** — the **bile** (the **yellow** **choler**), the **veins**, the **kidneys**, the **left** **ear**, the **genitals**
- **Sun** — the **brain** (the **sensorium**), the **heart**, the **right** **eye**, the **vital** **heat**
- **Venus** — the **flesh**, the **womb**, the **reproductive** **organs**, the **throat**
- **Mercury** — the **reason** (the **brain** **nerves**), the **tongue**, the **speech**, the **hands**, the **humoral** **mixture**
- **Moon** — the **stomach**, the **belly**, the **left** **eye**, the **moisture**, the **phlegm**

The **signs** **rule** the **corresponding** **parts**: **Aries** the **head** and **face**, **Taurus** the **neck** and **throat**, **Gemini** the **shoulders** and **arms**, and so down to **Pisces** the **feet**. A **planet** **afflicted** in a **sign** is **read** as a **predisposition** to **disease** in the **ruled** **part** — the **basis** of the **natal** **medical** **judgement**.

## The Critical Days — The Lunar Cycle of Crisis

The **critical days** (*krisis*) are the **days** on which a **disease** is **expected** to **turn** — toward **health** or **death**. The **Hellenistic** **doctrine** (Valens, **Galen**) ties the **crisis** to the **Moon's** **separations** and **aspects**: the **4th day** (the **Moon** at the **square**), the **7th day** (the **Moon** at the **opposition**), the **14th day** (the **Moon** back at the **conjunction**), and the **modified** **week** of **3⅓** days (the **Moon** moving **~40°**) — the **earliest** **crisis** at **day 3-4**.

The **principle** is **cosmic** **sympathy** (see [[03-Babylonian-Astrology-Real-Omen-Tradition]]): the **Moon**, the **body** of **moisture**, **governs** the **humors**, and the **humors** **shift** as the **Moon** **aspects** the **place** of the **decanbiture** (the **chart** of the **onset** of the **illness**). The **physician** **times** the **treatment** — the **bloodletting**, the **purging**, the **medication** — to the **Moon's** **favorable** **aspect** (see [[05-Electional-Astrology-Real-Practice]]).

## The Perso-Arabic and Medical Synthesis

The **Islamic** **physicians** **absorbed** the **Hellenistic** **melothesia** into the **humoral** **medicine** of **Galen**. **Al-Razi** (Rhazes) and **Ibn Sina** (Avicenna) **retained** the **planetary** **rulerships** as a **diagnostic** **and** **prognostic** **aid** within the **Qanun** (the **Canon** of **medicine**). **Abu Ma'shar** **systematized** the **elections** for **bloodletting** and **phlebotomy** by the **Moon's** **place** and **aspect**.

**Culpeper** (1651) **translated** and **adapted** this into **English**: his **"Astrological Judgement of Diseases"** gives the **planet** **ruling** each **disease**, the **herb** **ruled** by that **planet** as the **remedy**, and the **decumbiture** **chart** for **prognosis**. The **doctrine** that **"like** **cures** **like"** by **planetary** **sympathy** is the **medical** **cousin** of the **alchemical** **principle** (see [[04-Alchemy-Transmutation/01-Alchemy-Overview]]).

## The Real Honesty Note

Medical astrology is a **real** **historical** **practice**, preserved in **real** **manuscripts** and **printed** **editions**; it is **not** a **legend** and **not** a **modern** **invention**. It is, however, **not** **scientifically** **validated** in the **modern** **sense**: the **humoral** **theory** on which it **rests** was **superseded** by **germ** **theory** and **physiology** in the **19th century**. It is a **cultural** and **historical** **discipline**, a **real** **chapter** in the **history** of **medicine**, and a **useful** **resource** for the student of the **Western** **esoteric** and **medical** **traditions** — not a **substitute** for **evidence-based** **care**.
