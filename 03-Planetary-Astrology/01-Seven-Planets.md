# The Seven Classical Planets

## The Order (Chaldean / al-Buni)

The classical order is **Saturn → Jupiter → Mars → Sun → Venus → Mercury → Moon**. This is the order of their assumed orbital periods (Saturn slowest, Moon fastest) and it is the order that matters for talismanic and *'ilm al-huruf* work.

In al-Buni's *Shams al-Ma'arif*, this is the *huruf al-falakiyya* (the planetary letters). Each planet rules a specific subset of the 28 Arabic letters, and the order encodes the relationship.

| # | Planet | Arabic | Day | Metal | Gem | Color | Day of the Week | Body Part |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Saturn | زحل (Zuhal) | Saturday | Lead | Onyx | Black | السبت | Bones, knees, ears |
| 2 | Jupiter | المشتري (al-Mushtari) | Thursday | Tin | Sapphire | Blue / Silver | الخميس | Liver, thighs, blood |
| 3 | Mars | المريخ (al-Mirrrikh) | Tuesday | Iron | Ruby | Red | الثلاثاء | Gallbladder, blood, head |
| 4 | Sun | الشمس (al-Shams) | Sunday | Gold | Diamond | Yellow / Gold | الأحد | Heart, spine, brain |
| 5 | Venus | الزهرة (al-Zuhra) | Friday | Copper | Emerald | Green / White | الجمعة | Kidneys, throat, ovaries |
| 6 | Mercury | عطارد (al-'Utarid) | Wednesday | Mercury (quicksilver) | Agate | Multi-colored | الأربعاء | Lungs, hands, tongue |
| 7 | Moon | القمر (al-Qamar) | Monday | Silver | Pearl | White | الإثنين | Brain, stomach, eyes |

## The Modern Note

Note that the modern astronomical list has more than 7 (Uranus, Neptune, Pluto, etc.). For classical talismanic and *'ilm al-huruf* work, the **7 are sufficient** and the modern additions are treated as sub-aggregates (Uranus = higher-octave Mercury, Neptune = higher-octave Venus, etc.).

## The Weekly Order

The seven-day week is the seven planets in sequence:

- Sunday = Sun
- Monday = Moon
- Tuesday = Mars (*Mardi* in French, *März* in German, *Martis dies* in Latin)
- Wednesday = Mercury (*Mercredi*, *Mittwoch*, *Mercurii dies*)
- Thursday = Jupiter (*Jeudi*, *Donnerstag*, *Iovis dies*)
- Friday = Venus (*Vendredi*, *Freitag*, *Veneris dies*)
- Saturday = Saturn (*Samedi*, *Samstag*, *Saturni dies*)

The English (and most Romance) names are mixed: Sun, Moon, Tiw (Norse war god = Mars), Woden (Norse = Mercury), Thor (= Jupiter), Frigg (= Venus), Saturn (Latin). The Romance languages preserve the Latin planetary names.

## The Spiritual Hierarchy (al-Buni)

In al-Buni's cosmology, the planets are not objects — they are **angels** in the sense of "intelligences that govern a part of creation". Each planet has:

- A *haykal* (form / body) — the visible planet
- A *ruh* (spirit) — the angel that rules it
- A *malik* (sovereign) — the archangel of the planetary sphere (e.g., Jibra'il/Jupiter, Mika'il/Sun, Israfil/Mercury, Azra'il/Moon, Samha'il/Mars)

This is the same hierarchy that later shows up in Agrippa and the Christian Kabbalistic tradition, and ultimately descends to the **Olympian spirits** of theurgy (Iamblichus).

## Mapping to Human Activity

The planets map to domains. This is the **operating manual** for talismanic timing:

| Planet | Domain | When to invoke |
|---|---|---|
| Saturn | Structure, endurance, karma, time, binding | Long-term projects, banishing, protection from enemies |
| Jupiter | Expansion, wisdom, generosity, growth, law | Education, travel, contracts, leadership |
| Mars | Action, courage, conflict, energy, surgery | Warfare, athletics, surgery, decisive action |
| Sun | Glory, success, authority, health, father | Promotion, royal favor, vital force |
| Venus | Love, art, beauty, pleasure, harmony | Romance, art, social harmony, fertility |
| Mercury | Commerce, communication, learning, travel | Trade, study, writing, networking |
| Moon | Emotion, intuition, body, mother, cycles | Mood, sleep, women's health, fishing, agriculture |

## The Planetary Hours

Each day is divided into 24 planetary hours. The first hour after sunrise is ruled by the planet of the day (Sunday = Sun, Monday = Moon, …). The 24 hours cycle through the seven planets in the Chaldean order. See [[05-Planetary-Hours]].
