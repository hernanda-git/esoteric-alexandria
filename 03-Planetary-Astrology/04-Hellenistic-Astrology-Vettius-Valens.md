# Hellenistic Astrology — Vettius Valens and the Anthology

## The Real Document

The **Anthologiae** (**"**Anthology**"**, **"**collection**"**, **the** **title** **means** **"**the** **compilation** **of** **astrological** **texts**"**) **of** **Vettius** **Valens** **is** **the** **major** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **text**; the **Anthologiae** **is** **a** **comprehensive** **handbook** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology**; the **Anthologiae** **is** **preserved** **in** **Greek** **manuscripts** **in** **the** **Vatican** **Library** (**Vatican** **Graecus** **191**; **the** **earliest** **known** **manuscript**; **the** **manuscript** **dates** **to** **c.** **1500** **CE**; **the** **manuscript** **is** **the** **textus** **receptus** of **the** **Greek** **text**).

The **Anthologiae** **is** **the** **most** **comprehensive** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **text**; the **Anthologiae** **is** **the** **canonical** **source** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology**; the **Anthologiae** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Western** **astrological** **tradition** **from** **the** **2nd** **c.** **CE** **to** **the** **21st** **c.** **CE**.

## The Real Author

**Vettius** **Valens** **of** **Antioch** (**c.** **120**-**175** **CE**; **the** **dates** **are** **approximate**) **is** **the** **author** of **the** **Anthologiae**; **Valens** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **Hellenistic** **astrologer**; **Valens** **is** **the** **most** **famous** **practitioner** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **tradition**.

The **biographical** **information** **about** **Valens** **is** **derived** **from** **the** **Anthologiae** **itself**; **Valens** **is** **a** **native** of **Antioch** (**the** **capital** of **the** **Roman** **province** of **Syria**; **the** **major** **cultural** **center** of **the** **2nd**-**c.** **Roman** **East**); **Valens** **studies** **astrology** **with** **the** **masters** **of** **his** **time** (**the** **master** **Theon**; **the** **master** **Antiochus**; **the** **specific** **masters** **are** **named** in **the** **Anthologiae**); **Valens** **practices** **astrology** **in** **the** **Roman** **East** **for** **a** **period** **of** **at** **least** **40** **years** (**from** **c.** **130** **CE** **to** **c.** **170** **CE**); **Valens** **writes** **the** **Anthologiae** **in** **the** **later** **part** of **his** **career** (**c.** **154**-**174** **CE**).

The **biographical** **details** of **the** **Anthologiae** **are** **the** **specific** **records** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**; **the** **biographical** **details** **are** **the** **most** **detailed** **biographical** **record** of **any** **ancient** **astrologer**.

## The Real Structure — The 9 Books

The **Anthologiae** **is** **divided** **into** **9** **books**; the **9** **books** **have** **specific** **content**; the **9** **books** **are** **a** **comprehensive** **handbook** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology**.

### Book 1 — The Introduction (Introduction to Astrology)

The **first** **book** **deals** **with** **the** **introduction** to **the** **astrology**; the **first** **book** **is** **the** **theoretical** **foundation** of **the** **text**; the **first** **book** **discusses**:

- The **classification** of **the** **astrological** **questions** (the **specific** **classification**; the **questions** **are** **organized** **as** **generic** (**the** **general** **state** of **the** **life**), **catarchic** (**the** **electional** **moment** of **a** **specific** **action**), **interrogational** (**the** **horary** **moment** of **a** **specific** **question**), **natal** (**the** **moment** of **birth**))
- The **classification** of **the** **celestial** **bodies** (the **specific** **classification** of **the** **7** **planets** + **the** **zodiacal** **signs** + **the** **lots** (**the** **specific** **Arabic**/**Greek** **mathematical** **points**))
- The **classification** of **the** **astrological** **techniques** (the **specific** **classification** of **the** **astrological** **techniques**; the **techniques** **include** **the** **natal** **chart** **interpretation**, **the** **electional** **chart** **interpretation**, **the** **horary** **chart** **interpretation**, **the** **mundane** **chart** **interpretation**)

The **first** **book** **is** **the** **most** **systematic** of **the** **9** **books**; the **first** **book** **is** **a** **manual** of **the** **astrological** **introduction**; the **first** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**.

### Book 2 — The Planets (Specific Planetary Rulerships)

The **second** **book** **deals** **with** **the** **planets**; the **second** **book** **is** **the** **most** **practical** **part** of **the** **text**; the **second** **book** **discusses**:

- The **planetary** **rulers** of **the** **12** **signs** (the **specific** **rulerships**; the **rulerships** **are** **the** **domicile** **rulers**; the **domicile** **rulers** **are** **the** **specific** **planets** **who** **rule** **the** **specific** **signs**; the **domicile** **rulers** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**)
- The **planetary** **exaltations** (the **specific** **exaltations**; the **exaltations** **are** **the** **specific** **signs** **where** **the** **planets** **are** **exalted**; the **exaltations** **are** **the** **specific** **degrees** of **exaltation**)
- The **planetary** **triplicities** (the **specific** **triplicities**; the **triplicities** **are** **the** **specific** **signs** **that** **belong** to **a** **specific** **element**; the **triplicities** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**)
- The **planetary** **terms** (the **specific** **terms**; the **terms** **are** **the** **specific** **degree** **ranges** **of** **the** **signs** **where** **the** **specific** **planets** **rule**; the **terms** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**)
- The **planetary** **decans** (the **specific** **decans**; the **decans** **are** **the** **specific** **10**-**degree** **segments** of **the** **signs**; the **decans** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**)

The **second** **book** **is** **the** **most** **technical** of **the** **9** **books**; the **second** **book** **is** **a** **manual** of **the** **planetary** **rulers**; the **second** **book** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrological** **practice**.

### Books 3-5 — The Natal Chart Interpretation (The Life of the Native)

The **third**, **fourth**, and **fifth** **books** **deal** **with** **the** **natal** **chart** **interpretation**; the **books** **are** **the** **practical** **core** of **the** **text**; the **books** **discuss**:

- The **specific** **techniques** of **the** **natal** **chart** **interpretation** (the **techniques** **include** **the** **planetary** **strength**, **the** **aspect** **patterns**, **the** **house** **rulers**, **the** **lot** **rulers**)
- The **specific** **themes** of **the** **natal** **chart** **interpretation** (the **themes** **include** **the** **life** **span**, **the** **health**, **the** **wealth**, **the** **marriage**, **the** **children**, **the** **career**, **the** **travel**, **the** **death**)
- The **specific** **case** **studies** of **the** **natal** **chart** **interpretation** (the **case** **studies** **are** **the** **specific** **examples**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **author**'s **own** **chart**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **charts** of **the** **master** **Theon**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **charts** of **the** **master** **Antiochus**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **charts** of **the** **specific** **clients** of **the** **author**)

The **third**-**fifth** **books** **are** **the** **most** **practical** of **the** **9** **books**; the **books** **are** **a** **manual** of **the** **natal** **chart** **interpretation**; the **books** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **natal** **astrology**.

### Books 6-7 — The Electional and Horary (The Choice of the Moment)

The **sixth** and **seventh** **books** **deal** **with** **the** **electional** and **horary** **chart** **interpretation**; the **books** **are** **the** **practical** **extension** of **the** **text**; the **books** **discuss**:

- The **specific** **techniques** of **the** **electional** **chart** **interpretation** (the **techniques** **include** **the** **planetary** **timing**, **the** **planetary** **rulers**, **the** **planetary** **strength**, **the** **planetary** **configurations**)
- The **specific** **techniques** of **the** **horary** **chart** **interpretation** (the **techniques** **include** **the** **planetary** **timing**, **the** **planetary** **rulers**, **the** **planetary** **strength**, **the** **planetary** **configurations**)
- The **specific** **case** **studies** of **the** **electional** and **horary** **chart** **interpretation** (the **case** **studies** **are** **the** **specific** **examples**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **specific** **examples** of **the** **author**)

The **sixth**-**seventh** **books** **are** **the** **most** **useful** of **the** **9** **books** **for** **the** **practical** **astrologer**; the **books** **are** **a** **manual** of **the** **electional** and **horary** **chart** **interpretation**.

### Books 8-9 — The Mundane and the Catarchic (The World and the Empire)

The **eighth** and **ninth** **books** **deal** **with** **the** **mundane** **and** **the** **catarchic** **chart** **interpretation**; the **books** **are** **the** **practical** **conclusion** of **the** **text**; the **books** **discuss**:

- The **specific** **techniques** of **the** **mundane** **chart** **interpretation** (the **techniques** **include** **the** **planetary** **timing**, **the** **planetary** **rulers**, **the** **planetary** **strength**, **the** **planetary** **configurations**; the **techniques** **are** **applied** to **the** **specific** **charts** **of** **the** **kingdoms** and **the** **empires**)
- The **specific** **techniques** of **the** **catarchic** **chart** **interpretation** (the **techniques** **include** **the** **planetary** **timing**, **the** **planetary** **rulers**, **the** **planetary** **strength**, **the** **planetary** **configurations**; the **techniques** **are** **applied** to **the** **specific** **charts** **of** **the** **imperial** **enterprises** and **the** **state** **actions**)
- The **specific** **case** **studies** of **the** **mundane** and **catarchic** **chart** **interpretation** (the **case** **studies** **are** **the** **specific** **examples**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **specific** **examples** of **the** **author**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **specific** **charts** **of** **the** **Emperor** **Antoninus** **Pius**; the **case** **studies** **include** **the** **specific** **charts** **of** **the** **Emperor** **Marcus** **Aurelius**)

The **eighth**-**ninth** **books** **are** **the** **most** **political** of **the** **9** **books**; the **books** **are** **a** **manual** of **the** **state** **astrology**; the **books** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **state** **astrology**.

## The Real Techniques — The Hellenistic Tools

The **Hellenistic** **astrology** **developed** **a** **specific** **set** of **techniques**; the **techniques** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **modern** **Western** **astrology**; the **techniques** **are** **the** **specific** **Hellenistic** **contributions**.

### The Lots (Κλῆροι, the Mathematical Points)

The **Lots** (**Greek** **κλῆροι**, **"**allotments**"**) **are** **the** **mathematical** **points** **of** **the** **chart**; the **lots** **are** **calculated** **as** **the** **arc** **between** **two** **planets** **projected** **from** **the** **Ascendant**; the **lots** **are** **the** **specific** **Hellenistic** **mathematical** **technique**.

The **major** **Lots** **are**:

- The **Lot** of **Fortune** (**τύχη**; **the** **first** **Lot**; **the** **most** **important** **Lot**; **calculated** **as** **the** **arc** from **the** **Sun** to **the** **Moon** **projected** from **the** **Ascendant**; **the** **Lot** **of** **Fortune** **is** **the** **specific** **Lot** of **the** **material** **fortune**; **the** **Lot** **of** **Fortune** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **natal** **astrology**)
- The **Lot** of **Daimon** (**δαίμων**; **the** **second** **Lot**; **the** **Lot** **of** **the** **Spirit**; **calculated** **as** **the** **arc** from **the** **Moon** to **the** **Sun** **projected** from **the** **Ascendant**; **the** **Lot** **of** **Daimon** **is** **the** **specific** **Lot** of **the** **spiritual** **purpose**; **the** **Lot** **of** **Daimon** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **natal** **astrology**)
- The **Lot** of **Eros** (**Ἔρως**; **the** **third** **Lot**; **the** **Lot** of **Love**; **calculated** **as** **the** **arc** from **the** **Sun** to **Venus** **projected** from **the** **Ascendant**; **the** **Lot** of **Eros** **is** **the** **specific** **Lot** of **the** **romantic** **love**; **the** **Lot** of **Eros** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **love** **astrology**)
- The **Lot** of **Necessity** (**ἀνάγκη**; **the** **fourth** **Lot**; **calculated** **as** **the** **arc** from **the** **Mercury** to **the** **Lot** of **Fortune** **projected** from **the** **Ascendant**; **the** **Lot** of **Necessity** **is** **the** **specific** **Lot** of **the** **necessary** **events**; **the** **Lot** of **Necessity** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **restrictive** **astrology**)

The **lots** **are** **the** **specific** **Hellenistic** **innovation**; the **lots** **are** **the** **mathematical** **foundation** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology**.

### The Aspects (the Planetary Relationships)

The **aspects** **are** **the** **planetary** **relationships**; the **aspects** **are** **the** **specific** **angular** **distances** **between** **the** **planets**; the **aspects** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **chart** **interpretation**.

The **major** **aspects** **are**:

- The **conjunction** (**0**°; **the** **most** **powerful** **aspect**; **the** **planets** **are** **in** **the** **same** **place**; **the** **conjunction** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **planetary** **combination**)
- The **sextile** (**60**°; **the** **harmonious** **aspect**; **the** **planets** **are** **in** **the** **harmonious** **relationship**; **the** **sextile** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **planetary** **cooperation**)
- The **square** (**90**°; **the** **challenging** **aspect**; **the** **planets** **are** **in** **the** **challenging** **relationship**; **the** **square** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **planetary** **conflict**)
- The **trine** (**120**°; **the** **most** **harmonious** **aspect**; **the** **planets** **are** **in** **the** **most** **harmonious** **relationship**; **the** **trine** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **planetary** **harmony**)
- The **opposition** (**180**°; **the** **most** **challenging** **aspect**; **the** **planets** **are** **in** **the** **most** **challenging** **relationship**; **the** **opposition** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **planetary** **polarity**)

The **aspects** **are** **the** **specific** **Hellenistic** **technique**; the **aspects** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **chart** **interpretation**.

The **houses** **are** **the** **specific** **Hellenistic** **innovation**; the **houses** **are** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **chart** **interpretation**.
