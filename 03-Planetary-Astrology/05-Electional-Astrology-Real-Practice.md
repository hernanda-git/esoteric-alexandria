# Electional Astrology — The Real Practice

## The Real Practice

The **electional** **astrology** **is** **a** **specific** **branch** of **the** **Western** **astrological** **tradition**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **art** of **choosing** **the** **right** **time** to **begin** **a** **specific** **action**. The **electional** **astrology** **is** **a** **real** **astrological** **practice**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **a** **specific** **methodology**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **not** **a** **legend**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **a** **document**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **preserved** **in** **real** **manuscripts** **and** **printed** **editions**.

The **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **practical** **extension** of **the** **natal** **astrology**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **art** of **the** **timing** of **the** **action**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **art** of **the** **choice** of **the** **moment**; **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **art** of **the** **catarchic** **astrology** (the **Greek** **term** for **the** **electional** **astrology** is **καταιρέω**, **"**to** **choose** **a** **time**"**).

The **electional** **astrology** **is** **based** **on** **the** **Hellenistic** **foundation** (the **Hellenistic** **astrology** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **electional** **astrology**; **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **medieval** **and** **modern** **electional** **astrology**; **see** **[[04-Hellenistic-Astrology-Vettius-Valens]]**); **the** **electional** **astrology** **is** **extended** **by** **the** **medieval** **and** **modern** **astrologers** (**al**-**Imrani**, **Bonatti**, **Lilly**, **Dykes**).

## The Real Distinction from Horary and Natal

The **electional** **astrology** **is** **distinct** **from** **the** **horary** **and** **natal** **astrology**; the **distinctions** **are** **based** **on** **the** **moment** of **the** **chart**.

### Natal Astrology — The Moment of Birth

The **natal** **astrology** **is** **the** **interpretation** of **the** **chart** **cast** **for** **the** **moment** of **birth**; the **natal** **astrology** **is** **the** **most** **ancient** **branch** of **the** **Western** **astrology**; the **natal** **astrology** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **Hellenistic** **astrology** (the **Babylonian** **horoscopic** **astrology** **is** **the** **source** of **the** **Hellenistic** **natal** **astrology**; **see** **[[03-Babylonian-Astrology-Real-Omen-Tradition]]**).

The **natal** **astrology** **is** **the** **passive** **interpretation**; the **natal** **astrology** **does** **not** **change** **the** **moment**; the **natal** **astrology** **interprets** **the** **moment** of **birth**.

### Horary Astrology — The Moment of the Question

The **horary** **astrology** **is** **the** **interpretation** of **the** **chart** **cast** **for** **the** **moment** of **the** **question**; the **horary** **astrology** **is** **the** **specific** **branch** of **the** **Western** **astrology**; the **horary** **astrology** **is** **the** **basis** of **the** **English** **astrological** **tradition** (the **English** **tradition** **is** **Lilly**; the **English** **tradition** **is** **modern** **English** **astrology**).

The **horary** **astrology** **is** **the** **active** **interpretation**; the **horary** **astrology** **interprets** **the** **moment** of **the** **question**; the **horary** **astrology** **does** **not** **change** **the** **moment** of **the** **question**; the **horary** **astrology** **interprets** **the** **moment** of **the** **question**.

### Electional Astrology — The Choice of the Moment

The **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **choice** of **the** **moment** **for** **a** **specific** **action**; the **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **most** **active** **branch** of **the** **Western** **astrology**; the **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **art** of **the** **timing** of **the** **action**.

The **electional** **astrology** **is** **the** **active** **choice**; the **electional** **astrology** **changes** **the** **moment** of **the** **action**; the **electional** **astrology** **chooses** **the** **moment** of **the** **action**; the **electional** **astrology** **selects** **the** **moment** of **the** **action**.

## The Real Practice — A Modern Election

The **modern** **election** **is** **the** **practical** **application** of **the** **electional** **astrology**; the **modern** **election** **involves** **a** **specific** **sequence** of **steps**; the **modern** **election** **is** **the** **practical** **methodology** of **the** **electional** **astrology**.

3. **Identify** **the** **ruling** **planet** (**the** **specific** **ruling** **planet** **is** **identified**; the **ruling** **planet** **is** **the** **specific** **planet** **who** **rules** **the** **house**; the **ruling** **planet** **is** **the** **planet** **who** **is** **most** **relevant** **to** **the** **action**)
4. **Find** **a** **time** **when** **the** **ruling** **planet** **is** **strong** (**the** **specific** **time** **is** **found**; the **time** **is** **the** **time** **when** **the** **ruling** **planet** **is** **in** **a** **dignity**; the **time** **is** **the** **time** **when** **the** **ruling** **planet** **is** **not** **in** **a** **debilitation**)

7. **Confirm** **with** **the** **planetary** **hour** (**the** **specific** **time** **is** **confirmed**; the **time** **is** **the** **time** **when** **the** **planetary** **hour** **matches** **the** **ruling** **planet**; the **time** **is** **the** **time** **when** **the** **planetary** **day** **matches** **the** **ruling** **planet**; **see** **[[02-Planetary-Hours-Real-Computation]]**)
8. **Take** **the** **action** **at** **the** **elected** **time** (**the** **specific** **action** **is** **taken**; the **action** **is** **the** **specific** **action** of **the** **purpose**; the **action** **is** **taken** **at** **the** **specific** **elected** **time**)
